## Item

**Vampire Blood Gelato** is a frozen indulgence imbued with nocturnal energy. 
The ice cream base is sweetened with nightfruit and coagulated vampiric essence, and its crowning feature, a candied heart, which grants a brief glimpse into the pulse of immortality.

A rare delicacy served during crimson eclipses in vampire enclaves.
The heart garnish pulses faintly if held too long.
