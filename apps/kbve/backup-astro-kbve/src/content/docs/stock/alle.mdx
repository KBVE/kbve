## ALLE - Security Solutions Leader

Allegion plc (NYSE: ALLE) is a leading global provider of security products and solutions that keep people safe, secure, and productive. Founded in 1908 and headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, with operational headquarters in Carmel, Indiana, Allegion has evolved from a traditional lock manufacturer into a comprehensive security solutions company serving commercial, institutional, and residential markets worldwide.

With operations spanning over 130 countries and a portfolio of trusted brands, Allegion focuses on security around the door and adjacent areas. The company designs, manufactures, and markets a comprehensive range of products including mechanical and electronic locks, door hardware, access control systems, time and attendance solutions, and integrated security platforms.

## Comprehensive Security Portfolio

Allegion's diverse product portfolio addresses every aspect of security and access control:

### Electronic Access Control
Advanced electronic security solutions for modern buildings:

- **Card and Mobile Credentials**: Proximity cards, smart cards, and mobile access solutions
- **Biometric Systems**: Fingerprint, facial recognition, and multi-modal biometric readers
- **Wireless Locks**: Battery-powered electronic locks for retrofit applications
- **Networked Access Control**: Centralized management and monitoring systems
- **Time and Attendance**: Workforce management and tracking solutions
- **Visitor Management**: Check-in systems and temporary access credentials

### Mechanical Hardware
Traditional and advanced mechanical security products:

- **Cylindrical Locks**: Standard door locks for commercial and residential applications
- **Mortise Locks**: Heavy-duty locks for institutional and commercial buildings
- **Deadbolts**: High-security deadbolt locks for residential and light commercial use
- **Exit Devices**: Panic hardware and emergency exit solutions
- **Door Closers**: Automatic door closing mechanisms for safety and security
- **Architectural Hardware**: Hinges, handles, and decorative door hardware

### Integrated Security Systems
Comprehensive platforms combining multiple security technologies:

- **Access Control Platforms**: Centralized management of multiple security systems
- **Video Integration**: Combination of access control with surveillance systems
- **Intrusion Detection**: Door and window sensors for security monitoring
- **Fire Safety Integration**: Life safety systems integrated with access control
- **Building Automation**: Integration with HVAC and lighting systems
- **Cloud-Based Solutions**: Software-as-a-Service security management platforms
