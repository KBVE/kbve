## CVX - Global Integrated Energy Excellence

Chevron Corporation (NYSE: CVX) is one of the world's largest integrated energy companies engaged in oil and gas exploration, production, refining, marketing, and chemicals across the entire energy value chain globally. Founded in 1879 and headquartered in San Ramon, California, Chevron has established itself as a premier energy company with operations in more than 180 countries, combining technological innovation with operational excellence to meet global energy demand responsibly.

With proven reserves, advanced drilling capabilities, and integrated downstream operations, Chevron delivers energy products that power economic growth while maintaining a strong commitment to environmental stewardship and safety excellence. The company's strategic focus on capital discipline, low-cost production, and technology innovation positions it as a leader in traditional energy production while investing in emerging energy technologies and carbon reduction solutions.

## Comprehensive Integrated Energy Operations

Chevron operates through two primary business segments:

### Upstream
Oil and natural gas exploration, development, and production:

- **Conventional Oil & Gas**: Traditional oil and gas exploration and production worldwide
- **Shale and Tight Oil**: Unconventional resource development in the Permian Basin and other plays
- **Deepwater Operations**: Offshore oil and gas development in the Gulf of Mexico and globally
- **International Projects**: Major international oil and gas projects and joint ventures
- **LNG Operations**: Liquefied natural gas production and export facilities
- **Enhanced Oil Recovery**: Advanced recovery techniques maximizing resource extraction

### Downstream
Refining, marketing, and chemicals operations:

- **Refining**: Global refining operations producing gasoline, diesel, jet fuel, and specialty products
- **Marketing**: Retail gasoline stations and commercial fuel distribution
- **Chemicals**: Petrochemical manufacturing and specialty chemical products
- **Lubricants**: High-performance lubricants and specialty petroleum products
- **Supply Chain**: Global supply chain and logistics for refined product distribution
- **Renewable Fuels**: Investment in renewable diesel and sustainable aviation fuel
