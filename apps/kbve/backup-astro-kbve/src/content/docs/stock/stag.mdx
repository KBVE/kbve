## STAG - Pure-Play Industrial REIT

STAG Industrial, Inc. (NYSE: STAG) is a real estate investment trust focused exclusively on the acquisition, development, ownership, and operation of industrial properties throughout the United States. Founded with a clear mission to deliver attractive long-term stockholder returns through disciplined investment in high-quality industrial real estate, STAG has positioned itself as a leading pure-play industrial REIT.

The company's strategy centers on single-tenant warehouse and distribution facilities, targeting properties that offer attractive returns across CBRE-EA Tier 1 industrial markets. With a portfolio spanning 41 states and approximately 117.6 million rentable square feet, STAG has built a diversified platform designed to capitalize on the growing demand for modern industrial real estate driven by e-commerce and supply chain evolution.

## Portfolio Overview

### Scale and Diversification
As of March 31, 2025, STAG's impressive portfolio includes:
- **597 Buildings** across the United States
- **117.6 Million** rentable square feet
- **41 States** providing geographic diversification
- **$9.9 Billion** enterprise value
- **96.5%** occupancy rate demonstrating strong demand

### Tenant and Industry Diversification
STAG maintains a well-diversified tenant base with:
- **No single tenant** accounting for more than 2.9% of total annualized base rental revenue
- **No single industry** representing more than 11.3% of total revenue
- **Diverse industry exposure** across logistics, manufacturing, distribution, and e-commerce fulfillment
