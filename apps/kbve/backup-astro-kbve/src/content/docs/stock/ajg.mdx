## AJG - Global Insurance Brokerage and Risk Management Leader

Arthur J. Gallagher & Co. (NYSE: AJG) is a leading global insurance brokerage, risk management, and consulting company that has been serving clients for over 95 years. Founded in 1927 and headquartered in Rolling Meadows, Illinois, Gallagher provides comprehensive insurance brokerage, consulting, and third-party claims settlement services to clients ranging from Fortune 500 companies to small and mid-sized businesses across virtually every industry.

With operations in more than 40 countries and territories worldwide, Gallagher serves clients through its extensive network of retail and wholesale brokerage operations, specialty practices, and claim settlement services. The company's mission is to protect clients against financial and operational risks while helping them achieve their business objectives through innovative risk management solutions.

## Comprehensive Service Platform

Gallagher operates through three primary business segments:

### Brokerage Segment
Comprehensive insurance brokerage and consulting services:

- **Commercial Lines**: Property, casualty, and specialty insurance for businesses
- **Personal Lines**: Individual insurance solutions including auto, home, and life insurance
- **Employee Benefits**: Group health, dental, vision, and voluntary benefits consulting
- **Retirement Services**: 401(k) plan administration and retirement consulting
- **Risk Management**: Loss control, claims management, and safety consulting
- **Captive Insurance**: Alternative risk transfer and captive insurance solutions

### Risk Management Segment
Third-party claims administration and risk management services:

- **Claims Administration**: Comprehensive third-party claims administration services
- **Loss Control**: Safety and loss prevention consulting and services
- **Risk Analytics**: Data analytics and risk assessment services
- **Managed Care**: Medical bill review and managed care services
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Compliance consulting and regulatory support
- **Technology Solutions**: Claims management systems and technology platforms

### Corporate and Other
Support functions and strategic initiatives:

- **Acquisitions**: Strategic acquisition integration and development
- **Technology Investment**: Digital transformation and technology platform development
- **International Operations**: Global expansion and international market development
- **Specialty Practices**: Niche market expertise and specialized consulting services
