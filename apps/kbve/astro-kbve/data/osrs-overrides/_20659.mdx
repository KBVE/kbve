## Obtaining

Giant squirrel is a skilling pet obtained while training Agility. The drop rate depends on the agility course and your Agility level.

**Base Drop Rates (at level 99):**
- Gnome Stronghold: ~1/35,609
- Draynor Village: ~1/33,005
- Varrock: ~1/24,410
- Canifis: ~1/36,842
- Falador: ~1/26,806
- Seers' Village: ~1/35,205
- Pollnivneach: ~1/33,422
- Rellekka: ~1/31,063
- Ardougne: ~1/34,440
- Prifddinas: ~1/25,146
- Hallowed Sepulchre: ~1/35,000 per hallowed mark

**Requirements:**
- No specific requirement, but higher Agility level improves chances
- Members world required

## Pet Info

Giant squirrel is an oversized squirrel that serves as the Agility skilling pet. It has a dark form variant obtainable from the Hallowed Sepulchre.

**Examine text:** "Nuts!"

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate improves with level
- Chance rolled per lap completion (not per obstacle)
- Hallowed Sepulchre is most efficient method
- Average time to obtain: ~12-15M Agility XP
