## Obtaining

Grind kebbit teeth with pestle and mortar:
- Kebbit teeth from hunting sabre-toothed kebbits
- 51 Hunter required to catch kebbits
- Location: Rellekka Hunter area (north of Rellekka)

## Herblore Uses

| Potion | Base | Level | XP |
|--------|------|-------|-----|
| Hunter potion(3) | Avantoe potion (unf) | 53 | 120 |

Hunter potions boost Hunter by 3 levels.

## Market Strategy

**Hunter Training:**
- Hunt sabre-toothed kebbits for teeth
- Good Hunter XP while gathering
- Grind teeth for herblore profit

**Hunter Potion Economics:**
- Compare teeth dust + avantoe cost vs potion price
- Potions used for high-level hunter catches
- Useful for herbiboar hunting

**Ironman Notes:**
- Must hunt kebbits personally
- Stock up teeth while training Hunter
- Hunter potions helpful for black chins

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours
