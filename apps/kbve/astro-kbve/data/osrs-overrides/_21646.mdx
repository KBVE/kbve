## Obtaining

**Drops:** Wyverns in the Wyvern Cave — Spitting wyvern, Ancient wyvern, Taloned wyvern, and Long-tailed wyvern. Drop rates range from 1/512 to 1/600 depending on variant.

## Comparison

The granite longsword sits between the [Rune longsword](/osrs/rune-longsword/) (+47 slash, +49 str) and the dragon longsword (+58 slash, +60 str) in stats. It requires both 50 Attack and 50 Strength, making it less accessible than the dragon longsword which only needs 60 Attack. Notably, it is the only granite weapon without a special attack. The [Granite hammer](/osrs/granite-hammer/) and [Granite maul](/osrs/granite-maul/) are often preferred for their special attacks. A [Leaf-bladed sword](/osrs/leaf-bladed-sword/) is a comparable alternative at 50 Attack with no strength requirement.
