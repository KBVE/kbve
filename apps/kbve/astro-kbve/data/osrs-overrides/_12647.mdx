## Obtaining

Kalphite princess is a pet dropped by the Kalphite Queen at a rate of 1/3,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Ability to deal with KQ's two phases
- Rope to access the lair (first time)

**Location:**
- Kalphite Lair, accessed via Shantay Pass area

## Pet Info

Kalphite princess is a miniature version of the Kalphite Queen. It can metamorphose between crawling and airborne forms, just like the boss has two phases.

**Examine text:** "A very cute and only mildly dangerous Kalphite princess."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/3,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~2,077 kills (63.2% chance)
- Both phases must be killed to count as a kill
