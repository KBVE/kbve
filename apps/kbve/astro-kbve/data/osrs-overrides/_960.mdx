## Obtaining

Convert [Logs](/osrs/logs/) at a Sawmill:

| Method | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Sawmill operator | 100 GP per plank |
| Plank Make spell | 70 GP + runes |
| Woodcutting Guild sawmill | 90 GP per plank |

**Spawns:** Port Khazard, Barbarian Outpost, various locations.

**Note:** Regular planks are the only plank type that sometimes requires [nails](/osrs/steel-nails/) for construction.

## Construction

| XP per plank | 29 |
|--------------|-----|
| Buy limit | 13,000 per 4 hours |
| Min Construction level | 1 |

**Early Training (levels 1-33):**
- Crude wooden chairs (2 planks, 58 XP)
- Wooden bookcases (4 planks, 115 XP)
- Exit room to reset furniture

**Note:** Requires nails for some furniture - bring extras as they can bend.

## Market Strategy

**Why Use Regular Planks:**
- Cheapest plank option
- Required for early Construction training
- Used in some Mahogany Homes contracts
- Required for Dragon Slayer I and other quests

**Upgrade Path:**
1. Regular planks (29 XP) - levels 1-33
2. [Oak planks](/osrs/oak-plank/) (60 XP) - levels 33-52
3. [Teak planks](/osrs/teak-plank/) (90 XP) - levels 52-99
4. [Mahogany planks](/osrs/mahogany-plank/) (140 XP) - fastest 99
