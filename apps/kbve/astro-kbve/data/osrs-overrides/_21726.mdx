## Obtaining

Dropped by Gargoyles and related monsters. Grotesque Guardians always drop granite dust.

**Requirements:** 75 Slayer to kill Gargoyles

**Locations:**
- Slayer Tower (Gargoyles)
- Grotesque Guardians (top of Slayer Tower)

| Monster | Quantity | Drop Rate |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Gargoyle | 1-55 | 1/125 |
| Marble gargoyle | 2-110 | 1/125 |
| Grotesque Guardians | 250-500 | Always |

## Usage

**Upgrades Granite maul:**
- Requires 200 Granite dust
- Reduces special attack cost from 60% to 50%
- Essential for PvP players

**Permanent upgrade, cannot be reversed.**

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 13,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Stackable, commonly traded in bulk
- Consistent demand from PvP players
- Grotesque Guardians are the best source
