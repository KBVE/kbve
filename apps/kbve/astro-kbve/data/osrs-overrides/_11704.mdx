## Obtaining

Dropped by General Graardor in God Wars Dungeon (1/508 drop rate).

**Requirements:**
- 70 Strength to access Bandos area
- Bandos KC or ecumenical key

## Usage

Combine with [Godsword blade](/osrs/godsword-blade/) to create [Bandos godsword](/osrs/bandos-godsword/).

**Creation:**
- Use hilt on godsword blade
- No skill requirements
- Instantly creates BGS

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 8 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Price tied to BGS price
- BGS popular for defence drain spec
- Often cheaper than other hilts
