## Obtaining

**Mage Arena II (Wilderness):**
1. Complete Mage Arena I first
2. Speak to Kolodion about the second challenge
3. Hunt three Wilderness bosses:
   - Justiciar Zachariah (drops Justiciar's hand)
   - Porazdir (drops Demon's heart)
   - Derwen (drops Ent's roots)
4. Return all three items to Kolodion
5. Choose which god cape to imbue

**Requirements:**
- 75 Magic
- Completed Mage Arena I
- Able to survive Wilderness PKers
