## Obtaining

Dropped by Grotesque Guardians on the Slayer Tower rooftop.

**Drop rate:** 1/3,000

**Requirements:**
- 75 Slayer
- Brittle key (dropped by Gargoyles)
- Gargoyle Slayer task

## Pet Information

**Examine:** "The mini Guardian of Dawn!"

A miniature gargoyle that follows the player.

**Metamorphosis:**
- Can transform into Midnight (alternate form)
- Toggle between forms freely
- Both forms available once obtained

**Properties:**
- Untradeable
- Cannot be alchemised
- Stored at Probita if inventory full
