## Overview

Coins (gp) are the primary currency in Old School RuneScape. Used for trading, NPC services, and the Grand Exchange.

## Key Facts

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Max stack | 2,147,483,647 (max cash) |
| Tradeable | Yes |
| Stackable | Yes |
| F2P | Yes |

## Obtaining

**Common Sources:**
- Monster drops
- Selling items to shops
- High/Low Alchemy spells
- Grand Exchange trades
- Quest rewards

**Ring of Wealth:**
- Auto-collects coin drops
- Useful for Slayer and bossing

## Uses

**NPC Services:**
- Spell modifications
- Item repairs (Barrows, etc.)
- Tanning hides
- Decanting potions
- Butler payments (Construction)

**Grand Exchange:**
- Primary trading currency
- All tradeable items priced in gp

**Construction:**
- Mahogany homes costs
- Plank costs (Sawmill)
- Servant payments

## Alchemy

Cannot cast alchemy on coins themselves. Common alch targets:

| Item | High Alch |
|------|-----------|
| Rune platebody | 39,000 gp |
| Rune 2h sword | 38,400 gp |
| Yew longbow | 768 gp |
| Magic longbow | 1,536 gp |
