## Obtaining

The Amulet of rancour is created by attaching an Araxyte fang to an Amulet of torture.

**Requirements:**
- 86 Crafting
- 90 Hitpoints to equip

**Components:**
- Amulet of torture
- Araxyte fang (1/600 from Araxxor)

## Stats Comparison

| Stat | Torture | Rancour | Difference |
|------|---------|---------|------------|
| Melee attack | +15 | +25 | +10 |
| Strength | +10 | +12 | +2 |
| Prayer | +0 | +2 | +2 |
| Magic | +0 | -6 | -6 |
| Ranged | +0 | -8 | -8 |

## Usage

**Best-in-slot melee amulet** for:
- All melee combat
- Bossing
- Slayer
- PvP

**Note:** Significant magic/ranged penalties make it unsuitable for hybrid setups.

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 8 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Araxyte fang is untradeable, only the finished amulet is tradeable
- High demand as BiS melee neck
- 90 HP requirement limits some accounts
