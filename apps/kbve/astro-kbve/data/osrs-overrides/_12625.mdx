Per dose:
- **Restores:** 20% run energy
- **Reduces:** Run energy drain by 70% for 2 minutes
- Effect resets (doesn't stack) with additional doses

## Usage

Essential for:
- Runecrafting (especially blood/soul runes)
- Agility training
- Raids (constant running)
- Hunter (tracking)
- General efficiency
