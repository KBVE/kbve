## Obtaining

Upgraded from Void knight robe by speaking to the Elite Void Knight.

**Requirements:**
- Void knight robe
- 200 commendation points
- Hard Western Provinces Diary completed

**Location:** Void Knights' Outpost

## Elite Set Bonus

When worn with Elite void top and a void helm:
- **Void ranger helm:** +12.5% ranged damage (vs +10% regular)
- **Void mage helm:** +5% magic damage bonus (in addition to accuracy)

**Prayer Bonus:** +3 (vs 0 on regular robe)

## Usage

Essential for:
- Vorkath (ranged setup)
- Theatre of Blood
- Chambers of Xeric
- Any content where void ranged is used

## Tips

- Repair cost if broken: 250,000 coins
- Complete Hard Western Provinces Diary first
- Storable in costume room armour case
