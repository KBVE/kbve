## Creation

Mix [Snapdragon potion (unf)](/osrs/snapdragon-potion-unf/) + [Red spiders' eggs](/osrs/red-spiders-eggs/).

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Herblore Level | 63 |
| XP gained | 142.5 |

## Effects

Per dose restores:
- All stats except Hitpoints
- Prayer points: 8 + 25% of level
- Other stats: 8 + 25% of level

**At 99 Prayer:** Restores 32 prayer points per dose.

## Usage

Essential for high-level PvM:
- Raids (CoX, ToB, ToA)
- Bossing (GWD, Nex, etc.)
- Inferno attempts
- Used with [Saradomin brew](/osrs/saradomin-brew4/) (3:1 ratio)

**Note:** Counters stat drain from Saradomin brews.
