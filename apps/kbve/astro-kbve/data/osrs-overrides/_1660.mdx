## Creation

**Crafting** (Level 40): [Ruby](/osrs/ruby/) + [Gold bar](/osrs/gold-bar/) at a furnace with necklace mould (75 XP)

> *"I wonder if this is valuable."*

## Enchanting → Digsite Pendant

**Lvl-3 Enchant** (49 Magic): 1 cosmic rune + 5 fire runes (59 XP)

The **Digsite pendant** provides 5 charges of teleportation to:
- **Digsite** — near the Exam Centre
- **House on the Hill** — requires Bone Voyage completion (Fossil Island access)
- **Lithkren** — requires Dragon Slayer II (partial)

The House on the Hill teleport is particularly valuable — it's the primary way to reach **Fossil Island** for birdhouse runs, volcanic mine, and herbiboar.

**Note:** The enchantment must be learned from the archaeologists at the Varrock Museum before it can be cast.
