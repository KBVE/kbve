## Obtaining

Dropped by K'ril Tsutsaroth in God Wars Dungeon (1/508 drop rate).

**Requirements:**
- 70 Hitpoints to access Zamorak area
- Zamorak KC or ecumenical key

## Usage

Combine with [Godsword blade](/osrs/godsword-blade/) to create [Zamorak godsword](/osrs/zamorak-godsword/).

**Creation:**
- Use hilt on godsword blade
- No skill requirements
- Instantly creates ZGS

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 8 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- ZGS has freeze special attack
- Niche PvP and PvM uses
- Often cheapest godsword hilt
