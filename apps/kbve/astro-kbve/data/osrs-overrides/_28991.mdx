## Obtaining

Atlatl darts can be obtained through:

| Method | Amount | Requirements |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| Fletching | 20 per craft | 74 Fletching |
| Lunar Chest | 72-120 | None |

**Crafting Materials:**
- 20 Headless atlatl darts
- 20 Atlatl dart tips (from broken antlers)

## Usage

Ammunition for the Eclipse atlatl weapon.

**Unique Mechanic:**
- Despite being ranged ammunition, damage is calculated using the player's **melee strength bonus**
- Cannot be used with toxic blowpipe
- Incompatible with Dizana's quiver
