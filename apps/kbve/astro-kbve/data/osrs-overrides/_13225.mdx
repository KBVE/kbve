## Obtaining

TzRek-Jad is a pet obtained by completing the Fight Caves at a rate of 1/200 per completion, or by sacrificing a Fire cape at a rate of 1/200.

**Requirements:**
- Moderate to high combat stats (70+ recommended)
- Ability to defeat TzTok-Jad

**Location:**
- Fight Caves, accessed via TzHaar City

## Pet Info

TzRek-Jad is a miniature version of TzTok-Jad, the final boss of the Fight Caves. It was the first pet to be released for a boss encounter.

**Examine text:** "This baby is too hot to handle."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/200 per Fight Caves completion
- Can also be obtained by sacrificing a Fire cape (1/200)
- Average completions to obtain: ~138 completions (50% chance)
