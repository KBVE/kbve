## Obtaining

Pet kraken is a pet dropped by the Kraken boss at a rate of 1/3,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- 87 Slayer level
- Kraken Slayer task assigned

**Location:**
- Kraken Cove (Piscatoris Fishing Colony area)

## Pet Info

Pet kraken is a miniature sea creature representing the Kraken boss. The Kraken is a Slayer boss requiring a Kraken task to kill.

**Examine text:** "The most adorable pool of water with tentacles you ever saw."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/3,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~2,077 kills (63.2% chance)
- Requires ongoing Kraken Slayer tasks to farm
- Cave kraken (small versions) do not drop the pet
