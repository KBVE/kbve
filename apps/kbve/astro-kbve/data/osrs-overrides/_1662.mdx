## Creation

**Crafting** (Level 56): [Diamond](/osrs/diamond/) + [Gold bar](/osrs/gold-bar/) at a furnace with necklace mould (90 XP)

> *"I wonder if this is valuable."*

## Enchanting → Phoenix Necklace

**Lvl-4 Enchant** (57 Magic): 1 cosmic rune + 10 earth runes (67 XP)

The **Phoenix necklace** is an emergency survival item:
- Activates when HP drops **below 20%** of maximum
- **Heals 30%** of maximum Hitpoints
- Destroyed after one use
- Works up to **level 30 Wilderness**

At 99 HP: triggers at 19 HP, heals 29 HP (bringing you to ~48 HP).

## PvP Usage

Phoenix necklaces are popular in **PvP**:
- Emergency healing when being comboed
- Useful in **Last Man Standing** and **Bounty Hunter**
- Can save you from a KO attempt
- Triggers automatically — no action needed

## Comparison to Ring of Life

| Item | Trigger | Effect | Wilderness |
|------|---------|--------|-----------|
| Phoenix necklace | <20% HP | **Heals 30% HP** | Lvl 30 |
| Ring of life | ≤10% HP | Teleport to spawn | Lvl 30 |

Phoenix necklace keeps you in the fight; ring of life removes you.
