## Creation

**Crafting** (Level 23): [Sapphire](/osrs/sapphire/) + [Gold bar](/osrs/gold-bar/) at a furnace with bracelet mould (60 XP)

> *"A sapphire bracelet."*

## Enchanting → Bracelet of Clay

**Lvl-1 Enchant** (7 Magic): 1 cosmic rune + 1 water rune (17.5 XP)

The **Bracelet of clay** makes soft clay from hard clay **without needing water** — each use consumes one charge (28 charges per bracelet). Essential for:
- **Construction training** — mass soft clay production
- **Crafting training** — pottery items
- Saves inventory space (no water containers needed)
