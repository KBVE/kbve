## Obtaining

Infernal eels are caught in **TzHaar City (Mor Ul Rek)**:

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Fishing Level | 80 |
| Fishing XP | 95 per catch |
| Tool | Oily fishing rod + bait |

Access requires Fire Cape to enter inner city.

## Dissection

Use a knife on infernal eels to extract resources:

| Product | Quantity | Chance |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Tokkul | 1 | Always |
| Onyx bolt tips | 1-4 | Common |
| Lava scale shard | 1-5 | 1/8.75 |

## Market Strategy

**AFK Money Making:**
- Very AFK fishing method
- Profitable from onyx bolt tips
- Safe alternative to wilderness lava dragons

**Lava Scale Shards:**
- 1/8.75 chance per eel
- Much safer than lava dragons
- Lower rate but zero risk

**Tokkul Farming:**
- Passive tokkul from every eel
- Use for TzHaar shops
- Onyx purchases if enough saved

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours
