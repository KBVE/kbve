## Obtaining

**Shop:** Purchased from the Happy Heroes' Hemporium in the Heroes' Guild for 50,000 coins.

**Requirements:**
- Heroes' Quest completion

**Drops:** Various monsters (Brutal dragons, etc.)

## Usage

**Special Attack - Shatter:**
- Uses only 25% special attack energy
- 50% increased damage
- Ignores opponent's Protect from Melee prayer

**Combat Features:**
- +5 Prayer bonus (unique among dragon weapons)
- Crush attack style (good vs plate armor)
- Same speed as scimitar
