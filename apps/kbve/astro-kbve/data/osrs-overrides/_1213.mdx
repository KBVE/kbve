## Comparison

The rune dagger is fast (4 tick) but has significantly lower strength (+24) and accuracy (+25 stab) compared to the [Rune scimitar](/osrs/rune-scimitar/). The small +1 magic attack bonus is unique among rune melee weapons. In F2P, the [Rune sword](/osrs/rune-sword/) is a better stab weapon with the same speed but higher bonuses. The [Dragon dagger](/osrs/dragon-dagger/) is the direct members upgrade with a powerful special attack.

## Market Strategy

**High Alch:** 4,800 gp

**Buy Limit:** 70 per 4 hours

Low value and poor combat stats make this a niche item. Primarily used for alching or as a budget stab weapon in F2P. The poisoned variants (members) see more use in PvP.
