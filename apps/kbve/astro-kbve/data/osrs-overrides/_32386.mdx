## Obtaining

The Medallion of the deep is assembled from eight medallion fragments after reading a waterlogged journal.

**Requirements:**
- 63 Sailing
- Crafting skill (level TBD)
- Read waterlogged journal first
- Collect all 8 medallion fragments

**Experience Gained:**
- 750 Sailing XP
- 500 Crafting XP

## Usage

The Medallion of the deep is a neck slot item that allows the wielder to breathe underwater during Sailing activities.

**Features:**
- Allows underwater breathing
- Essential for underwater exploration
- Purely utility - no combat bonuses

**Restrictions:**
- Cannot be worn while diving in Fossil Island's underwater area
- "The currents around these waters seem pretty strong"

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable** - Cannot be bought or sold on the Grand Exchange.

**Tips:**
- Assemble from medallion fragments found during Sailing
- Read the waterlogged journal before attempting to assemble
- Essential for underwater content in Sailing
- Cannot replace other underwater breathing equipment at Fossil Island
