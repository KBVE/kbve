## Obtaining

**Smithing:**
- Requires 34 Smithing
- 1 [Steel bar](/osrs/steel-bar/) = 15 nails
- Grants 37.5 XP

**Shops:**
- Sawmill Operator: 5 GP each
- Construction supplies shops

**Drops:**
- Various monsters drop steel nails

## Construction Use

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Bend rate | Medium |
| Construction level | 1+ |
| Used with | [Planks](/osrs/plank/) |

**Most common nails for Construction training.** Reasonable bend rate with affordable cost.

**Required for:**
- Furniture using regular [planks](/osrs/plank/)
- Some quest builds (e.g., Priest in Peril bridge)

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 13,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Best value nails for Construction
- Good balance of cost and bend rate
- Popular for early Construction training
- Required for various quests
