## Creation

Create an oily fishing rod by combining:
1. Fishing rod (buy from fishing shops)
2. Blamish oil (herblore creation)

**Making Blamish Oil:**
1. Buy sample bottle from Canifis general store
2. Catch thin or fat snail
3. Use snail with pestle and mortar (blamish snail slime)
4. Add to harralander potion (unf)
5. Apply oil to fishing rod

## Usage

The oily fishing rod is used to catch **infernal eels** in TzHaar City:

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Fishing Level | 80 |
| Location | TzHaar City (requires Fire Cape for inner city) |
| Bait | Fishing bait |

## Infernal Eel Products

Dissect eels with a knife for:
- Tokkul (always)
- Onyx bolt tips (common)
- Lava scale shards (1/8.75)

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable Tool:**
- Must be self-made
- Materials are cheap
- One-time creation (keep in bank)

**Infernal Eel Profit:**
- Very AFK fishing method
- Safe alternative to lava dragons
- Profitable from onyx bolt tips

**Ironman Tip:**
- Essential for lava scale shards without PKer risk
- Passive tokkul for TzHaar shop purchases
