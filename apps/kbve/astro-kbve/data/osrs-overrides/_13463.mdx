## Prayer Training

| Method | XP |
|--------|-----|
| Reanimate (Dark Altar) | 480 |

**Magic Level Required:** 3
**Spell:** Basic Reanimation

## Obtaining

Dropped by bears when Arceuus favour is unlocked.

## Usage

1. Cast Basic Reanimation spell on the head
2. Kill the reanimated creature
3. Receive Prayer XP

## Market Flip Strategy

**Low-Tier Heads:**
- Budget Prayer training option
- Decent early game XP

**Buy Limit:** 50 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Not commonly traded
- Bears easy to kill
- Consider giant heads instead
