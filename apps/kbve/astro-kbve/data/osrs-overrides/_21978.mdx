## Usage

Super antifire provides **complete dragonfire immunity** for 3 minutes:
- No anti-dragon shield required
- Works against all dragonfire variants
- Essential for efficient dragon killing

## Creation

| Input | Output | Herblore Level | XP |
|-------|--------|----------------|-----|
| Antifire(4) + Crushed superior dragon bones | Super antifire(4) | 92 | 130 |

## Market Strategy

**Superior Dragon Bones:**
- Dropped by Fossil Island wyverns
- Must be crushed with pestle and mortar
- Primary cost driver
