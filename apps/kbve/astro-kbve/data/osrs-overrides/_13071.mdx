## Obtaining

Chompy chick is a pet obtained from killing chompy birds after achieving 4,000+ kills.

**Drop Rate:**
- 0% chance until 4,000 kills
- 1/500 per kill after 4,000 kills

**Requirements:**
- Completion of Big Chompy Bird Hunting quest
- Ogre bow and ogre arrows
- Bellows and toads for bait

**Location:**
- Feldip Hills, south of Yanille

## Pet Info

Chompy chick is a baby chompy bird. It requires a significant grind of 4,000+ kills before you even have a chance at obtaining it.

**Examine text:** "Just a wee little chompy."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/500 per kill (after 4,000 kill threshold)
- Average kills to obtain: ~4,346 total kills (50% chance after threshold)
- Chompy killing is relatively slow and tedious
- Elite Void Knight diary requires 1,000 kills; pet hunters need 4,000+
