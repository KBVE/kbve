## Obtaining

Purchased from Lanthus at Castle Wars for 75 Castle Wars tickets.

**Location:** Castle Wars

## Usage

**God Wars Dungeon:**
- Acts as a Saradomin item
- Prevents Saradomin followers from attacking

**Combat Stats:**
- Defensive bonuses similar to black full helm
- +3 Prayer bonus
- +11 Magic defence (black full helm has 0)

**Bandit Camp:** Will aggro bandits when worn.

## Tips

- Useful for Commander Zilyana (Saradomin boss)
- Storable in costume room armour case
- Repair cost if broken: 25,000 coins
- Untradeable
