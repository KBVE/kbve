## Obtaining

Dropped by Turoth and Kurask in Slayer dungeons.

**Requirements:** 55 Slayer to kill these monsters

**Locations:**
- Fremennik Slayer Dungeon
- Kourend Catacombs

## Usage

**Essential for killing:**
- Turoth (requires leaf-bladed weapon or magic dart)
- Kurask (requires leaf-bladed weapon or magic dart)

**Combat stats:**
- 5-tick attack speed
- Can stab, slash, or crush
- Provides poison immunity while equipped
- Decent all-around bonuses

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 70 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Used by some for Corporeal Beast (spear type weapon)
- Poison immunity is useful in certain scenarios
- Slower than leaf-bladed sword but has defensive bonuses
