## Obtaining

Nexling is a pet dropped by Nex at a rate of approximately 1/500 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Completion of The Frozen Door miniquest
- High combat stats (90+ recommended)
- Team coordination for efficient kills

**Location:**
- Ancient Prison, beneath the God Wars Dungeon

## Pet Info

Nexling is a miniature version of Nex, the powerful Zarosian general found in the Ancient Prison. It is one of the more accessible boss pets due to its relatively good drop rate.

**Examine text:** "A cute little nihil."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: ~1/500 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~346 kills (50% chance)
- MVP mechanics affect individual drop chances
- One of the better boss pet drop rates
