## Obtaining

Obtained from the Font of Consumption using an Unsired.

**Acquisition process:**
1. Kill Abyssal Sire (1/100 Unsired drop rate)
2. Use Unsired on Font of Consumption
3. 62/128 chance for any bludgeon piece

**Effective drop rate:** ~1/207 per Abyssal Sire kill

## Usage

**Creates Abyssal bludgeon:**
- Combine with Bludgeon spine and Bludgeon axon
- Speak to The Overseer or use Overseer's book
- Creates BiS crush training weapon

**Note:** Untradeable until combined into bludgeon
