## Obtaining

Lava scales drop from:

| Monster | Location | Drop Rate |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Lava dragon | Lava Dragon Isle (Wilderness) | Always |
| Brutal black dragon | Catacombs of Kourend | 3/128 |

**Warning:** Lava dragons are in deep Wilderness (PKer risk).

## Processing

Grind lava scales with pestle and mortar:
- Base yield: 3-6 shards per scale
- Hard Wilderness Diary: 4-9 shards per scale
- Higher Herblore = higher average yield
