## Creation

**Crafting** (Level 90): [Onyx](/osrs/onyx/) + [Gold bar](/osrs/gold-bar/) at a furnace with amulet mould (165 XP). String with ball of wool.

> *"An onyx amulet."*

## Enchanting → Amulet of Fury

**Lvl-6 Enchant** (87 Magic): 1 cosmic rune + 20 fire runes + 20 earth runes (97 XP)

The [Amulet of fury](/osrs/amulet-of-fury/) is the **best hybrid amulet** in OSRS:
- **+10** to all attack styles, **+15** to all defence styles
- **+8 Strength**, **+5 Prayer**
- Best all-round amulet before specialized zenyte jewelry
- Used in virtually every combat style

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 8 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Onyx cost (from TzHaar shops or Zulrah) drives the price
- Fury has massive demand — BiS hybrid neck
- High-value enchant — check margins
