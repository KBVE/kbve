## Market Flip Strategy

**Arrow Shaft Processing:**
- 1 Maple log = 60 arrow shafts
- Calculate: `(Arrow shaft × 60) - Maple log = Profit`
- Good volume, mid-tier processing

**Bow Making:**
- Maple longbow (u) + [Bow string](/osrs/bow-string/) = [Maple longbow](/osrs/maple-longbow/)
- High Alch: 576 GP
- Compare crafting cost to alch value

**Buy Limit:** 15,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Popular F2P Woodcutting training
- Seers' Village maple trees
- Mid-tier fletching option
