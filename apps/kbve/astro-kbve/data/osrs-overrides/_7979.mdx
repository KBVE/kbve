## Obtaining

Dropped by Abyssal demons and from Unsired.

**Drop sources:**
- Abyssal demon: 1/6,000
- Greater abyssal demon: 1/6,000
- Unsired (Font of Consumption): 10/128

**Via Unsired:**
- Abyssal Sire drops Unsired at 1/100
- Effective rate: ~1/1,280 per Sire kill

## Usage

**POH decoration:**
- Mount in Skill Hall with stuffed heads
- Requires 56 Construction
- Shows off Slayer achievement

**Ensouled version:**
- Used for Prayer training
- Different item (Ensouled abyssal head)

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 8 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Trophy item for collection
- Very rare from regular demons
- Faster via Abyssal Sire
- Popular for POH flex
