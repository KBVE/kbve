## Obtaining

**Creation:** Combine Dragon sq shield left half and right half with 60 Smithing.

**Requirements:**
- Legends' Quest completion
- 60 Smithing

**Component Sources:**
- Left half: Rare drop from various monsters, Gorak
- Right half: Very rare drop from high-level monsters

## Usage

**Defensive Shield:**
- Good defensive stats for level 60 gear
- Outclassed by Dragonfire shield and defenders

**Mostly Cosmetic:**
- Popular for fashionscape
- Classic dragon equipment look
