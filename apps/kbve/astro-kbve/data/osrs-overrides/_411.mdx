## Examine

> _"A little pearl."_

## Obtaining

Found inside **oysters** from big net fishing. Members only.

## About

An oyster pearl can be used with a chisel to craft a pearl bolt tip (41 Crafting) or a pearl ring. Worth slightly less than oyster pearls.
