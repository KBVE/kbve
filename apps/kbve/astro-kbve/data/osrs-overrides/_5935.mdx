## Creation

Make coconut milk in two steps:

1. Use hammer on coconut to get half coconut
2. Use half coconut on empty vial

This produces:
- 1 Coconut milk (vial)
- 1 Coconut shell (supercompost material)

## Herblore Uses

| Potion | Ingredients | Level | XP |
|--------|-------------|-------|-----|
| Antidote++(3) | Coconut milk + Irit + Magic roots | 79 | 155 |

Coconut milk replaces the standard vial of water for antidote++ potions.

## Market Strategy

**Coconut Supply:**
- Coconuts from palm tree farming
- Also from Tai Bwo Wannai cleanup
- Kingdom of Miscellania (hardwood workers)

**Profit Chain:**
1. Coconut (crack) -> Half coconut
2. Half coconut + Vial -> Coconut milk + Shell
3. Coconut milk -> Antidote++

**Supercompost Bonus:**
- Coconut shell makes supercompost
- Added value from byproduct

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours
