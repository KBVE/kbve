## Market Flip Strategy

**Marks of Grace Conversion:**
- 10 Marks = 100 Amylase crystals
- Calculate: `(Amylase × 100) / 10 = GP per Mark`
- Compare to Graceful recoloring value

**Stamina Profit:**
- 4 crystals + [Super energy(4)](/osrs/super-energy-4/) = [Stamina(4)](/osrs/stamina-potion-4/)
- Calculate: `Stamina(4) - Super energy(4) - (Amylase × 4) = Profit`

**Buy Limit:** 11,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Rooftop agility best source
- Marks untradeable, crystals tradeable
- Consistent stamina demand
- Check GP/Mark vs outfit pieces
