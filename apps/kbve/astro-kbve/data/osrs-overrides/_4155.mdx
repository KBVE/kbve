## Obtaining

Purchased from any Slayer master for 1 coin.

Can also be obtained from:
- Slayer masters as task reward
- Hunter kit (Lunar spell)

| Property | Value |

## Usage

**Communication:**
- Contact Slayer masters remotely
- Check current task progress
- Get tips about current assignment
- Ask for master locations

**Crafting Component:**
- [Slayer helmet](/osrs/slayer-helmet/) - Requires 55 Crafting
- [Slayer ring](/osrs/slayer-ring/) - Requires 75 Crafting

**Kill Log:**
- Tracks kills of most Slayer monsters
- Permanent record per account
