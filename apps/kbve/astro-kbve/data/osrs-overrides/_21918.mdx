## Obtaining

Dropped by high-level metal dragons.

**Best sources:**
- Rune dragon (1/800 drop rate)
- Adamant dragon (1/1,000 drop rate)

**Requirements:**
- Dragon Slayer II for Rune dragons
- 331+ combat stats recommended

## Usage

**Creates Dragon crossbow:**
- Attach to Magic stock
- Requires 78 Fletching
- Grants 70 Fletching XP

**Dragon crossbow stats:**
- +94 Ranged attack
- Can fire dragon bolts
- Special attack for increased accuracy

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Primary value from Dragon crossbow crafting
- Rune dragons more profitable source
- Also obtainable from Brimstone chest
- Part of Dragon Slayer II content
