## Obtaining

Rocky is a skilling pet obtained while training Thieving. The drop rate depends on the target pickpocketed and your Thieving level.

**Base Drop Rates (at level 99):**
- Men/Women: ~1/257,211
- Master Farmers: ~1/257,211
- Ardougne Knights: ~1/257,211
- Elves: ~1/99,175
- TzHaar-Hur: ~1/176,743
- Vyres: ~1/42,838

**Requirements:**
- No specific requirement, but higher Thieving level improves chances
- Members world required

## Pet Info

Rocky is a raccoon that serves as the Thieving skilling pet. It can be recolored to different variants by using certain items on it.

**Examine text:** "A procyon with a penchant for pilfering."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate improves with level and target type
- Higher level targets generally have better rates
- Blackjacking and other methods check per successful pickpocket
- Average time to obtain: ~30-40M Thieving XP
