## Obtaining

Purchased from a Void Knight at the Void Knights' Outpost for 250 commendation points.

**Requirements:**
- 42 Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, Ranged, Magic
- 22 Prayer

**Location:** Void Knights' Outpost

## Usage

Part of the Void Knight equipment set:
- High magic defence bonus for leg armor
- Higher than blessed and black dragonhide chaps
- No negative attack bonuses
- Non-degradable

**Set Bonus:** Requires top, robe, gloves, and a helm for damage/accuracy bonuses.

## Tips

- Can be upgraded to Elite void robe (200 more points + Hard Western Provinces Diary)
- Untradeable
- Storable in costume room armour case
