## Usage

BiS magic spec weapon for:
- PvP (one-shot potential)
- KO combos
- Burst damage situations
- Wilderness PKing

## PvP Strategy

The Volatile nightmare staff is primarily used in PvP due to its exceptional burst damage potential. Players often stack it with other specs (like Dragon claws or AGS) for massive combo damage.
