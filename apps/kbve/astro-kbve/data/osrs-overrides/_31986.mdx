## Obtaining

The Captain's log is obtained during Sailing skill progression.

**Recovery:** If lost, obtain another from Junior Jim on the Pandemonium or from a boat's cargo hold.

## Usage

"You wouldn't want to lose this out at sea."

The Captain's log is an essential utility item that tracks courier and bounty tasks, as well as sea charting completion progress.

**Features:**
- Tracks courier tasks
- Tracks bounty tasks
- Monitors sea charting completion
- Required when speaking to certain NPCs for Sailing training
- Required for unlocking boat paints

**Important:**
- Must be in inventory when speaking to certain Sailing NPCs
- Needed for accessing specific Sailing features and unlocks

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable** - Cannot be bought or sold on the Grand Exchange.

**Tips:**
- Keep in inventory when doing Sailing activities
- Required for many Sailing interactions
- Easily recoverable if lost from Junior Jim or cargo hold
- Essential item - do not destroy
