## Prayer Training

Basic bones are the lowest tier bones for Prayer training:

| Method | XP per bone |
|--------|-------------|
| Bury | 4.5 |
| Gilded altar (2 burners) | 15.75 |
| Chaos altar (Wilderness) | 15.75 (50% saved) |
| Ectofuntus | 18 |

## Obtaining

Dropped by almost all monsters in the game:
- Chickens (1)
- Goblins (2)
- Cows (2)
- Men/Women (2)
- Skeletons (various)

## Market Flip Strategy

**F2P Training:**
- Cheapest Prayer XP available
- Extremely high supply from bots/players
- Low demand except from F2P players

**Buy Limit:** 3,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Very low value per bone
- High volume but low profit potential
- Better to collect Big bones instead
