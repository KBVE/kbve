## Obtaining

| Method | Requirements |
|--------|--------------|
| Brimhaven Agility Arena | 1 Agility (recommended 40+) |

Tickets are earned by tagging dispensers on pillars in the correct sequence. The arena assigns random pillars to tag, and players must navigate obstacles to reach them within the time limit.

### Ticket Rates

| Agility Level | Tickets/Hour |
|---------------|--------------|
| 40-50 | 10-15 |
| 50-60 | 15-20 |
| 60-70 | 20-25 |
| 70-80 | 25-30 |
| 80+ | 30-40 |

## About

The Brimhaven Agility Arena is a unique agility training method on Karamja. While not the most efficient XP per hour, it offers unique rewards and variety from standard rooftop courses.
