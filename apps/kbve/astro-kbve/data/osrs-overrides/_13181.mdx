## Obtaining

Scorpia's offspring is a pet dropped by Scorpia at a rate of 1/2,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Willingness to risk gear in the Wilderness
- Protection from scorpion poison

**Location:**
- Scorpia's Den (level 53-54 Wilderness)

## Pet Info

Scorpia's offspring is a miniature scorpion representing Scorpia, a Wilderness boss. Due to the Wilderness location, players must be careful of PKers while hunting this pet.

**Examine text:** "A very young and inexperienced scorpion."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/2,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~1,386 kills (50% chance)
- Wilderness boss - risk of losing items to PKers
- Can also be obtained from Artio (demi-boss version)
