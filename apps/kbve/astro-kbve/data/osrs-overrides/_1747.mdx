## Tanning

Take to a tanner to convert into [Black dragon leather](/osrs/black-dragon-leather/).

| Cost | Location |
|------|----------|
| 20 GP | Most tanners |
| 45 GP | Canifis tanner |

**Buy Limit:** 11,000 per 4 hours

## Market Flip Strategy

**Tanning Profit:**
- Buy hide → Tan (20 GP) → Sell leather
- Calculate: `Black dragon leather - Black dragonhide - 20 GP = Profit`
- Highest tier dragonhide = best margins

**Tan Leather Spell (78 Magic):**
- Tans 5 hides per cast
- Faster than running to tanner
- Factor in rune costs

**Drop Sources:**
- Black dragons (Taverley Dungeon, Catacombs)
- King Black Dragon
- Brutal black dragons
