## Special Attack

**Blood Sacrifice** (50% energy):

**On unfrozen target:**
- Hits for damage
- Heals 25% of damage dealt
- Freezes target for 6 ticks (3.6s)

**On already frozen target:**
- Hits for damage
- Heals 10% of damage dealt
- Cannot refreeze

## Usage

Primarily used in PvP:
- Freeze + heal combo
- Wilderness PKing
- Hybrid fights
- Anti-PK situations

**Niche uses in PvM:**
- Extended trips where sustain matters
- Budget healing option
