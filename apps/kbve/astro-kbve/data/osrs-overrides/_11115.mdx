## Creation

**Crafting** (Level 74): [Dragonstone](/osrs/dragonstone/) + [Gold bar](/osrs/gold-bar/) at a furnace with bracelet mould (110 XP)

> *"A dragonstone bracelet."*

## Enchanting → Combat Bracelet

**Lvl-5 Enchant** (68 Magic): 1 cosmic rune + 15 earth runes + 15 water runes (78 XP)

The [Combat bracelet](/osrs/combat-bracelet/) provides combat bonuses (+7 melee attack, +6 Str) and **4 teleport charges**:
- **Warriors' Guild** — defender farming
- **Champions' Guild** — south of Varrock
- **Edgeville Monastery** — near Black Knights
- **Ranging Guild** — archery minigame

Rechargeable at the **Legends' Guild** fountain.

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Combat bracelet widely used for Warriors' Guild teleport
- Dragonstone cost drives price
- Enchanting often profitable
