## Passive Effect

**Harmonised:**
- Standard spellbook spells cast at 4 ticks (2.4s) instead of 5 ticks (3.0s)
- Matches the speed of powered staves
- Only works with Standard spellbook (not Ancient Magicks)
- Can autocast Standard spellbook spells

**Fastest standard spell casting in the game.**

## Usage

- Fire Surge DPS
- PvP (standard spellbook)
