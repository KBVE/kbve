## Obtaining

Dropped by Vorkath after completing Dragon Slayer II.

**Drop Rate:**
- Guaranteed on first kill after completing the quest
- 1/50 chance on subsequent kills

## Usage

**Ava's Assembler:** Give to Ava in Draynor Manor along with Ava's accumulator to create Ava's assembler

**Mounted Head:** Can be stuffed by the Taxidermist in Canifis for 50,000 coins, then mounted in a player-owned house Skill Hall (requires 82 Construction)

## Notes

- Untradeable
- Can only be obtained after Dragon Slayer II
- Required for best-in-slot ranged cape
