## Obtaining

Pet penance queen is a pet obtained from high-level gambles at Barbarian Assault at a rate of 1/1,000.

**Drop Rate:**
- 1/1,000 per high-level gamble
- High-level gambles cost 500 honour points each

**Requirements:**
- Level 5 in all Barbarian Assault roles
- Significant honour point accumulation
- Team coordination for efficient runs

**Location:**
- Barbarian Outpost, east of the Grand Tree

## Pet Info

Pet penance queen is a miniature penance queen. Due to the honour point requirements and team-based nature of Barbarian Assault, it is one of the more tedious pets to grind.

**Examine text:** "A miniature penance queen."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/1,000 per high-level gamble
- Average gambles to obtain: ~693 gambles (50% chance)
- Each high-level gamble requires 500 honour points in any role
- Considered a "completionist" pet due to tedious requirements
