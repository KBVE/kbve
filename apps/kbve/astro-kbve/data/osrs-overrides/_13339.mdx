## Obtaining

Sacred eels are caught at **Zul-Andra**:
- Requires 87 Fishing
- 105 Fishing XP per catch
- Use a fishing rod with regular bait
- AFK fishing method

## Dissection

Use a knife on sacred eels to extract Zulrah's scales:

| Cooking Level | Scales per Eel |
|---------------|----------------|
| 72 | 3 minimum |
| 99 | 9 maximum |

Higher Cooking levels yield more scales on average.
