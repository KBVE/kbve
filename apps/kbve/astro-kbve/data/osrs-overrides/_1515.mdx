## Fletching

Use a [Knife](/osrs/knife/) on **Yew logs** to create unstrung bows.

| Output | Fletching Level | XP |
|--------|-----------------|-----|
| [Yew shortbow (u)](/osrs/yew-shortbow-u/) | 65 | 67.5 |
| [Yew longbow (u)](/osrs/yew-longbow-u/) | 70 | 75 |
| [Yew stock](/osrs/yew-stock/) | 72 | 50 |

## Market Flip Strategy

**Yew Longbow (u) Processing:**
1. Buy [Yew logs](/osrs/yew-logs/) on GE
2. Fletch into [Yew longbow (u)](/osrs/yew-longbow-u/)
3. Either sell unstrung OR add [Bow string](/osrs/bow-string/) for [Yew longbow](/osrs/yew-longbow/)

**Profit Calculation:**
- `Yew longbow (u) price - Yew log price = Fletching profit`
- `Yew longbow price - (Yew log + Bow string) = Strung profit`

**Tips:**
- Yew longbows alch for 768 GP - compare to crafting cost
- High volume item, consistent demand from alchers
