## Obtaining

Primary sources:
- General Graardor (Bandos GWD boss)
- Zulrah (common drop)
- Ourg bones spawn (Myths Guild)

## Market Flip Strategy

**Boss Byproduct:**
- Supply from GWD and Zulrah
- Second highest XP bones after superior dragon

**Buy Limit:** 7,500 per 4 hours

**Price Drivers:**
- Bandos and Zulrah activity
- Prayer training demand
- Often overlooked option

**Tips:**
- Compare GP/XP to superior dragon bones
- Good value for high-XP training
- Lower supply than dragon bones
