## Obtaining

The Blood moon chestplate is obtained from the Lunar Chest in Neypotzli with a 1/224 drop rate.

**Requirements:** 75 Strength, 50 Defence to equip

## Set Effect

When wearing the full Blood moon armour set with the Dual macuahuitl:
- **Bloodrager:** 33% chance upon successful hit to attack one tick earlier

## Degradation

| Property | Value |

## Usage

Part of the Blood moon armour set for melee combat:
- Excellent defensive stats
- +4 strength bonus
- High ranged defence (+79)
