## Obtaining

The Hueycoatl hide is a crafting material dropped by The Hueycoatl boss.

**Drop Rate:** 11/315 (~1/30)
**Quantity:** 3 hides per drop

## Crafting Recipes

Hueycoatl hide can be crafted into a full ranged armor set:

| Product | Crafting Level | Hides Required |
|---------|---------------|----------------|
| Hueycoatl hide coif | 76 | 2 |
| Hueycoatl hide vambraces | 76 | 1 |
| Hueycoatl hide chaps | 77 | 2 |
| Hueycoatl hide body | 78 | 3 |

**Total for full set:** 8 Hueycoatl hides

**Additional materials:** Thread is required for each piece.

## Usage

This hide is used exclusively to craft the Hueycoatl hide armor set, which provides:
- Competitive ranged attack bonuses
- Strong defensive stats
- Prayer bonuses on each piece
- Requirements: 40 Defence, 70 Ranged

The armor set serves as a mid-to-high tier ranged option with unique prayer bonuses.
