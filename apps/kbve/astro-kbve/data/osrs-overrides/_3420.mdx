## Obtaining

**Crafting:**
- Use a [Chisel](/osrs/chisel/) on [Limestone](/osrs/limestone/)
- Requires 12 Crafting
- Grants 6 XP per brick

**Mining Limestone:**
- Limestone rocks in Silvarea (east of Varrock)
- Silvarea mine near Paterdomus
- Arandar mine (after Regicide)

**Shop Purchase:**
- Razmire Builders Merchants (Mort'ton) - 26 GP each

## Construction Uses

| Furniture | Bricks | Level | XP |
|-----------|--------|-------|-----|
| Stone fireplace | 2 | 33 | 40 |
| Limestone attack stone | 3 | 59 | 200 |

**Budget Construction:**
- Cheaper alternative to marble blocks
- Used for basic stone furniture
- Lower XP than premium materials

## Market Strategy

**Crafting Profit:**
- Buy limestone, craft into bricks, sell
- Calculate: `Brick price - Limestone price = Profit`

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Low demand construction material
- [Marble blocks](/osrs/marble-block/) preferred for high-level builds
- Useful for Ironman construction
