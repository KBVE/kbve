## Enchantment Effect — Clear Mind

Sapphire dragon bolts (e) carry the **Clear Mind** enchantment. On activation, a portion of the target's **Prayer points are drained** and transferred to the attacker. The drain amount and activation chance differ between NPCs and players. This is a unique utility effect among bolt enchantments, as it directly restores the attacker's Prayer while diminishing the target's.

## Creation

**Enchant Crossbow Bolt (Sapphire)** requires **Magic level 7** and grants **17.5 XP** per cast. Each cast enchants **10 bolts** at a time.

## Market Strategy

Sapphire dragon bolts (e) fill a niche utility role. The Clear Mind effect is most valuable in PvP situations where draining an opponent's Prayer can disable overhead protection prayers. In PvM, the effect is less impactful since most players rely on Prayer potions. These bolts are relatively cheap to produce given the low Magic requirement.

**Tips:**
- Best used in PvP to drain opponent Prayer
- Low enchant requirement (Magic 7)
- Utility-focused rather than damage-focused
