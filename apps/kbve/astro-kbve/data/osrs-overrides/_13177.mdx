## Obtaining

Venenatis spiderling is a pet dropped by Venenatis at a rate of 1/2,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Willingness to risk gear in the Wilderness
- Protect from Magic to reduce damage

**Location:**
- East of the Bone Yard (level 27-35 Wilderness)

## Pet Info

Venenatis spiderling is a baby spider representing Venenatis, the giant spider boss. The area is a hotspot for PKers, making pet hunting risky.

**Examine text:** "A very cute but incredibly venomous spider."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/2,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~1,386 kills (50% chance)
- Wilderness boss - risk of losing items to PKers
- Can also be obtained from Spindel (demi-boss version)
