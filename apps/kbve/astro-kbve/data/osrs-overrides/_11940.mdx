## Usage

Dark fishing bait is required to catch **dark crabs**:
- 85 Fishing required
- Wilderness Resource Area only
- 1 bait consumed per catch

## Market Strategy

**Bait Cost Analysis:**
- Compare bait cost to dark crab value
- Cheap bait due to common drops
- Low cost per crab caught

**Wilderness Fishing:**
- Dark crabs heal 22 HP
- Profitable but PKer risk
- Stock up on bait before trips

**Ankou Farming:**
- Ankous drop 60 bait frequently
- Also drop good loot
- Non-wilderness option for bait

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours
