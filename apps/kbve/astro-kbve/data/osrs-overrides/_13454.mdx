## Prayer Training

| Method | XP |
|--------|-----|
| Reanimate (Dark Altar) | 286 |

**Magic Level Required:** 3
**Spell:** Basic Reanimation

## Obtaining

Dropped by imps when Arceuus favour is unlocked.

## Usage

1. Cast Basic Reanimation spell on the head
2. Kill the reanimated creature
3. Receive Prayer XP

## Market Flip Strategy

**Low-Tier Heads:**
- Early game Prayer option
- More XP than goblin heads
- Cheap but low demand

**Buy Limit:** 50 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Budget Prayer training
- Not commonly traded
- Better heads give more XP/gp
