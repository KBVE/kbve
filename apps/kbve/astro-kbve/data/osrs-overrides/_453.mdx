## Smithing

Coal is required to smelt higher tier bars:

| Bar | Coal Required | Other Ore | Smithing Level |
|-----|---------------|-----------|----------------|
| Steel bar | 2 | 1 Iron ore | 30 |
| Mithril bar | 4 | 1 Mithril ore | 50 |
| Adamantite bar | 6 | 1 Adamantite ore | 70 |
| Runite bar | 8 | 1 Runite ore | 85 |

## Market Flip Strategy

**Blast Furnace Smelting:**
- At Blast Furnace, coal requirement is halved
- Steel: 1 coal, Mithril: 2 coal, Adamant: 3 coal, Rune: 4 coal
- Major cost savings for bar production

- `Bar price - (Ore price + Coal price Ã— amount) = Smelting profit`
