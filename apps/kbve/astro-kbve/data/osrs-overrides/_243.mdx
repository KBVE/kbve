## Obtaining

Common sources:
- Ground spawns near blue dragons (Taverley, Corsair Cove, Myths' Guild)
- Brutal blue dragon drops
- Knife on scaly blue dragonhide (50 noted scales)
- Durrik's Goods shop (125 gp)

## Usage

Crushed with pestle and mortar to make **dragon scale dust**.

Dragon scale dust used for:
- Antifire potions
- Weapon poison

Very cheap but high volume. Can be processed for profit.
