  Dark dye is used to recolor standard graceful outfit pieces to the Hallowed (dark) variant, featuring a black and red color scheme.

  ### Recoloring

  | Base Item | Result |
  |-----------|--------|
  | Graceful hood | Graceful hood (Hallowed) |
  | Graceful cape | Graceful cape (Hallowed) |
  | Graceful top | Graceful top (Hallowed) |
  | Graceful legs | Graceful legs (Hallowed) |
  | Graceful gloves | Graceful gloves (Hallowed) |
  | Graceful boots | Graceful boots (Hallowed) |

  ### Total Cost

  - 6 dyes needed for full set
  - 1,800 hallowed marks total
  - Approximately 6-10 hours of Hallowed Sepulchre

  Dark dye can only be obtained from the Hallowed Sepulchre reward shop. The Hallowed graceful recolor is one of the most prestigious graceful variants, as it requires significant time investment in the challenging Sepulchre minigame.

  ### Reverting

  The recolor can be reverted by using a dark dye remover on the Hallowed graceful piece. The dye is lost in the process.
