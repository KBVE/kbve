## Obtaining

Purchase from Brass Hand Harry at the Pandemonium for 99,000 coins (included with the Sailing cape purchase).

**Requirements:** Level 99 Sailing

## Usage

The Sailing hood is a purely cosmetic skillcape hood that can be worn with the Sailing cape. It provides no offensive or defensive bonuses and is worn for aesthetic purposes only.

**Features:**
- Purely cosmetic headwear
- Matches the Sailing cape
- No combat bonuses

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable** - Cannot be bought or sold on the Grand Exchange.

**Tips:**
- Obtained alongside the Sailing cape
- Purely aesthetic - no functional benefits
- Can be stored in a costume room
