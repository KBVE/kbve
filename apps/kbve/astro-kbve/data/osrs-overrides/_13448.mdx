## Prayer Training

Ensouled goblin heads are the lowest tier ensouled head:

| Method | XP |
|--------|-----|
| Reanimate (Dark Altar) | 130 |

**Magic Level Required:** 3
**Spell:** Basic Reanimation

## Obtaining

Dropped by goblins when Arceuus favour is unlocked.

## Usage

1. Cast Basic Reanimation spell on the head
2. Kill the reanimated creature
3. Receive Prayer XP

## Market Flip Strategy

**Entry-Level Heads:**
- Lowest tier ensouled head
- Low supply and demand
- Budget Prayer training option

**Buy Limit:** 50 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Not commonly traded
- Better heads available
- Very cheap XP option
