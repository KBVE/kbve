## Obtaining

Heron is a skilling pet obtained while training Fishing. The drop rate depends on the type of fish caught and your Fishing level.

**Base Drop Rates (at level 99):**
- Monkfish: ~1/98,000
- Sharks: ~1/82,000
- Anglerfish: ~1/78,000
- Minnows: ~1/977,000 per minnow (~1/130 per inventory)
- Infernal eels: ~1/60,000
- Aerial fishing: ~1/426,000

**Requirements:**
- No specific requirement, but higher Fishing level improves chances
- Members world required

## Pet Info

Heron is a large wading bird that serves as the Fishing skilling pet. It can be transformed into a Great blue heron by feeding it 3,000 spirit flakes.

**Examine text:** "Her name's Ron."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate improves with level and fish type
- Higher XP fish generally have better rates
- 3-tick fishing methods don't affect rate per catch
- Average time to obtain: ~15-20M Fishing XP
