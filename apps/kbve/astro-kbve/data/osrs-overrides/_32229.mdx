## Obtaining

The Fathom pearl is crafted at a boat's facility hotspot.

**Requirements:**
- 91 Sailing (boostable)
- 83 Construction (boostable)
- Saw and Hammer

**Materials Required:**
| Item | Quantity | Approx. Cost |

**Total Build Cost:** Approximately 2,912,851 gp

## Usage

"A magical stone used to precisely locate shoals of fish while deep sea trawling."

The Fathom pearl is a high-level boat facility that allows players to locate fish shoals while at sea for trawling activities.

**Features:**
- Locate-shoal: Active ability that interrupts current actions
- Quick-locate: Passive option for seamless location
- Displays directional information to targeted shoals
- Shows minimap marker for shoals within range

**Functions:**
- Precisely locates fish shoals while deep sea trawling
- Essential for efficient trawling activities at high levels
- Upgrade from the basic Fathom stone

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable Facility** - Built directly on your boat.

**Tips:**
- Requires Echo pearl (dropped from orcas and dolphins)
- Best-in-slot for locating fish shoals
- High skill requirements (91 Sailing, 83 Construction)
- Consider farming Echo pearls from orcas before attempting to build
