## Market Flip Strategy

**Tanning Profit:**
- Buy hides → Tan → Sell leather
- Calculate: `Blue dragon leather - Blue dragonhide - 20 = Profit`
- High volume F2P equivalent

**Crafting Profit:**
- [Blue d'hide body](/osrs/blue-d-hide-body/) most common
- Check: `Body price - (Leather × 3) = Profit`
- Popular mid-level training

**Buy Limit:** 13,000 per 4 hours

**Drop Sources:**
- Blue dragons (Taverley Dungeon, Heroes' Guild)
- Brutal blue dragons
- Vorkath (noted drops)

**Tips:**
- Mid-tier dragonhide
- Highest F2P ranged body
- Good crafting XP/GP
