## Usage

Ancient brew provides unique effects:
- **Boost:** +2 Magic level
- **Restore:** Prayer points (like super restore)
- **Drain:** Attack, Strength, Defence by 10%+2

Best for:
- Magic-focused PvM
- Situations where melee stats don't matter
- Raids (ToA especially)
