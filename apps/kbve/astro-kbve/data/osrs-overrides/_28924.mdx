## Item Info

Sunfire splinters are stackable materials used to charge powerful ranged equipment from the Fortis Colosseum. These glowing shards contain concentrated solar energy and are essential for maintaining the Tonalztics of ralos and Dizana's quiver.

**Type:** Material
**Stackable:** Yes
**Tradeable:** Yes

## Obtaining

Sunfire splinters can be obtained from multiple sources:

### Fortis Colosseum
- Awarded as completion rewards
- Quantity scales with wave reached
- Primary source for bulk splinters

### Sunlight Antelope
- **Requirement:** 72 Hunter
- Located in the Varlamore region
- Provides a skilling alternative to combat

### The Hueycoatl
- Dropped by this Varlamore boss
- Additional combat option for gathering

## Usage

Sunfire splinters are used to charge the following items:

### Tonalztics of ralos
- **Max Charges:** 20,000
- **Effect:** Enables double hit mechanic
- Significantly increases DPS when charged

### Dizana's quiver
- **Max Charges:** 20,000
- **Charged Bonus:** +1 Ranged Strength, +10 Ranged accuracy
- Enhances the quiver's combat effectiveness

## Charging Guide

To charge an item:
1. Have the uncharged item in your inventory
2. Use Sunfire splinters on the item
3. Select quantity to add

**Cost Calculation:**
- Tonalztics full charge: 20,000 x ~145 GP = ~2.9M GP
- Dizana's quiver full charge: 20,000 x ~145 GP = ~2.9M GP
