## Obtaining

**Stonemason (Keldagrim):**
- Purchase price: 130,000 GP each
- Located in the Keldagrim marketplace
- Requires starting The Giant Dwarf quest to access

**Tradeable on Grand Exchange.**

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 11,000 per 4 hours

**Investment Required:**
- Gilded altar: 520,000 GP (4 leaves)
- Ornate pool: 650,000 GP (5 leaves)
- Ornate jewellery box: 260,000 GP (2 leaves)

**Tips:**
- Buy in bulk from Stonemason
- Most premium builds need both gold leaf and [marble blocks](/osrs/marble-block/)
- Used for Master STASH units
