| Product | Cost | Location |

| [Hard leather](/osrs/hard-leather/) | 3 GP | Any tanner |

## Market Flip Strategy

**Tanning Profit:**
- Buy [Cowhide](/osrs/cowhide/) → Tan → Sell [Leather](/osrs/leather/) or [Hard leather](/osrs/hard-leather/)
- Calculate: `Leather price - Cowhide price - 1 GP = Profit`
- Very high volume, consistent margins

**Best Tanning Locations:**
- Al Kharid (closest to bank)
- Canifis (requires Priest in Peril)
- Crafting Guild (requires 40 Crafting)

**Tips:**
- Hard leather has better margin but lower volume
- Popular botted activity
- Cowhide supply from Lumbridge cow farm

**Similar Names:**
- Cow Hide
