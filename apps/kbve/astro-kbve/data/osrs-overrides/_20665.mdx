## Obtaining

Rift guardian is a skilling pet obtained while training Runecraft. The drop rate depends on the rune crafted and your Runecraft level.

**Base Drop Rates (at level 99):**
- Air runes: ~1/1,795,758
- Blood runes: ~1/804,984
- Soul runes: ~1/782,999
- Guardians of the Rift: ~1/1,133 per reward point

**Requirements:**
- No specific requirement, but higher Runecraft level improves chances
- Members world required

## Pet Info

Rift guardian is a small elemental creature that serves as the Runecraft skilling pet. It has many color variants matching different rune types. Can be upgraded to Greatish guardian using 1,000 Abyssal pearls.

**Examine text:** "A rift from another world."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate improves with level and rune type
- Guardians of the Rift is most efficient method
- Blood/soul runecrafting have decent rates
- Average time to obtain: ~15-20M Runecraft XP
