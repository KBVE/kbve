## Obtaining

Obtained as a reward from completing Monkey Madness II.

**Requirements:**
- Monkey Madness II quest completion

## Transformations

The monkey can be transformed into different variants by completing laps at the Ape Atoll Agility Course:

| Variant | Laps Required |
|---------|---------------|
| Karamjan | 100 |
| Zombie | 250 |
| Maniacal | 500 |
| Skeleton | 1,000 |
| Kruk Jr. | 1,500 |
| Princely | 2,000 |

## Usage

Purely cosmetic pet that follows the player.

## Notes

- Cannot be traded or dropped
- Stored in menagerie in player-owned house
- One of few pets obtained from quests
- Can be reclaimed from Zooknock if lost
