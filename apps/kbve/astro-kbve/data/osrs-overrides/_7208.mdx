## Obtaining

**Cooking** (Level 85, 240 XP): Pie shell → add raw bear meat → add raw chompy → add raw rabbit → cook on range

Can also be made guaranteed with **Bake Pie** (Lunar spell).

> *"A triumph of man over nature."*

## Boost Effect

| Skill | Boost |
|-------|-------|
| **Slayer** | **+5 levels** |
| Ranged | +4 levels |

**Healing:** 22 HP total (11 per bite, 2 bites)

## Highest Slayer Boost

The wild pie provides the **highest Slayer level boost** in the game at +5. This is essential for:
- Killing **Slayer monsters above your level** (e.g., accessing cerberus at 86 instead of 91)
- Boosting for **boss unlocks** that require Slayer levels
- Completing **diary tasks** that need high Slayer

## Comparison

| Item | Slayer Boost | Other Boost | Heals |
|------|-------------|------------|-------|
| **Wild pie** | **+5** | +4 Ranged | 22 HP |
| Spicy stew (orange) | -5 to +5 | Random | 1 HP |

Wild pie is **reliable** (+5 guaranteed) versus spicy stew which is random. However, spicy stew can potentially boost any skill.
