## Creation

Use a pestle and mortar on superior dragon bones:
- No skill requirement to crush
- 1 bone = 1 crushed bones

## Herblore Uses

| Potion | Base | Level | XP |
|--------|------|-------|-----|
| Super antifire(4) | Antifire(4) | 92 | 130 |
| Extended super antifire(4) | Extended antifire(4) | 98 | 160 |

Both potions provide complete dragonfire immunity.

## Market Strategy

**Supply Chain:**
- Superior dragon bones from Vorkath (2 per kill)
- Rare drop from frost dragons
- Crush bones for herblore use

**Price Comparison:**
- Compare bone price vs crushed price
- Crushing is instant, no XP loss
- Usually minimal price difference

**Potion Economics:**
- Calculate: `Super antifire - Antifire - Crushed bones = Profit`
- High demand from Vorkath and dragon killers

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours
