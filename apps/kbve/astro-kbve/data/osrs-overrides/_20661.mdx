## Obtaining

Tangleroot is a skilling pet obtained while training Farming. The drop rate depends on the crop harvested and your Farming level.

**Base Drop Rates:**
- Herbs: ~1/98,364
- Trees: ~1/5,000
- Fruit trees: ~1/9,000
- Hardwood trees: ~1/5,000
- Hespori: 1/65
- Giant seaweed: ~1/7,500
- Redwood: ~1/5,000

**Requirements:**
- No specific requirement, but higher Farming level improves chances
- Members world required

## Pet Info

Tangleroot is a small plant creature that serves as the Farming skilling pet. It has many metamorphosis variants to match different crop types.

**Examine text:** "A scion of the earth."

## Drop Mechanics

- Chance rolled at harvest, not at planting
- Tree patches have best rates per harvest
- Hespori has excellent 1/65 rate but limited spawns
- Average time to obtain: ~20-30M Farming XP
