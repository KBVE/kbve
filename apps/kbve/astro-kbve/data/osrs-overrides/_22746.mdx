## Obtaining

Ikkle hydra is a pet dropped by the Alchemical Hydra at a rate of 1/3,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- 95 Slayer level
- Hydra Slayer task assigned
- Access to Mount Karuulm (Kebos Lowlands)

**Location:**
- Karuulm Slayer Dungeon (lower level)

## Pet Info

Ikkle hydra is a miniature multi-headed creature representing the Alchemical Hydra. It can metamorphose between four forms matching the boss's phases: serpentine, electric, fire, and extinguished.

**Examine text:** "It breathes fire, chemicals, lightning...and cuteness!"

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/3,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~2,077 kills (63.2% chance)
- Requires 95 Slayer - highest skill requirement for any boss pet
- Hydra is excellent money, so many obtain this pet while making GP
