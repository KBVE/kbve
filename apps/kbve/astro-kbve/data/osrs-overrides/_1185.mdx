## Obtaining

**Smithing:** 93 Smithing with 2 runite bars (150 XP).

**Drops:** Rare drop table monsters, Greater Nechryael, Magpie impling jars.

## Comparison

The rune sq shield has lower defensive bonuses than the [Rune kiteshield](/osrs/rune-kiteshield/) across all categories (e.g. +40 slash vs +48 slash on the kiteshield), but is lighter at 3.628 kg vs 5.443 kg. In F2P, the kiteshield is strictly better for tanking. Members should aim for a [Rune defender](/osrs/rune-defender/) or higher for the offensive bonuses. The [Dragon sq shield](/osrs/dragon-sq-shield/) is the direct upgrade.

## Market Strategy

**High Alch:** 23,040 gp

**Buy Limit:** 70 per 4 hours

Decent alch value makes this a profitable smithing product at high levels. The sq shield is generally cheaper than the kiteshield on the GE but also less useful, so demand is lower.
