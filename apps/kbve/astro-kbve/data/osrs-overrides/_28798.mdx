## Obtaining

Scurrius' spine is dropped by Scurrius, the Rat King, with a 1/33 drop rate.

**Location:** Varrock Sewers (F2P accessible boss)

## Uses

The spine can be attached to base weapons to create rat bone weapons:

| Weapon | Base Item | Skill | Level |
|--------|-----------|-------|-------|
| Bone mace | Rune mace | Smithing | 35 |
| Bone shortbow | Yew shortbow | Fletching | 48 |
| Bone staff | Battlestaff | Crafting | 48 |

**Alternative:** Pay Historian Aldo 50,000 coins to combine items (no level required).

**Experience Lamps:** Exchange excess spines with Historian Aldo for XP lamps.
