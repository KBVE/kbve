## Obtaining

Olmlet is a pet obtained from the Chambers of Xeric (CoX). The drop rate is based on contribution points.

**Drop Rate Formula:**
- Base rate: 1/53 per 8,676 points
- Maximum rate cap at 570,000 points

**Requirements:**
- High combat stats (90+ recommended)
- Completion of Client of Kourend quest for access
- Team coordination for efficient runs

**Location:**
- Chambers of Xeric, Mount Quidamortem

## Pet Info

Olmlet is a miniature version of the Great Olm, the final boss of the Chambers of Xeric. The Olmlet can also metamorphose into various other CoX boss forms.

**Examine text:** "What has it got in its handses?"

## Drop Mechanics

- Points-based drop system
- Higher contribution = better chance
- Can be obtained in both regular and Challenge Mode
- CM has slightly improved rates
