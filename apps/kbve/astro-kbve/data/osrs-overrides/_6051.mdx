## Obtaining

Magic roots are obtained by digging up fully grown **magic trees**:
- Requires 75 Farming to plant magic trees
- Harvest roots when checking health or digging up
- Also available from Kingdom of Miscellania

## Herblore Uses

| Potion | Base | Level | XP |
|--------|------|-------|-----|
| Antidote++(3) | Irit potion (unf) OR Coconut milk + Irit | 79 | 155 |

Antidote++ is the base for anti-venom potions.

## Market Strategy

**Farming Route:**
- Plant magic trees for seeds + XP + roots
- Roots are bonus income from tree runs
- Consider tree protection payments

**Antidote++ Chain:**
1. Magic roots for antidote++
2. Antidote++ + scales for anti-venom
3. Anti-venom + torstol for anti-venom+

**Kingdom of Miscellania:**
- Can passively collect magic roots
- Set workers to hardwood/mahogany
- Roots as secondary reward

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours
