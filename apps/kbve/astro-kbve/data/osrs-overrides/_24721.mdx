## Usage

The Hallowed grapple is a special tool used in the Hallowed Sepulchre minigame. When equipped, it guarantees successful crossing of pillar obstacles, eliminating the chance of failure.

### Mechanics

- Must be equipped in weapon slot to function
- Provides 100% success rate on pillar crossings
- Saves time by preventing failed attempts
- Especially useful on higher floors with more obstacles

## About

Pillar obstacles in the Hallowed Sepulchre normally have a chance to fail based on your Agility level. The Hallowed grapple removes this RNG element, making runs more consistent and faster.

This is one of four specialized tools for the Hallowed Sepulchre, each targeting a specific obstacle type.
