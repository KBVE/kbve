## Obtaining

Purchase from Brass Hand Harry at the Pandemonium for 99,000 coins.

**Requirements:** Level 99 Sailing

## Usage

The Sailing cape is the skillcape awarded to players who have achieved level 99 in the Sailing skill.

**Perk Benefits:**
- Rename boats without visiting an NPC
- Unlimited teleports to the Pandemonium
- Note: Cargo will be lost when using the boat teleport feature

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable** - Cannot be bought or sold on the Grand Exchange.

**Tips:**
- Required 99 Sailing to obtain
- Automatically becomes trimmed when achieving 99 in a second skill
