## Obtaining

Scurry is the pet dropped by Scurrius, the Rat King, with a 1/3,000 drop rate (MVP only).

**Location:** Varrock Sewers

## Pet Information

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|

## Personalities

Scurry has three distinct personalities when interacted with:
- **Scurry** - Melee personality
- **Chewy** - Ranged personality
- **Nibbles** - Mage personality

## Usage

Cosmetic follower pet. Upon acquisition, Scurry attempts to follow the player as a companion.

**Examine text:** "I wonder if it sits."
