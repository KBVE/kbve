## Obtaining

**Cook seaweed** on range, oven, or fire:
- Regular seaweed: 1 soda ash
- Giant seaweed: Up to 6 soda ash (level dependent)

Cooking level 1 required. Giant seaweed success rate varies from 0% (level 1) to 100% (level 51+).

## Usage

Combine with **bucket of sand** at furnace to create **molten glass**.

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 13,000 per 4 hours

**Price:** ~23 gp

High volume material. Essential for glass crafting.
