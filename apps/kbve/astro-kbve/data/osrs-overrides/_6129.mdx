## Obtaining

- **Skulgrimen** in Rellekka: 3 dagannoth hides + 1 rock-shell shard + 10,000 coins
  - Total crafting cost: ~29,900 coins | Profit: ~8,200 coins
  - Crafting time: 1 tick (0.6s)
- **Dagannoth Rex** (Level 303) — 1/128 drop rate

Requires completion of **The Fremennik Trials**.

> *"A sturdy body armour made from rock crab pieces."*

## Usage

**Range Tank PvP:** The Ava's device compatibility is the defining feature. Players can wear this for melee defence while still benefiting from Ava's ammo-saving effect, making it viable for range tank builds at 40 Defence.

**Storage:** Can be stored in costume room armour case as part of complete Rock-shell set.
