## Obtaining

Callisto cub is a pet dropped by Callisto at a rate of 1/2,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Willingness to risk gear in the Wilderness
- High combat stats to tank Callisto's hits

**Location:**
- South of the Demonic Ruins (level 41-44 Wilderness)

## Pet Info

Callisto cub is a baby bear representing Callisto, the giant Wilderness bear boss. It is considered one of the more tedious pets to obtain due to Callisto's mechanics and PKer activity.

**Examine text:** "A very cute but awfully hungry bear cub."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/2,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~1,386 kills (50% chance)
- Wilderness boss - risk of losing items to PKers
- Can also be obtained from Artio (demi-boss version)
