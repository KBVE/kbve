## Usage

Add to incomplete Book of balance (Guthix god book) to complete it.

**Requires all 4 pages:**
- Guthix page 1
- Guthix page 2

## Illuminated Book of Balance

When complete, the Book of balance provides:
- +4 attack bonuses (all styles)
- +1 defence bonuses (all styles)
- Can be illuminated after Horror from the Deep
