| Tool Created | Requirements |
|--------------|--------------|
| Crystal axe | 76 Smithing, 76 Crafting |

**Alternatives:**
- Exchange for 100 crystal shards (Amrod)
- Convert to crystal acorn

**Note:** Pay 60 extra shards for NPC crafting if lacking requirements.
