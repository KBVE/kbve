## Obtaining

**Smithing:**
- Requires 19 Smithing
- 1 [Iron bar](/osrs/iron-bar/) = 15 nails
- Grants 25 XP

**Shops:**
- Sawmill Operator: 2 GP each
- Construction supplies shops

## Construction Use

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Bend rate | High |
| Construction level | 1+ |
| Used with | [Planks](/osrs/plank/) |

**Note:** Iron nails still have a high bend rate. Consider [steel nails](/osrs/steel-nails/) instead.

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 13,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Slightly better than bronze nails
- Still bends frequently
- [Steel nails](/osrs/steel-nails/) recommended for training
