## Obtaining

Rewarded by Ghommal in the Warrior's Guild after completing all Easy Combat Achievements.

**Requirements:**
- Easy Combat Achievement tier completed

## Usage

Cosmetic ornament kit that can be applied to the following weapons:
- Abyssal whip
- Dragon scimitar
- Dragon dagger
- And other compatible weapons
