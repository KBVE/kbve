## Obtaining

**Mage Arena I (Wilderness):**
1. Travel to the Mage Arena in level 56 Wilderness
2. Speak to Kolodion and defeat him
3. Choose Saradomin and cast Saradomin Strike
4. Pray at the Saradomin statue to receive cape

**Requirements:**
- 60 Magic
- Must be able to cast Saradomin Strike (requires unlocking)

## Upgrade Path

Imbue at Mage Arena II for better stats:
1. Complete Mage Arena II
2. Collect: Justiciar's hand, Ent's roots, Demon's heart
3. Return items to Kolodion
4. Receive [Imbued saradomin cape](/osrs/imbued-saradomin-cape/)
