## Obtaining

Purchase from Giants' Foundry for 15 Foundry Reputation.

## Effect

**When smelting:**
- Halves coal requirements
- Doubles Smithing XP

**Works with:**
- Furnace smelting
- Superheat Item spell
- Blast Furnace (XP only, not coal reduction)

**Applicable bars:** Steel, Mithril, Adamantite, Runite, Elemental, Lovakite.

## Economics

Opportunity cost: ~2,500 gp per catalyst.

Most profitable with Runite bars at regular furnaces.
