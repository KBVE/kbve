## Obtaining

- **Hard Treasure Trails** — rare reward from caskets

> *"An ornament kit for a pair of dragon boots."*

## Usage

Apply to [Dragon boots](/osrs/dragon-boots/) to create **Dragon boots (g)** — a cosmetic gold-trimmed variant with **identical stats**. The ornament kit is purely cosmetic.

The kit can be removed at any time to recover both the base boots and the kit.
