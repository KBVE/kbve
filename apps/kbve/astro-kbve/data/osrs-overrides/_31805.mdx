## Obtaining

The Current duck is unlocked after completing the "Current Affairs" quest.

**Requirements:**
- 22 Sailing
- Current Affairs quest completion

## Usage

"Used to track current flow at sea. There is simply no other way to achieve this."

The Current duck is a utility tool used for sea charting activities within the Sailing skill. It tracks ocean currents by following water flow until reaching a designated endpoint.

**Features:**
- Tracks ocean currents for sea charting
- Follows water currents automatically
- Despawns two minutes after stopping at endpoint
- Essential tool for charting activities

**Mechanics:**
- Deploy the duck at sea to track current flow
- The duck will follow the water current direction
- Observe the path to chart currents on your map
- Used for navigation and sea charting completion

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable** - Cannot be bought or sold on the Grand Exchange.

**Tips:**
- Quest reward item - complete Current Affairs to obtain
- Essential for sea charting activities
- Can be reclaimed if lost through quest reward methods
