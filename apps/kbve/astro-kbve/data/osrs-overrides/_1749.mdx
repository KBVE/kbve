## Market Flip Strategy

**Tanning Profit:**
- Buy hides → Tan → Sell leather
- Calculate: `Red dragon leather - Red dragonhide - 20 = Profit`
- Crafting Guild tanner (bank nearby)

**Crafting Profit:**
- [Red d'hide body](/osrs/red-d-hide-body/) is most common
- Check: `Body price - (Leather × 3) = Profit`
- Popular crafting training

**Buy Limit:** 11,000 per 4 hours

**Drop Sources:**
- Red dragons (Brimhaven Dungeon, Forthos)
- Brutal red dragons
- Red dragon bots common

**Tips:**
- Second-highest dragonhide tier
- Higher volume than black
- Good crafting XP/GP balance
