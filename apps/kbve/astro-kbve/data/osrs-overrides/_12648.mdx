## Obtaining

Pet smoke devil is dropped by the Thermonuclear smoke devil at a rate of 1/3,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- 93 Slayer level
- Smoke devil Slayer task assigned

**Location:**
- Smoke Devil Dungeon (Pollnivneach)

## Pet Info

Pet smoke devil is a miniature smoke cloud creature representing the Thermonuclear smoke devil boss. It is one of the highest Slayer level requirements for any pet.

**Examine text:** "The cutest little pile of smoke you ever saw."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/3,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~2,077 kills (63.2% chance)
- Requires 93 Slayer - one of the highest skill requirements for a pet
- Regular smoke devils do not drop the pet
