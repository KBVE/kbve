## Obtaining

Vorki is a pet dropped by Vorkath at a rate of 1/3,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Completion of Dragon Slayer II quest
- High combat stats recommended (90+ in combat skills)

**Location:**
- Vorkath's island, accessed via boat from Rellekka after DS2

## Pet Info

Vorki is a miniature version of the undead dragon boss Vorkath. It is one of the most prestigious boss pets due to Vorkath being a popular money-making boss.

**Examine text:** "A very young, very cute, very dead dragon."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/3,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~2,077 kills (63.2% chance)
- No increased chance from diary or other bonuses
