## Equipment Info

The Tome of earth is a charged magic off-hand item that provides powerful bonuses for earth spells. It requires 50 Magic to equip and must be charged with Soiled pages.

**Slot:** Shield/Off-hand
**Weight:** 1.0 kg
**Tradeable:** Yes

## Stats

| Stat | Bonus |
|------|-------|
| Magic Attack | +8 |
| Magic Defence | +8 |

## Passive Effect

When charged, the Tome of earth provides the **Earth Mastery** effect:

- **10% damage boost** to all earth spells
- **Infinite earth runes** while equipped
- Consumes 1 charge per spell cast

**Affected Spells:**
- Earth Strike, Earth Bolt, Earth Blast, Earth Wave, Earth Surge
- Any spell requiring earth runes benefits from infinite supply

## Charging

- **Charging Material:** Soiled page
- **Maximum Charges:** 20,000
- **Charges per Cast:** 1
- **Empty Version:** Tome of earth (empty) (ID: 30066)

When charges are depleted, the tome reverts to its empty form.

## Obtaining

Charge a Tome of earth (empty) with Soiled pages. The empty tome drops from The Hueycoatl (1/90).

## Usage

**Best uses:**
- Earth Surge casting for training
- Budget magic setups using earth spells
- Any content where earth spells are effective
- Saves inventory space (no earth runes needed)

**Comparison to other tomes:**
- Tome of fire: Fire spells (most popular for Fire Surge)
- Tome of water: Water spells
- Tome of earth: Earth spells (this item)
