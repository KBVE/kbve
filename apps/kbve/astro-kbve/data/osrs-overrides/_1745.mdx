## Market Flip Strategy

**Tanning Profit:**
- Buy hides → Tan → Sell leather
- Calculate: `Green dragon leather - Green dragonhide - 20 = Profit`
- Highest volume dragonhide

**Crafting Profit:**
- [Green d'hide body](/osrs/green-d-hide-body/) most traded
- Check: `Body price - (Leather × 3) = Profit`
- Entry-level dragonhide crafting

**Buy Limit:** 13,000 per 4 hours

**Drop Sources:**
- Green dragons (Wilderness, Corsair Cove)
- Brutal green dragons
- Popular bot farming target

**Tips:**
- Lowest tier dragonhide
- Entry-level ranged armor
- Very high trade volume
- Price volatile from bots
