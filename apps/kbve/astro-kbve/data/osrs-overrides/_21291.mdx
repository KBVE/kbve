## Obtaining

Jal-nib-rek is a pet obtained by completing the Inferno at a rate of 1/100 per completion, or by sacrificing an Infernal cape at a rate of 1/100.

**Requirements:**
- High combat stats (99 in combat skills recommended)
- Expensive gear setup
- Significant skill and practice

**Location:**
- The Inferno, accessed via TzHaar City

## Pet Info

Jal-nib-rek is a miniature version of TzKal-Zuk, the final boss of the Inferno. It is considered one of the most prestigious pets in the game due to the extreme difficulty of the Inferno.

**Examine text:** "Hot to the touch."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/100 per Inferno completion
- Can also be obtained by sacrificing an Infernal cape (1/100)
- Average completions to obtain: ~69 completions (50% chance)
