## Obtaining

Obtained from the Font of Consumption using an Unsired.

**Acquisition process:**
1. Kill Abyssal Sire (1/100 Unsired drop rate)
2. Use Unsired on Font of Consumption
3. 5/128 chance for Abyssal orphan

**Effective drop rate:** ~1/2,560 per Abyssal Sire kill

## Pet Information

**Examine:** "A very young sire."

A miniature Abyssal Sire that follows the player.

**Properties:**
- Untradeable
- Cannot be alchemised
- Stored at Probita if inventory full

**Chatbox message:** "The Font appears to have revitalised the Unsired!"
