## Obtaining

Purchased from Commander Connad at the Barbarian Outpost.

**Requirements:**
- 275 Honour points in all four roles
- 1 Penance Queen kill

**Location:** Barbarian Outpost

## Usage

- Magic attack bonus helm from Barbarian Assault
- Similar stats to Farseer helm
- Cosmetic/fashionscape option

**Note:** Outclassed by most magic helms for practical use.

## Tips

- Repair cost: 45,000 coins if broken below level 20 Wilderness
- Can be stored in costume room armour case
- Untradeable
