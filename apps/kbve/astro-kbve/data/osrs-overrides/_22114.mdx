## Obtaining

Purchased from the Mythical Cape rack in the Mythical Guild for 10,000 coins after completing Dragon Slayer II.

**Requirements:**
- Dragon Slayer II quest completion
- 10,000 coins

**Location:** Mythical Guild, accessed through the Legends' Guild

## Usage

Provides unlimited teleports to the Mythical Guild.

**Useful for:**
- Access to Mythical Guild basement (blue dragons, baby blue dragons)
- Wrath altar via statue
- Cooking range closest to a bank

## Notes

- Can be mounted in a player-owned house with 47 Construction
- Free replacement from the cape rack if lost
