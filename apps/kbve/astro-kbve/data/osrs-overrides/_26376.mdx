## Obtaining

Created by combining damaged Torva platelegs with two Bandosian components.

| Drop Source | Rate |
|-------------|------|
| Nex | 1/43 per unique roll |

**Requirements:**
- 90 Smithing (or pay Abbot Langley 1,500,000 gp)
- Bandos tassets deconstructed into components

**Note:** This process is not reversible.
