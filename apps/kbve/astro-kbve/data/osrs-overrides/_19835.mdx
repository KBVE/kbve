## Obtaining

Master clue scrolls obtained from:
- Rare reward from other tier reward caskets
- Watson (trade one easy, medium, hard, and elite clue)
- Various high-level skilling activities

## Completion

**Steps Required:** 6-8 steps per clue

Master clues feature:
- Complex multi-step puzzles
- High-level skill requirements
- Quest requirements
- Challenging combat encounters

## Rewards

Complete to receive [Reward casket (master)](/osrs/reward-casket-master/).

**Notable Rewards:**
- Third age pieces
- Bloodhound pet
- Ranger gloves
- Holy wraps
- Ankou outfit pieces

## Tips

- Most difficult tier of Treasure Trails
- Requires extensive game knowledge
- High requirements for many steps
- Can hold only one at a time
