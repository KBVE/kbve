## Obtaining

Crafted at a furnace with 75 Crafting.

**Requirements:**
- 75 Crafting
- "Ring bling" unlock (150 Slayer reward points)

**Materials:**
- 1 Enchanted gem
- 1 Gold bar
- Ring mould

**Grants:** 15 Crafting XP

## Usage

**8 teleport charges:**
- Slayer Tower
- Fremennik Slayer Dungeon
- Tarn's Lair
- Stronghold Slayer Cave

**Additional features:**
- Contact Slayer master
- Check remaining Slayer task

**Charges:**
- 8 charges per ring
- Crumbles when depleted
- Multiple rings can be made

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable item**

**Tips:**
- Very useful for Slayer training
- Quick teleports to Slayer locations
- Consider Eternal version for unlimited uses
- Gem is tradeable, ring is not
