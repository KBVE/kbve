## Obtaining

The Perfected quetzal whistle is crafted using:
- Enhanced quetzal whistle
- Perfected quetzal whistle blueprint (from Hunters' Rumours)
- Redwood logs
- Knife

**Alternative:** Torn perfected quetzal whistle blueprint (obtained after 250 Hunters' Rumours without receiving regular blueprint)
