## Obtaining

The Ring of shadows is received upon completing the Desert Treasure II quest.

**If lost:** Purchase another from the Mysterious Bandit in the Ancient Vault for 75,000 coins.

## Stats

The ring provides solid all-around bonuses:
- +4 to all melee attack styles
- +5 magic attack and defence
- +4 ranged attack
- +2 melee strength
- +2 prayer

## Teleport Locations

When charged with runes, the ring provides teleports to five locations:
- Frozen location
- Scarred location
- Sirenic location
- Strangled location
- Ancient Vault

**Charging requires:** Specific tablets to unlock each destination

## Usage

Excellent utility ring for:
- Quick access to DT2 boss locations
- Hybrid setups requiring balanced stats
- Prayer bonus builds
- General purpose ring
