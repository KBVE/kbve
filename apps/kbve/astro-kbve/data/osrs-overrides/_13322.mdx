## Obtaining

Beaver is a skilling pet obtained while training Woodcutting. The drop rate depends on the type of tree cut and your Woodcutting level.

**Base Drop Rates (at level 99):**
- Regular trees: ~1/317,647
- Oak trees: ~1/361,146
- Willow trees: ~1/289,286
- Teak trees: ~1/264,336
- Mahogany trees: ~1/220,623
- Magic trees: ~1/72,321
- Redwood trees: ~1/72,321
- Sulliuscep: ~1/341,000

**Requirements:**
- No specific requirement, but higher Woodcutting level improves chances
- Members world required

## Pet Info

Beaver is a furry rodent that serves as the Woodcutting skilling pet. It can be recolored to match different log types by using logs on it.

**Examine text:** "I shall call him Woody."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate improves with level and tree type
- Higher level trees generally have better rates
- 2-tick or 1.5-tick woodcutting doesn't affect rate per log
- Average time to obtain: ~60-70M Woodcutting XP
