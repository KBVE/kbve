## Enchantment Effect — Lucky Lightning

Opal dragon bolts (e) carry the **Lucky Lightning** enchantment. On a successful hit, there is a **5% chance** (5.5% with the hard Kandarin Diary completed) that a lightning bolt strikes the opponent, dealing **extra damage equal to 10% of the attacker's visible Ranged level**. This effect triggers independently of the normal damage roll, providing a small but consistent damage boost over time.

## Creation

**Enchant Crossbow Bolt (Opal)** requires **Magic level 4** and grants **9 XP** per cast. Each cast enchants **10 bolts** at a time.

The dragon bolt base provides significantly higher ranged strength, making enchanted dragon bolts the top-tier ammunition choice for crossbow users.

## Market Strategy

Opal dragon bolts (e) are among the cheapest enchanted dragon bolts, making them a budget-friendly option for players who want dragon bolt stats without the high cost of dragonstone or onyx variants. The Lucky Lightning effect adds modest extra damage, best suited for general PvM where every bit of DPS counts but the cost per bolt matters.

**Tips:**
- Lowest enchant level of any bolt enchantment (Magic 4)
- Cheapest dragon bolt (e) variant to produce
- Effect is minor compared to higher-tier enchantments
