## Special Effect

When worn, the Ring of stone transforms the player's appearance into a rock resembling an empty mining rock. The transformation ends as soon as the player moves. This is a purely cosmetic effect and provides no combat advantages.

The ring can also be used to break free from Sarachnis's sticky web attack when activated immediately after the attack appears.
