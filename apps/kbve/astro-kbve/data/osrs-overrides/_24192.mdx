## Obtaining

Purchased from Justine's stuff for the Last Shopper Standing for 450 Last Man Standing points.

**Location:** Ferox Enclave

## Usage

**God Wars Dungeon:**
- Acts as an Armadyl item
- Prevents Armadylean followers from attacking

**Combat Stats:**
- Defensive bonuses similar to black full helm
- +3 Prayer bonus
- +11 Magic defence

## Tips

- Useful for Kree'arra (Armadyl boss)
- Storable in costume room armour case
- Repair cost if broken: 25,000 coins
- Can be locked with Trouver parchment
- Untradeable
