## One-Handed Advantage

Unlike bows, the Toktz-xil-ul is **one-handed** — allowing a shield in the off-hand. This makes it uniquely versatile:
- Equip a book of balance or unholy book for prayer
- Use a defender for accuracy
- Tank with a shield while still dealing ranged damage
