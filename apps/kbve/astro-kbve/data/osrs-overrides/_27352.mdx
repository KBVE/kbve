## Obtaining

Tumeken's guardian is a pet obtained from the Tombs of Amascut (ToA). The drop rate scales with raid level.

**Drop Rate Formula:**
- Base rate: ~1/350 at raid level 0
- Improved rates at higher invocation levels
- Expert mode (300+ invocations) has significantly better rates

**Requirements:**
- High combat stats (90+ recommended)
- Completion of Beneath Cursed Sands quest
- Understanding of ToA mechanics

**Location:**
- Tombs of Amascut, Necropolis (Kharidian Desert)

## Pet Info

Tumeken's guardian is a feline companion representing the god Tumeken. It can metamorphose into various forms including Elidinis' guardian.

**Examine text:** "A guardian of Tumeken."

## Drop Mechanics

- Raid level (invocation) affects drop rate
- Higher invocations = better chance
- Can be obtained solo or in teams
- Entry mode has reduced rates
