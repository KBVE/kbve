**Cleaning Profit:**
- Buy [Grimy toadflax](/osrs/grimy-toadflax/) → Clean → Sell clean
- Small margin, high volume

**Sara Brew Making:**
- Toadflax + [Crushed nest](/osrs/crushed-nest/) → [Saradomin brew](/osrs/saradomin-brew-4/)
- High demand from raiders
- Calculate: `Brew price - Toadflax - Crushed nest - Vial = Profit`

**Buy Limit:** 13,000 per 4 hours

**Farming:**
- [Toadflax seed](/osrs/toadflax-seed/) (38 Farming)
- Profitable herb to farm
