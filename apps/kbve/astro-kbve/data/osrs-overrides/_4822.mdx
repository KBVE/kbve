## Obtaining

**Smithing:**
- Requires 54 Smithing
- 1 [Mithril bar](/osrs/mithril-bar/) = 15 nails
- Grants 50 XP

**Shops:**
- Sawmill Operator: 10 GP each
- Construction supplies shops

## Construction Use

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Bend rate | Low |
| Construction level | 1+ |
| Used with | [Planks](/osrs/plank/) |

Good option for Construction with low bend rate.

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 13,000 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Good balance of cost and reliability
- Rarely bends during construction
- More expensive than steel but saves time
