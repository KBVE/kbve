## Fletching

Use a [Knife](/osrs/knife/) on **Magic logs** to create unstrung bows or arrow shafts.

| Output | Fletching Level | XP | Qty |
|--------|-----------------|-----|-----|
| [Arrow shaft](/osrs/arrow-shaft/) | 75 | 5 | 90 |
| [Magic shortbow (u)](/osrs/magic-shortbow-u/) | 80 | 83.3 | 1 |
| [Magic longbow (u)](/osrs/magic-longbow-u/) | 85 | 91.5 | 1 |
| [Magic stock](/osrs/magic-stock/) | 78 | 70 | 1 |

## Market Flip Strategy

**Magic Longbow Processing:**
1. Buy [Magic logs](/osrs/magic-logs/) on GE
2. Fletch into [Magic longbow (u)](/osrs/magic-longbow-u/)
3. Add [Bow string](/osrs/bow-string/) for [Magic longbow](/osrs/magic-longbow/)
4. High Alch for 1,536 GP

**Profit Calculation:**
- `1,536 - Magic log - Bow string - Nature rune = Alch profit`
- Higher margins than yew, but slower sell speed
