## Obtaining

Little nightmare is a pet dropped by The Nightmare of Ashihama at a rate of approximately 1/4,000 per kill (or 1/2,000 from Phosani's Nightmare).

**Requirements:**
- High combat stats (90+ recommended)
- Understanding of Nightmare mechanics
- Team for regular Nightmare, solo for Phosani's

**Location:**
- Sisterhood Sanctuary (Slepe)

## Pet Info

Little nightmare is a miniature version of The Nightmare, a ghostly boss found beneath Slepe. Phosani's Nightmare (solo version) has an improved pet drop rate.

**Examine text:** "A newborn nightmare. Aw."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: ~1/4,000 (regular Nightmare)
- Drop rate: ~1/2,000 (Phosani's Nightmare)
- Phosani's is more efficient for pet hunting
- Average kills to obtain: ~2,770 regular / ~1,386 Phosani's (50% chance)
