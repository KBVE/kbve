## Obtaining

Rock golem is a skilling pet obtained while training Mining. The drop rate depends on the type of ore mined and your Mining level.

**Base Drop Rates (at level 99):**
- Iron ore: ~1/741,600
- Runite ore: ~1/42,377
- Amethyst: ~1/46,350
- Gem rocks: ~1/211,886
- Volcanic ash: ~1/244,725
- Shooting stars: ~1/294,270

**Requirements:**
- No specific requirement, but higher Mining level improves chances
- Members world required

## Pet Info

Rock golem is a small stone creature that serves as the Mining skilling pet. It can be recolored to match different ore types by using the ore on it.

**Examine text:** "Rock solid, some might say."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate improves with level and ore type
- Higher level ores generally have better rates
- 3-tick mining methods don't affect rate per ore
- Average time to obtain: ~35-40M Mining XP
