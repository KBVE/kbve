## Obtaining

Pet snakeling is a pet dropped by Zulrah at a rate of 1/4,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Completion of Regicide quest
- Understanding of Zulrah's rotation patterns
- 75+ Magic and Ranged recommended

**Location:**
- Zul-Andra (accessed via fairy ring BJS or charter ship)

## Pet Info

Pet snakeling is a miniature snake representing Zulrah, the solo boss snake. It can metamorphose between normal, magma, and tanzanite forms to match Zulrah's phases.

**Examine text:** "I will call him squishy and he will be my squishy."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/4,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~2,770 kills (50% chance)
- Zulrah is a popular money-maker, so many players obtain this pet passively
