## Obtaining

Skotos is a pet dropped by Skotizo at a rate of 1/65 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Dark totem to summon Skotizo (obtained from Catacombs of Kourend)
- Arclight recommended to deal extra damage

**Location:**
- Catacombs of Kourend (altar entrance)

## Pet Info

Skotos is a miniature demon representing Skotizo, the demi-boss found beneath the Catacombs of Kourend. Despite the excellent drop rate, the requirement of dark totems makes farming this pet time-consuming.

**Examine text:** "A small and cute...darkness?"

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/65 per kill
- Best pet drop rate among all boss pets
- Limited by dark totem acquisition (can take hours to assemble)
- Average totems to obtain pet: ~45 totems (50% chance)
