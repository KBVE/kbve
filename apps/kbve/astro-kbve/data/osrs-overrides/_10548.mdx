## Obtaining

Purchased from Commander Connad at the Barbarian Outpost.

**Requirements:**
- 275 Honour points in all four roles
- 1 Penance Queen kill

**Location:** Barbarian Outpost

## Usage

Best-in-slot for:
- +5 Stab attack bonus (best in slot for stab)
- Melee hybrid helm

**Notable Feature:** Highest stab attack bonus of any helmet in the game.

## Tips

- Useful for stab-based weapons like Dragon hunter lance
- Cosmetic alternative to Neitiznot helm
- Untradeable - must be earned through minigame
