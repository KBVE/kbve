## Obtaining

**Method 1:** Mix cactus spine with weapon poison (73 Herblore, 165 XP).

**Method 2:** Mix coconut milk + cactus spine + red spiders' eggs (73 Herblore, 165 XP).

**Alternative:** Purchase from Grand Exchange.

## Usage

Apply to weapons to add poison damage:
- Applies 5 starting poison damage
- Works on melee weapons, arrows, bolts, darts, javelins, knives
- Stronger than basic weapon poison
