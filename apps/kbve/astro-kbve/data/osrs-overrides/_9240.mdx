## Enchantment — Clear Mind

**Proc chance:** 5% (5.5% with Kandarin hard diary)

On activation, **drains the opponent's Prayer points** by 1/20th of the attacker's Ranged level. The attacker **recovers half** the drained amount as Prayer points.

At 99 Ranged: drains ~4 Prayer from target, restores ~2 Prayer to attacker.

## PvP Application

Sapphire bolts are used in **PvP smiting strategies** — gradually draining Prayer to remove Protect Item. Most effective in prolonged fights where the cumulative drain adds up.
