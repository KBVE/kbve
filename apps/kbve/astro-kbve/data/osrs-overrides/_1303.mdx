## Obtaining

**Smithing:** 91 Smithing with 2 runite bars (150 XP).

**Shops:** Scavvo's Rune Store in Champions' Guild (32,000 gp), Quality Weapons Shop in Keldagrim (41,600 gp).

**Drops:** King Black Dragon, Kurask, various dragons, General Graardor, and others.

## Comparison

The rune longsword has higher slash and stab bonuses than the [Rune sword](/osrs/rune-sword/) but attacks 1 tick slower (5 vs 4). The [Rune scimitar](/osrs/rune-scimitar/) is generally preferred for training due to its faster attack speed and comparable DPS, making the longsword a niche pick for players who want higher max hits per swing. The [Dragon longsword](/osrs/dragon-longsword/) is the direct members upgrade with a useful special attack.

## Market Strategy

**High Alch:** 19,200 gp

**Buy Limit:** 70 per 4 hours

The rune longsword is a popular F2P alch item. Consistent supply from monster drops keeps prices near alch value. Not the best F2P melee weapon for DPS compared to the rune scimitar, but easier to obtain without completing the Champions' Guild requirement for a shop purchase.
