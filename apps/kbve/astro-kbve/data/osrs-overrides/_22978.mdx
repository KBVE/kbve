## Special Mechanics

**3-Hit Attack:**
- Attacks in 1x3 arc in front of player
- Hits multiple times on large enemies:

  - 3x3+ monsters: 3 hits
- Each successive hit deals 50% less damage

**Best Against:**
- Large bosses (Verzik, Nightmare, Nex)
- Multi-target scenarios
