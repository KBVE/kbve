## Obtaining

Crafted at a furnace with 75 Crafting.

**Requirements:**
- 75 Crafting
- "Ring bling" unlock (150 Slayer reward points)

**Materials:**
- 1 Eternal gem (from Superior Slayer monsters)
- 1 Gold bar
- Ring mould

**Grants:** 15 Crafting XP

## Usage

**Unlimited teleports:**
- Slayer Tower
- Fremennik Slayer Dungeon
- Tarn's Lair
- Stronghold Slayer Cave

**Additional features:**
- Contact Slayer master
- Check remaining Slayer task
- Never runs out of charges

**Permanent item:**
- Unlimited uses
- Untradeable once made
- Cannot be reverted to gem
