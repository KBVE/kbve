**Smithing:**
- Requires 74 Smithing
- 1 [Adamantite bar](/osrs/adamantite-bar/) = 15 nails
- Grants 62.5 XP

**Shops:**
- Sawmill Operator: 25 GP each
- Construction supplies shops
