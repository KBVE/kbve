## Enchanting → Regen Bracelet

**Lvl-6 Enchant** (87 Magic): 1 cosmic rune + 20 fire runes + 20 earth runes (97 XP)

The [Regen bracelet](/osrs/regen-bracelet/) **doubles natural HP regeneration** (2 HP/min instead of 1). Also provides solid combat stats (+8 melee attack, +7 Str) — making it the best gloves for 1 Defence pures.
