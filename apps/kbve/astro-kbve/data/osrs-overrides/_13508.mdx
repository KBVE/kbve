**Magic Level Required:** 90
**Spell:** Master Reanimation

## Obtaining

Dropped by abyssal demons when Arceuus favour is unlocked.
- Requires 85 Slayer to kill abyssal demons

## Usage

1. Cast Master Reanimation spell on the head
2. Kill the reanimated creature
3. Receive Prayer XP
