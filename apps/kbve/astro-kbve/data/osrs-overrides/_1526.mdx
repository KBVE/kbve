## Obtaining

**Searching Jungle Vines:**
- 20 vines south-west of Tai Bwo Wannai
- Use Fairy Ring CKR, walk west
- Each vine can be searched repeatedly

**Monster Drops:**
- Jogres drop grimy snake weed
- Tribesmen drop grimy snake weed
- Clean at 3 Herblore for 2.5 XP

## Usage

Primary use is creating Sanfew serum:

| Potion | Ingredients | Level |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Sanfew serum(4) | Super restore + Unicorn horn dust + Snake weed + Nail beast nails | 65 |

## Market Strategy

**Gathering Method:**
- Jungle vines are fastest
- No combat required
- Good for ironmen

**Sanfew Economics:**
- Snake weed is one of four ingredients
- Usually cheap compared to other materials
- Sanfew serum highly valuable for CoX

**Buy Limit:** 10,000 per 4 hours
