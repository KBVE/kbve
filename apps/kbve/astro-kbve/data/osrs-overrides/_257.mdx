## Herblore

Clean Grimy ranarr weed to get Ranarr weed, then create potions:

| Potion | Secondary | Herblore Level | XP |
|--------|-----------|----------------|-----|
| Prayer potion(3) | Snape grass | 38 | 87.5 |

## Market Flip Strategy

**Potion Making:**
1. Buy Grimy ranarr weed or clean Ranarr weed
2. Buy Snape grass
3. Make Prayer potion(4)
4. Calculate: `Prayer potion price - Ranarr price - Snape grass price - Vial = Profit`

**Cleaning Profit:**
- Buy grimy → Clean → Sell clean
- Small margin but high volume

**Herb Farming:**
- Ranarr seed → Grow → Harvest
- One of the most profitable herbs to farm

**Buy Limit:** 11,000 per 4 hours

**Demand Drivers:**
- Prayer potions used by every account
- Essential for all PvM content
- High volume, stable prices
