**Magic Level Required:** 90

## Obtaining

Dropped by hellhounds when Arceuus favour is unlocked:
- Regular hellhounds
- Cerberus (boss)

## Usage

1. Cast Master Reanimation spell on the head
2. Kill the reanimated creature
3. Receive Prayer XP
