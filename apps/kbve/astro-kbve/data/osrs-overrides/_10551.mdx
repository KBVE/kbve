## Obtaining

Purchased from Commander Connad at the Barbarian Outpost.

**Requirements:**
- 375 Honour points in all four roles (Attacker, Defender, Collector, Healer)
- 1 Penance Queen kill

**Location:** Barbarian Outpost (south of Lighthouse)

## Usage

Best-in-slot for:
- Budget melee body with +4 Strength bonus
- Matches Bandos chestplate strength bonus
- Popular alternative before affording BCP

**Note:** Untradeable - must be earned through the minigame.
