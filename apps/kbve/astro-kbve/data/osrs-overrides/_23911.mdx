## Obtaining

Purchased from Islwyn in Prifddinas for 100,000 coins after completing Song of the Elves.

**Requirements:**
- Song of the Elves quest completion
- 100,000 coins

**Location:** Prifddinas (Ithell district)

## Usage

Purely cosmetic headwear representing the Ithell clan of Prifddinas.

**Features:**
- No combat stats
- Cosmetic only
- Eight variants available (one per elven clan)

## Notes

- All eight Crystal crown variants cost 100,000 coins each
- Can be stored in POH costume room
- Clan variants: Ithell, Amlodd, Cadarn, Crwys, Hefin, Iorwerth, Meilyr, Trahaearn
