## Obtaining

The Dragon salvaging hook is crafted at a boat's facility hotspot.

**Requirements:**
- 86 Sailing (boostable)
- 78 Construction (boostable)

**Materials Required:**
| Item | Quantity |
|------|----------|
| Rosewood plank | 4 |
| Dragon nails | 16 |
| Dragon metal sheet | 6 |
| Rope | 1 |
| Cupronickel bar | 4 |
| Broken dragon hook | 1 |

**Total Build Cost:** Approximately 8,734,242 gp

## Usage

The Dragon salvaging hook is the highest tier salvaging hook facility, used for pulling resources out of shipwrecks encountered during Sailing activities.

**Features:**
- Highest tier salvaging hook available
- Deckhandiness Level 4 for crewmates
- One hook maximum on rafts and skiffs
- Two hooks allowed on sloops

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable Facility** - Built directly on your boat.

**Tips:**
- Requires a Broken dragon hook as component (rare drop)
- Best-in-slot for salvaging shipwrecks
- High material cost but provides best salvaging efficiency
- Consider obtaining the Broken dragon hook before gathering other materials
