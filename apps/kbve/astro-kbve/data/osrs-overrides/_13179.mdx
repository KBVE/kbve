## Obtaining

Vet'ion jr. is a pet dropped by Vet'ion at a rate of 1/2,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Willingness to risk gear in the Wilderness
- High combat stats to deal with Vet'ion and hellhound spawns

**Location:**
- East of the Bone Yard (level 27-35 Wilderness)

## Pet Info

Vet'ion jr. is a miniature skeleton champion representing Vet'ion. It can metamorphose between normal and reborn forms, similar to Vet'ion's phases.

**Examine text:** "A reanimated... puppy? Kitten? Anyone's guess."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/2,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~1,386 kills (50% chance)
- Wilderness boss - risk of losing items to PKers
- Can also be obtained from Calvar'ion (demi-boss version)
