## Obtaining

Beginner clue scrolls drop from:
- Low-level monsters
- H.A.M. members (pickpocketing)
- Goblins, cows, and chickens

## Completion

**Steps Required:** 1-3 steps per clue

Beginner clues feature:
- Simple map clues
- Easy NPC interactions
- No combat requirements
