## Obtaining

Dense essence blocks are mined at the **Arceuus dense runestone mine**:

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Mining | 38 |
| Crafting | 38 |
| Mining XP | 12 |
| Crafting XP | 8 |

The Arceuus favour system provides bonuses to mining success rate.

## Processing Chain

1. Mine dense essence block (38 Mining/Crafting)
2. Take to Dark Altar
3. Transform into dark essence block
4. Chip into fragments
5. Craft blood or soul runes

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable Requirement:**
- Must be mined personally
- Part of blood/soul rune training
- Cannot be skipped or bought

**Efficiency Tips:**
- Use Raiments of the Eye for bonuses
- Higher Mining level = faster mining
- Bring chisel to chip blocks while running

**Buy Limit:** Untradeable
