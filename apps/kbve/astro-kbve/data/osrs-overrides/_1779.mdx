## Crafting

Spin **Flax** on a spinning wheel to create [Bow string](/osrs/bow-string/).

| Action | Crafting Level | XP |
|--------|----------------|-----|
| Spin flax | 1 | 15 |

## Market Flip Strategy

**Spinning Profit:**
- Buy [Flax](/osrs/flax/) → Spin → Sell [Bow string](/osrs/bow-string/)
- Calculate: `Bow string price - Flax price = Profit`
- Classic low-level money maker

**Spinning Locations:**
- Lumbridge Castle (closest to bank with agility shortcut)
- Seers' Village (close to flax field)
- Crafting Guild (requires 40 Crafting)

**Tips:**
- Very consistent margins
- Good for low-level accounts
- High volume item
- Flax can be picked for free near Seers' Village
