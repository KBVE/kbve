## Obtaining

Bloodhound is a pet obtained from master clue scroll caskets at a rate of 1/1,000 per casket.

**Drop Rate:**
- 1/1,000 per master clue casket
- No way to increase the drop rate

**Requirements:**
- Ability to complete master clue scrolls
- High skill levels for various master clue steps
- Access to many areas and items

## Pet Info

Bloodhound is a dog pet associated with Treasure Trails. It is one of the rarest pets due to the difficulty and time required to complete master clues.

**Examine text:** "A very clever dog."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/1,000 per master casket
- Average caskets to obtain: ~693 caskets (50% chance)
- Master clues take significant time to complete
- Considered one of the rarest pets due to master clue requirements
