## Obtaining

Claim from Tyss at the Dark Altar after completing the Hard Kourend & Kebos Diary.

## Requirements

- Hard Kourend & Kebos Diary completion

## Effect

**Ash Scattering:**
- Automatically scatters demonic ashes
- Grants 50% of normal prayer XP
- Consumes death runes or soul runes

## Charging

| Rune | Ashes per Rune |
|------|----------------|
| Death rune | 1 |
| Soul rune | 1 |

**Slayer:**

**AFK Training:**
- Passive prayer XP
- No extra clicks needed
