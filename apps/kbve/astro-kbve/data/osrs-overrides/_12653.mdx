## Obtaining

Prince black dragon is a pet dropped by the King Black Dragon at a rate of 1/3,000 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Anti-dragon shield or dragonfire protection
- Access to Wilderness (KBD lair entrance)

**Location:**
- King Black Dragon Lair (deep Wilderness, level 42)

## Pet Info

Prince black dragon is a miniature three-headed dragon, representing the King Black Dragon. It was one of the first boss pets added to the game.

**Examine text:** "I shall call him... Mini-King."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/3,000 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~2,077 kills (63.2% chance)
- KBD is relatively quick to kill, making this pet more accessible than drop rate suggests
