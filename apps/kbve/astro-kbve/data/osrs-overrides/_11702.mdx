## Obtaining

Dropped by Kree'arra in God Wars Dungeon (1/508 drop rate).

**Requirements:**
- 70 Ranged or crossbow to access Armadyl area
- Armadyl KC or ecumenical key

## Usage

Combine with [Godsword blade](/osrs/godsword-blade/) to create [Armadyl godsword](/osrs/armadyl-godsword/).

**Creation:**
- Use hilt on godsword blade
- No skill requirements
- Instantly creates AGS

## Market Strategy

**Buy Limit:** 8 per 4 hours

**Tips:**
- Price tied to AGS price
- Blade + hilt should equal finished sword
- Flip opportunity when prices misalign
