## Obtaining

Dropped from monsters during Konar quo Maten Slayer tasks.

**Drop rate formula:**
- Combat level 100+: 1/50 to 1/100 based on level
- Monsters with Slayer requirement get 20% boost
- Superior Slayer monsters always drop

**Best sources:**
- Cerberus (1/45)
- Abyssal Sire (1/40)
- Alchemical Hydra (1/50)

## Usage

**Opens Brimstone chest:**
- Located in Mount Karuulm
- Contains valuable loot including:
  - Dragon limbs
  - Dragon metal slice/lump
  - Onyx bolt tips
  - Various supplies

**Average value:** ~100,000 gp per key

## Market Strategy

**Untradeable item**

**Tips:**
- Farm during Konar tasks for bonus loot
- Higher combat level monsters = better drop rate
