## Obtaining

Purchase from Prospector Percy at Motherlode Mine for 100 golden nuggets.

## Effect

**Gem Storage:**
- Holds 60 of each uncut gem type
- Sapphire, emerald, ruby, diamond, dragonstone
- Total capacity: 300 gems
- Auto-collects when opened

## Usage

**Mining:**
- Shooting Stars
- Gem rocks
- Extended trips

**Thieving:**
- Gem stalls
- Auto-pickup gems

## Market Strategy

**Notes:**
- Untradeable
- Sells back for 80 nuggets
- Essential for gem collection
