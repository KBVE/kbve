## Creation

Dark essence blocks are created at the **Dark Altar** in Arceuus:

| Input | Output | XP |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Dense essence block | Dark essence block | 2.5 Runecraft |

Creating dark essence drains 1 prayer point per block.

## Runecrafting

Dark essence blocks are used to craft blood and soul runes:

| Rune | Level | XP per Fragment |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Blood rune | 77 | 23.8 |
| Soul rune | 90 | 29.7 |

Each dark essence block is chipped into fragments for crafting.
