## Equipment Info

The Dragon hunter wand is a powerful magic weapon dropped by The Hueycoatl boss. It requires 65 Magic to wield and serves as the mage counterpart to the Dragon hunter lance and Dragon hunter crossbow.

**Slot:** Main-hand weapon
**Weight:** 2.0 kg
**Tradeable:** Yes

## Stats

| Stat | Bonus |
|------|-------|
| Magic Attack | +16 |
| Magic Defence | +16 |
| Magic Damage | +10% |

## Passive Effect

The Dragon hunter wand possesses the powerful **Dragonbane** effect:

- **75% accuracy boost** against draconic creatures
- **75% damage boost** against draconic creatures
- Affects: Dragons, Wyrms, Wyverns, and Hydras

**Stacking:** The dragonbane effect stacks multiplicatively with:
- Slayer helmet (i) on task
- Elite Void Knight equipment

## Usage

**Best uses:**
- Vorkath (mage method)
- Alchemical Hydra
- Brutal black dragons
- Metal dragons
- Any draconic Slayer tasks

**Autocasting:** Supports both Standard and Ancient spellbook spells.

**Note:** This completes the "Dragon hunter" weapon trilogy, allowing mages to benefit from dragonbane bonuses previously only available to melee and ranged users.
