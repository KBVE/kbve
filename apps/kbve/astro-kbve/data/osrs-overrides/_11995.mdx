## Obtaining

Pet chaos elemental is dropped by the Chaos Elemental at a rate of 1/300 per kill.

**Requirements:**
- Willingness to risk gear in deep Wilderness
- Understanding of Chaos Elemental's mechanics (unequips items)

**Location:**
- Rogues' Castle area (level 50-52 Wilderness)

## Pet Info

The Pet chaos elemental is a miniature multi-colored elemental. It has one of the better drop rates among boss pets but is in a dangerous Wilderness location.

**Examine text:** "It's purely528 chaotic."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: 1/300 per kill
- Average kills to obtain: ~207 kills (50% chance)
- Despite good drop rate, Wilderness location adds significant risk
- Can also be obtained from Chaos Fanatic (1/1,000)
