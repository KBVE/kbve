## Creation

**Crafting** (Level 24): [Sapphire](/osrs/sapphire/) + [Gold bar](/osrs/gold-bar/) at a furnace with amulet mould (65 XP). Must be strung with a ball of wool.

> *"A sapphire amulet."*

## Enchanting → Amulet of Magic

**Lvl-1 Enchant** (7 Magic): 1 cosmic rune + 1 water rune (17.5 XP)

The **Amulet of magic** provides **+10 Magic attack** — the best F2P magic necklace. Essential for:
- F2P magic training
- Early-game magic accuracy
- Budget magic gear before glory/fury
