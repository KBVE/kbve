## Obtaining

Ghommal's lucky penny is obtained from Ghommal or Laidee Gnonock after claiming the **Master tier rewards** from the Combat Achievements system.

**Requirements:** Complete Master tier Combat Achievements

## Passive Effect

**Charge Conservation:**
- 5% chance to prevent charge consumption on items that use charges
- Works with weapons and jewelry
- Stacks independently with elven/celestial signets on crystal equipment
