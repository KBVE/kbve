## Obtaining

Lil' Zik is a pet obtained from the Theatre of Blood (ToB) at a rate of approximately 1/650 per completion.

**Requirements:**
- Very high combat stats (99 recommended)
- Expensive gear setup
- Team coordination for successful runs

**Location:**
- Theatre of Blood, Ver Sinhaza (Darkmeyer)

## Pet Info

Lil' Zik is a miniature version of Verzik Vitur, the final boss of the Theatre of Blood. It is one of the rarest boss pets due to the difficulty and time investment required for ToB.

**Examine text:** "A tiny vampyre queen."

## Drop Mechanics

- Drop rate: ~1/650 per Theatre completion
- Based on contribution and MVP status
- Hard Mode has improved rates
- Average completions to obtain: ~450 completions (50% chance)
