## Prayer Bonus

The **+4 Prayer bonus** is the rune mace's defining feature. In F2P, only the rune mace and its upgrade (bone mace) provide Prayer from a weapon slot. This makes it valuable for:
- Extended trips where Prayer matters (e.g., Protection prayers at bosses)
- F2P PKing with Protect Item

## Bone Mace Upgrade

Combine with **Scurrius' spine** (from the Scurrius boss) to create a **Bone mace** — a direct upgrade with higher stats and retained prayer bonus. Scurrius is located beneath Varrock in the Scurrius' Lair.
