## Item

The **Auto Cooker 9000** isn't just a kitchen appliance, it's a culinary AI assistant.
Once deployed, it scans nearby ingredients and recipes, enhances meal stats, and may even uncover secret cooking bonuses tied to item lore or planetary effects.

Developed by ByteKitchen Systems.
Reverse-engineered from a crashed alien food drone and reprogrammed with culinary AI.

## Usage

- **x{frontmatter.bonuses.cookingSpeed} cooking speed multiplier**  
- **+{frontmatter.bonuses.foodQuality} to meal quality stat**  
- Can **discover hidden traits in meals** during preparation  
- Cooldown: **{frontmatter.cooldown} seconds**  
- Deploys a permanent cooking unit until recalled or destroyed

## Notes

- Compatible with **Fish**, **Sauces**, **Spices**, and **Void-Safe Ingredients**  
- Comes with built-in **holographic display**, **flavor probe**, and **safety override (optional)**  
- Some advanced meals may require external power or soul heat
