## Item

The **Jar of Honey** is more than a sweet treat. Revered by alchemists and wanderers alike, it restores health and stamina while offering versatile applications across culinary, medicinal, and magical fields.

Rumors say a drop of this golden nectar forces even the most cunning liar to pause… and taste their words.

## Crafting

Crafted by processing raw honey from:
- **Steel Beehives**
- **Wild Apiaries**
- **Event-based Nectar Groves**

Combine with herbs or wax to create:
- **Healing salves**
- **Energy tonics**
- **Blessed mead**
