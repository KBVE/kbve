## Item

**Lobster Soup** is a delicacy of warmth and memory. 
Cooked low and long, this dish not only restores major health, but also boosts regeneration and links the consumer briefly to the rhythm of the tides.

Best enjoyed in silence or after battle, be sure to catch more [lobsters](/itemdb/lobster/)
