## Item

The **Z90 MurderBot** is a pocket console-shaped relic with glowing buttons and a flickering pixelated face. 
This cute gadget chirps cheerfully before initiating what may be a rescue protocol... or a bloodbath.
Holding it feels like playing a game whose cartridge was cursed by an emotionally unstable [android](/application/android/).

No deployment is the same, thus expect chaos, confusion, and maybe salvation.

## Usage

- **Deploys** a randomized **swarm** of drones (3–8 units)
- Each drone has **{Math.floor(frontmatter.bonuses.allyChance * 100)}% chance** to assist, and **{Math.floor(frontmatter.bonuses.foeChance * 100)}% chance** to attack
- High **{Math.floor(frontmatter.bonuses.glitchFactor * 100)}% glitch factor** introduces unexpected behavior
- Cooldown: **{frontmatter.cooldown} seconds**
