## Item

**Butter** is the foundation of decadence and alchemy. 
Whether used to baste meat, bind potions, or grease a hatch, its texture and taste are both beloved and powerful.

It's also been used to escape handcuffs. Just saying.

[Fresh milk](/itemdb/fresh-milk/) churned in ancient barrels or synthetically replicated via protein printers.
A base component in countless dishes, like [Lobster Soup](/itemdb/lobster-soup/).

## Usage

- Provides **+{frontmatter.bonuses.flavorBoost} flavor boost** to any crafted meal  
- Grants **+{frontmatter.bonuses.speed} speed** when used unconventionally  
- Cooldown: **{frontmatter.cooldown} seconds**

## Notes

- Essential in crafting *Lobster Meals*, *Fried Potatoes*, *Grilled Bread*  
- Can be melted and infused with herbs or mana  
- If left in inventory too long, may attract *Grease Elementals*
