## Item

The **Lunar Lantern** gleams with a pale glow even during the day. When the moon rises, its power peaks, casting silver beams that unveil hidden runes and shadowy passages.

## Usage

- Emits light within **{frontmatter.bonuses.lightRadius} meters**
- Reveals **illusions and hidden markings** at night
- Grants **+{frontmatter.bonuses.nightVision} night vision** while equipped
- Cooldown: **{frontmatter.cooldown} seconds**

Combine purified moonstone with enchanted glass and alchemical oils at a tier-two crafting station.
