## Item

The **Surfer Longboard** is your laid-back companion in a fast-moving world.
Whether weaving through coral dunes or gliding across space station promenades, this board speaks of calm waves, sunburnt rebellion, and the path of least resistance.

Once belonged to a pacifist war courier who surfed through post-flood ruins.
