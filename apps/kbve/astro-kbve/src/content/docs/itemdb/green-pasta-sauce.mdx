## Item

**Green Pasta Sauce** brings nature to the plate and into the bloodstream. 
The sauce is rich blend of herbs, oils, and engineered chlorophyll aids recovery and interacts with ambient light for accelerated healing and subtle perception boosts.

Originally developed in overgrown ruins by biobotanists attempting to create solar-powered cuisine.
Great with noodles, better in sunlight.
