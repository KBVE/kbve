## Overview

<ItemDBPanel data={frontmatter} />

A deployable floor candelabrum. Plant it down and the ring of candlelight steadies the mind — anyone standing near it regenerates mana over time. Press **R** while placing to rotate the stand. Pick it back up to fold it into the pack again.
