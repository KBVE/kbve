## Why Sign In?

Creating an account and signing in unlocks the full KBVE experience:

### Public Profile
Get your own profile page at `kbve.com/@username` where others can see your linked accounts and achievements.

### Save Your Progress
Your game progress, settings, and preferences are automatically saved to your account and synced across all your devices.

### Community Integration
Connect with the KBVE community through Discord and GitHub integrations.

### Cross-Device Access
Start on your desktop and continue on your laptop or mobile device - your data follows you everywhere.

## Supported Providers

### GitHub
Sign in with your GitHub account for quick and secure authentication. Perfect for developers and contributors!

### Discord
Use your Discord account to sign in and connect with the KBVE community on our Discord server.

<Aside type="note">
We only request basic profile information (username, email, avatar) from OAuth providers.
</Aside>

## Privacy First

We take your privacy seriously:

- We only request basic profile information (name, email, avatar)
- Your authentication data is encrypted and stored securely
- We never share your personal information with third parties
- You can delete your account at any time

## Already Have an Account?

Simply click your preferred sign-in method above. If you've signed in before, you'll be automatically logged into your existing account.
