## Spot Bitcoin ETFs

Approved January 2024, spot ETFs allow traditional investors to gain Bitcoin exposure through regulated brokerage accounts.

## Major Issuers

- BlackRock (IBIT)
- Fidelity (FBTC)
- Grayscale (GBTC)
- ARK/21Shares (ARKB)
