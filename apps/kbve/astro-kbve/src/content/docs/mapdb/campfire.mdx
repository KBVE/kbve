# Campfire

A deployed campfire. The grate sits dead-center on its tile and nothing paths through it. Stand a step back and the warmth knits cuts closed; stand on the coals and they take their due.

<MapDBPanel data={frontmatter} />

<Adsense />

## Behavior

- **Blocks its tile** — pathfinding routes around the grate.
- **Heal ring** — anything within 2 tiles (but not on the fire) regenerates 3 HP per second.
- **On-tile burn** — anything forced onto the center tile takes 8 burn damage per second.
