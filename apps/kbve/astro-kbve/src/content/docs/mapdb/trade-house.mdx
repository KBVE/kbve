# Trade House

The Market outgrew its awning. Where there were two wooden posts and a canvas stretched between, there are now beams, a counter, two trading windows, and a roof that doesn't care about weather. The goods stack higher, the prices quiet down, and for the first time the tender keeps a ledger.

<MapDBPanel data={frontmatter} />

## Game Role

- **Upgrade from Market (tier 1)** — reached via `BuildingUpgradeRequest` on a Market with `BuildingTier.Value == 0`. Costs 5 Gold Bar.
- **Visual remap only at this tier** — shader swaps the Market silhouette for the two-bay hall; behavior parity with Market until Merchants Guild lands more commerce logic.
- **Chain continuation** — Trade House → Merchants Guild (50 Gold Bar). Locked behind gold accumulation.
- **Footprint** — single hex.
- **Durability** — 220 HP, sturdier than the base Market.

## Lore

A Trade House is the moment a Market realises it is a business. Ledgers appear. Prices stabilise. The stallholder learns to say no. And somewhere in a back room, someone who has never sold anything with their own hands begins to write rules for everyone who does.
