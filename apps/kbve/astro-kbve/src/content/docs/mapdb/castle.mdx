# Castle

Wider, darker, taller. The Castle swallows the Keep's footprint and doubles down on everything — a longer curtain wall, two massive flanking towers, a multi-tier central keep capped with a watchtower, and four torches that never go out. The banner on the peak is larger than any single soldier's heraldic insignia. This is where a warlord becomes a monarch.

## Game Role

- **Upgrade from Keep (tier 2)** — reached via `BuildingUpgradeRequest` on a Barracks-typed building with `BuildingTier.Value == 1`. Costs 10 Timber + 24 Stone Block + 20 Gold Bar.
- **Terminal tier** — no further upgrade in the garrison chain.
- **Peak recruitment** — spawns a Soldier every single turn at 18 Coin.
- **Peak crafting** — 20 Arrow per 14s cycle.
- **Massive sleep capacity** — 20 bunks.
- **Footprint** — single hex.
- **Durability** — 900 HP, the sturdiest buildable structure.

## Lore

Every Castle is a statement. The stone is chosen for weight as much as defence; the banner is sewn in colours that cost more than the dye-maker's house. Visiting delegations understand the statement even if no words are exchanged. Wars have been won and lost on the silhouette of a Castle alone, seen from a ridge three days' march away.
