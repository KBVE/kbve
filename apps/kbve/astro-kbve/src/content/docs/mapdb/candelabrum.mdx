# Candelabrum Stand

A deployed candelabrum. The cast base sits dead-center on its tile and nothing paths through it. Stand a step back in the candlelight and your reserves knit themselves back together.

<MapDBPanel data={frontmatter} />

<Adsense />

## Behavior

- **Blocks its tile** — pathfinding routes around the stand.
- **Mana ring** — anything within 2 tiles (but not on the stand) regenerates 2 MP per second.
- **Rotatable** — placed in one of 4 facings; press **R** while placing to rotate.
