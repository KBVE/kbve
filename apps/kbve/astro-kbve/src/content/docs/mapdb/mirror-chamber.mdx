# Mirror Chamber

A hexagonal room whose walls are polished to a mirror finish. Your reflections move independently, sometimes ahead of you, sometimes behind. They seem to know things you don't.

## Interactions
- Reflections may reveal hidden items or secret passages
- Your mirror-self occasionally gestures warnings before ambushes
- Breaking a mirror summons a hostile shade — but may reveal a hidden alcove

## Lore
The mirrors were installed by a long-dead archmage who believed parallel worlds could be accessed through perfect reflection. The experiment failed, but the reflections gained a life of their own.
