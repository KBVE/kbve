# Mushroom Bazaar

A bioluminescent marketplace nestled among towering fungi. The air is warm and humid, thick with the scent of exotic spores. Everything here grows — including the economy.

## Services
- **The Glowing Cap Inn** — Sleep inside a giant mushroom. Surprisingly comfortable. Full HP restore.
- **Spore Market** — Alchemical ingredients, potions, and rare fungi. Prices fluctuate with the harvest.
- **The Mycelium Exchange** — Trade rare materials. The network reaches deeper than anyone admits.

## Lore
The Mushroom Bazaar sprouted overnight — or so the story goes. The fungi that form its structure are semi-sentient, slowly rearranging the market layout. Regulars know to memorize landmarks, not paths.
