# Luminous Alcove

A small sanctuary carved into the dungeon wall, radiating gentle warmth. An ancient shrine pulses with healing energy that predates the dungeon itself.

## Effects
- Restores HP over time while resting
- Cleanses minor debuffs (poison, burning)
- Provides a brief window of safety — enemies avoid this alcove

## Lore
Scholars believe these shrines were placed by the same civilization that built the Stone Sentinels. Whatever power fuels them has outlasted its creators by millennia.
