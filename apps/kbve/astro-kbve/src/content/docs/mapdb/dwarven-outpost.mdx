# Dwarven Outpost

A fortified settlement maintained by a stubborn clan of underground dwarves. The buildings are solid, the food is hearty, and outsiders are tolerated — barely.

## Services
- **The Iron Keg Tavern** — Hot meals and strong drink. Restores HP and clears minor debuffs.
- **Grumhold's Smithy** — Weapon and armor upgrades. The smith doesn't do small talk.
- **Supply Depot** — Basic consumables and ammunition at fair prices.

## Lore
The dwarves of this outpost have been here longer than the dungeon itself. They claim the dungeon grew around them, not the other way around. They may be right.
