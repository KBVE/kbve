# Tower

Single-hex spire. The base flares for stability, the shaft rises in stone coursed by horizontal mortar seams, and a parapet with crenellations caps it. Three torches burn at the top — centre and both sides — so a Tower reads across the map even at night. A small pennant trails from the parapet in faction colour.

## Game Role

- **Standalone build** — no upgrade chain, place directly inside Player territory for 2 Timber + 4 Stone Block.
- **Territory anchor** — emits a 3-hex `TerritoryEmitter` so a pair of Towers can stretch borders between settlements.
- **Future scout / volley** — tagged with `TowerTag` for the volley + vision pipeline to come online later; shader-ready today.
- **Footprint** — single hex.
- **Durability** — 320 HP.

## Lore

A Tower is a silhouette argument. Raised on the right ridge, it tells every traveller whose border they just crossed before they meet a single guard. Empires measure their confidence in how many Towers they can afford to leave un-manned — the structure alone doing diplomatic work the garrison never needs to.
