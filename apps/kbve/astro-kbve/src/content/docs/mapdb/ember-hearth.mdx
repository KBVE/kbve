# Ember Hearth

A stone fireplace that burns with an eternal pale blue flame. No wood, no coal — just quiet, steady warmth that has outlasted empires.

## Effects
- Sitting by the hearth restores HP gradually
- The blue flame wards off nearby enemies
- Cooking rations here doubles their healing effect

## Lore
The flame predates the dungeon. Miners who first broke through to this chamber found it already burning. Some theorize it is the last ember of a fire elemental who chose peace over fury.
