# Gold Vein

A regal deposit of gold hidden within reinforced granite ridges. Miners often encounter false pyrite layers guarding the true
metallic heart of these veins.

<MapDBPanel data={frontmatter} />

<Adsense />

## Mining Notes
- Requires mythril-grade pickaxe or explosive charges to fracture the granite shell
- Each harvest run provides 3 gold ore with a lengthy channel time
- High chance to uncover gem fragments alongside gold flakes
- Rare spawn, typically deep in ancient mountain keeps or collapsed vaults

## Crafting Applications
- Vital for enchanting conduit rings and royal insignia
- Used in currency presses and prestige-grade weapon filigree
- Powdered gold augments high-level alchemical elixirs

## Lore
Legends say gold veins remember the empires that mined them; disturbing one without tribute may awaken guardian constructs buried
in the surrounding stone.
