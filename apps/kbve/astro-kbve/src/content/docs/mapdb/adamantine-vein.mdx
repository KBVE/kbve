# Adamantine Vein

A near indestructible adamantine vein that resists conventional mining. 
Only the most advanced extraction rigs can cleave these crystalline ridges, yielding metal fit for immortal relics.

The metal will provide additional fire resistance.

## Mining Notes
- Requires adamant drill with resonance dampeners
- Yields 6 adamantine ore per successful extraction
- Overheats tools rapidly without proper coolant cycles
