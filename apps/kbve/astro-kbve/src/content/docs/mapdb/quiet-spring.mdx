# Quiet Spring

A natural hot spring that surfaces through ancient stonework. The water glows faintly blue and carries restorative properties that defy explanation.

## Effects
- Drinking restores HP and removes all negative status effects
- The spring never runs dry, but its healing weakens with overuse
- The sound of flowing water masks footsteps — safe to rest here

## Lore
Old dungeon maps mark these springs with a teardrop symbol. Some believe the water flows from a subterranean ocean that remembers the world before it was broken.
