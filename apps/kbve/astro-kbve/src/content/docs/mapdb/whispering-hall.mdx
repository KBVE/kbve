# Whispering Hall

A long corridor where the stone itself seems alive. Voices murmur from within the walls — fragments of conversations, warnings, and confessions from people long dead.

## Interactions
- Listening carefully reveals lore hints and quest clues
- Some whispers warn of traps or enemies in adjacent rooms
- Spending too long here inflicts mild confusion — the voices are persuasive

## Lore
The Hall absorbs the last words of everyone who dies in the dungeon. Scholars who have transcribed the whispers report overlapping languages spanning thousands of years.
