# Keep

The Barracks got walls. A curtain of grey stone wraps the hall, two corner towers flank the gate, and a central keep tower rises above them — archer slits stacked up its face, crenellations biting the sky, a banner snapping on top. At night a torch burns on each corner. This is where an empire stops training soldiers and starts housing them.

## Game Role

- **Upgrade from Barracks (tier 1)** — reached via `BuildingUpgradeRequest` on a Barracks with `BuildingTier.Value == 0`. Costs 6 Timber + 12 Stone Block.
- **Faster recruitment** — spawns a Soldier every 2 turns (vs. Barracks' 3) at 14 Coin each.
- **Better crafting** — 15 Arrow per cycle (vs. Barracks' 10) at the same inputs.
- **Larger sleep** — capacity 10 (vs. Barracks' 5) for a garrison-grade bunk.
- **Chain continuation** — Keep → Castle (10 Timber + 24 Stone Block + 20 Gold Bar).
- **Footprint** — single hex.
- **Durability** — 620 HP.

## Lore

A Keep is the first building in a kingdom that admits it is preparing for war. The Barracks drills soldiers; the Keep shelters them. Every corner tower watches a different horizon, every slit is aimed at a threat someone has already imagined. By the time the Keep rises, the peace it guards is no longer the same peace it was built to defend.
