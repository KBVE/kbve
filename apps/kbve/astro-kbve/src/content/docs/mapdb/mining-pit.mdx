# Mining Pit

A rough rock rim around a dark pit entrance, timber A-frame posts angling up to a crossbeam overhead, rope hanging dead-centre with a wooden bucket swaying at the end. Ore glitter pulses inside the pit whenever the crew swings a pick. A fine dust plume rises from the mouth most days — miners complain of it at every meal.

## Game Role

- **Production** — recipe buffer runs a `→ Stone` cycle (3s). Outputs ship to the Capital via `SurplusExport`.
- **Tender required** — needs a Miner on the hex (or sheltered inside) to advance the cycle. No Miner, no stone.
- **Biome constraint** — must be placed on a Sand tile. Refused by the placement validator on any other biome.
- **Ledger** — carries a `MiningPitLedger` buffer for local Stone storage before surplus ships.
- **Footprint** — single hex. Must be placed inside Player territory on Sand.
- **Durability** — 220 HP. Repaired by Builders pulling Timber from the Capital.

## Lore

Every mining pit eventually finds something it didn't expect. Seams of ore that weren't on any survey, water pockets that drown a morning's work, hollow chambers that echo back the sound of tools in ways no-one wants to think about too hard. Veterans keep a packed kit near the mouth, ready to run, because the pit is never finished telling you what it is.
