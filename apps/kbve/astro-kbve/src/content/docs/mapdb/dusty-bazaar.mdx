# Dusty Bazaar

A semi-permanent market stall run by a shrewd trader who has somehow made a comfortable living selling goods to desperate dungeon crawlers.

## Services
- Standard consumables, potions, and gear at market rates
- Gear appraisal — identify unknown equipment for a fee
- Bounty board with kill-quest rewards

## Lore
The Dusty Bazaar has been in the same spot for decades. The current owner inherited it from their parent, who inherited it from theirs. The family motto: "Adventurers die, but commerce endures."
