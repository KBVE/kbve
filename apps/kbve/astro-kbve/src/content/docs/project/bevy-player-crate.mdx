    faq:
        - question: What does the bevy_player crate do?
          answer: It is a kinematic character controller for Bevy 0.18 built on Avian3D physics, providing gravity, jumping, collision-aware movement, and fall damage.
        - question: How do I configure movement speed and jump?
          answer: Pass a PlayerConfig to PlayerPlugin::new — it tunes speed, gravity, and jump velocity, and spawn_player_entity spawns a fully configured player.
        - question: How is fall damage handled?
          answer: The controller emits a FallDamageEvent on hard landings so the game can apply damage or feedback in its own systems.
