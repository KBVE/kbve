## Overview

This page collects KBVE's **augmented reality (AR)** research — hardware notes on AR
glasses and their software development kits. It is an evolving concept page rather
than a shipped software package, capturing candidates for spatial and heads-up
experiences as they are evaluated.

## Focus areas

- **AR glasses hardware** — evaluating wearable displays such as the Nreal Air.
- **SDK evaluation** — reviewing each device's SDK for building AR applications.
- **Spatial / heads-up experiences** — how AR overlays could pair with KBVE apps.

## FAQ

**What is the KBVE AR project?**
It is a research and note-taking page for augmented reality work at KBVE, tracking AR
glasses hardware and their SDKs as candidates for building spatial and heads-up
experiences.

**Which AR glasses does this page cover?**
The current notes focus on the Nreal Air AR glasses and their SDK, with more hardware
and SDK evaluations to be added over time.
