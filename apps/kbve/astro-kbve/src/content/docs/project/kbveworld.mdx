## Overview

`KBVEWorld` is a game-agnostic procedural world toolkit for Unreal Engine. It
generates noise-driven terrain chunks, caches them in SQLite, places grass and
foliage asynchronously, and supplies sky plus post-process defaults. It also
ships a hex-world module for hex-grid worlds.

## Features

- **Noise-driven terrain** ŌĆö `KBVEWorldCore` provides seeds and noise; terrain
  chunk actors build meshes from the sampled heightfield.
- **SQLite chunk cache** ŌĆö generated chunks are persisted and rehydrated through
  the KBVESQLite-backed chunk cache and blob format.
- **Async grass and foliage** ŌĆö HISM-based grass and foliage placement with GPU
  compute-shader placement and render subsystems, kept off the game thread.
- **Hex world** ŌĆö `KBVEHexWorld` adds hex-grid noise, seed, terrain, and
  environment subsystems for hex-tile worlds.

## FAQ

**What is KBVEWorld?**
It is a game-agnostic procedural world toolkit for Unreal Engine ŌĆö noise-driven
terrain chunks, an SQLite chunk cache, async grass and foliage placement, and
sky plus post-process defaults.

**What are the KBVEWorld modules?**
It ships three runtime modules ŌĆö `KBVEWorldCore` (noise and seed), `KBVEWorld`
(terrain, grass, foliage, chunk cache), and `KBVEHexWorld` (hex-grid world
subsystems).

**What plugins does KBVEWorld depend on?**
It depends on `MassGameplay`, `ProceduralMeshComponent`, and the in-repo
`KBVESQLite` and `KBVEPerf` plugins.
