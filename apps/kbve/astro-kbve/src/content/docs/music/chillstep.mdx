## ChillStep

Chillstep is a subgenre of electronic dance music (EDM) that merges the soothing, mellow vibes of chillout music with the rhythmic and bass-heavy beats of dubstep.
It emerged in the early 2010s, featuring slow to mid-tempo rhythms, smooth melodies, and deep, rolling basslines.
This genre often includes atmospheric sounds, ethereal vocals, and minimalist arrangements, creating a serene and ambient listening experience.
Chillstep is beloved for its ability to evoke deep emotions and serve as a calming backdrop for relaxation, study, or introspection.
