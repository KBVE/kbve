## Ambient

Ambient music is a genre characterized by its focus on creating an atmospheric, immersive soundscape rather than traditional musical structure or rhythm.
It emphasizes tone and mood over melody and is often instrumental, using synthesizers, field recordings, and other electronic elements to evoke a sense of space and calm. Pioneered by artists like Brian Eno in the 1970s, ambient music is designed to enhance or transform the listener's environment, making it ideal for relaxation, meditation, and background music.
Its versatility and soothing qualities have made it a popular choice in various settings, from personal listening to public spaces.
