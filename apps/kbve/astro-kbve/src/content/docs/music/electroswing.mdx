## Electro Swing 

Electro Swing is a genre that blends the vibrant, rhythmic sounds of swing and jazz from the 1920s-1940s with modern electronic music elements like house, hip-hop, and EDM.
Characterized by its infectious energy, Electro Swing features lively brass sections, catchy melodies, and vintage vocals, seamlessly integrated with electronic beats, synths, and effects.
This fusion creates a unique and dynamic sound that retains the nostalgic charm of swing while infusing it with contemporary danceable rhythms.
Thus, ElectroSwing has gained popularity in both dance clubs and festivals, appealing to a wide audience with its blend of classic and modern musical styles.
