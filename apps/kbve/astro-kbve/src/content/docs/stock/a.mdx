## A - Life Sciences Measurement Leader

Agilent Technologies, Inc. (NYSE: A) is a global leader in life sciences, diagnostics, and applied chemical measurement technologies. Spun out from Hewlett-Packard in 1999, Agilent has evolved into a premier analytical instrumentation company serving laboratories worldwide. Headquartered in Santa Clara, California, the company provides application-focused solutions that enable customers to identify, quantify, and analyze the physical and biological properties of substances and products.

With approximately 18,000 employees globally, Agilent operates through three primary business segments: Life Sciences and Applied Markets, Diagnostics and Genomics, and Agilent CrossLab. The company's mission is to advance the quality of life by providing premier measurement technologies, services, and solutions to life sciences, diagnostics, and applied chemical markets.

## Business Segments

Agilent's comprehensive portfolio addresses critical measurement and analysis needs across multiple markets:

### Life Sciences and Applied Markets
Advanced analytical instrumentation and software solutions:

- **Liquid Chromatography (LC)**: Industry-leading HPLC and UHPLC systems and components
- **Gas Chromatography (GC)**: Complete GC systems with advanced detection capabilities
- **Mass Spectrometry**: LC-MS and GC-MS systems for complex molecular analysis
- **Spectroscopy**: ICP-MS, atomic absorption, and Raman spectroscopy instruments
- **Laboratory Software**: Data management, analytics, and workflow automation solutions
- **Vacuum Technologies**: High-performance vacuum pumps and measurement systems

### Diagnostics and Genomics
Genomics and pathology solutions for clinical and research applications:

- **Genomics Solutions**: Next-generation sequencing (NGS) and target enrichment technologies
- **Pathology**: Automated staining systems and reagents for histopathology
- **Companion Diagnostics**: Precision medicine solutions for drug development
- **Cell Analysis**: Flow cytometry, cell imaging, and microplate readers
- **Custom Manufacturing**: Contract manufacturing services for diagnostic companies
- **Research Services**: Genomics and cell analysis research support

### Agilent CrossLab
Comprehensive services and consumables supporting laboratory operations:

- **Service Solutions**: Installation, maintenance, repair, and consulting services
- **Consumables**: Columns, reagents, sample preparation products, and supplies
- **Software Services**: Software-as-a-Service and cloud-based solutions
- **Compliance Support**: Regulatory and quality assurance services
- **Training Programs**: Technical education and certification programs
- **Asset Management**: Laboratory equipment lifecycle management
