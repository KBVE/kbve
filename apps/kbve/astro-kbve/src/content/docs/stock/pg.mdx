## PG - Global Consumer Products and Brand Leadership

The Procter & Gamble Company (NYSE: PG) is a leading consumer goods company providing branded products and services to consumers in approximately 180 countries worldwide, with a portfolio of trusted household names across personal care, health care, fabric care, and home care categories. Founded in 1837 by William Procter and James Gamble and headquartered in Cincinnati, Ohio, P&G has built one of the world's strongest portfolios of consumer brands with deep market leadership positions and global recognition.

With operations spanning the globe and serving approximately 5 billion consumers daily, P&G combines brand innovation, marketing excellence, and supply chain efficiency to deliver superior consumer experiences. The company's focus on meaningful innovation, brand building, and operational excellence has established it as one of the most trusted and valuable consumer products companies in the world.
