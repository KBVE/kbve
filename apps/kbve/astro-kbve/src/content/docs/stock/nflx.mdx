## NFLX - Global Streaming Entertainment Pioneer

Netflix, Inc. (NASDAQ: NFLX) is the world's leading streaming entertainment service serving over 270 million paid memberships across 190+ countries, offering TV series, documentaries, and feature films across a wide variety of genres and languages. Founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph and headquartered in Los Gatos, California, Netflix revolutionized entertainment consumption by pioneering streaming video and transforming from a DVD-by-mail service into a global entertainment powerhouse.

With operations spanning virtually every country worldwide and content production in dozens of languages, Netflix combines content creation, technology innovation, and data analytics to deliver personalized entertainment experiences. The company's investment in original content, global expansion strategy, and technology platform has established it as the dominant force in streaming entertainment while disrupting traditional television and film industries.
