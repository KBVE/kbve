## AIG - Global Insurance and Risk Solutions Leader

American International Group, Inc. (NYSE: AIG) is a leading global insurance organization providing a wide range of property casualty insurance and other financial services to businesses and individuals in more than 200 countries and jurisdictions. Founded in 1919 and headquartered in New York City, AIG has undergone a significant transformation in recent years, emerging as a focused, efficient, and profitable insurance organization following its historic turnaround.

With a market capitalization of approximately $48.6 billion and operations spanning the globe, AIG provides world-class products and expertise through AIG operations, licenses, authorizations, and network partners. The company is committed to using its insights and thought leadership not only to manage risks but to make positive differences in every community it serves.

## Comprehensive Insurance Operations

AIG operates through focused business segments providing specialized insurance and risk solutions:

### General Insurance
Comprehensive property casualty insurance for businesses and individuals:

- **Commercial Lines**: Property, casualty, financial lines, and specialty insurance for businesses
- **Personal Lines**: Auto, homeowners, and personal umbrella insurance for individuals
- **International Operations**: Global insurance coverage across multiple international markets
- **Specialty Products**: Aviation, marine, energy, cyber, and other specialized coverages
- **Professional Liability**: Directors and officers, errors and omissions, and professional indemnity
- **Financial Lines**: Trade credit, political risk, and surety products

### Life and Retirement
Life insurance and retirement solutions for individuals and groups:

- **Individual Life Insurance**: Term, whole life, and universal life insurance products
- **Group Life and Disability**: Employee benefit solutions for corporations
- **Retirement Services**: Annuities, retirement planning, and investment products
- **Investment Management**: Asset management and investment advisory services
- **International Life**: Life insurance operations in key international markets

### Reinsurance and Capital Management
Strategic use of reinsurance and capital optimization:

- **Reinsurance Strategy**: Disciplined use of reinsurance to optimize capital and risk management
- **Catastrophe Protection**: Protection against natural catastrophes and large losses
- **Capital Allocation**: Efficient capital deployment across business segments
- **Alternative Capital**: Access to capital markets and alternative risk transfer solutions
