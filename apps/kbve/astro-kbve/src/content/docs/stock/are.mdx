## ARE - Premier Life Science Real Estate Platform

Alexandria Real Estate Equities, Inc. (NYSE: ARE) is a best-in-class, mission-driven life science REIT that has pioneered the life science real estate niche since its founding in 1994. As an S&P 500 company, Alexandria is the preeminent and longest-tenured owner, operator, and developer of collaborative megacampus ecosystems in premier life science innovation cluster locations across North America.

With approximately 552 employees and a total market capitalization of $28.8 billion, Alexandria operates an asset base that includes 39.6 million square feet of operating properties and 4.0 million square feet of Class A/A+ properties under construction. The company's strategic focus on AAA life science innovation clusters enables it to serve the world's leading life science companies and institutions.
