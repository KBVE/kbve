## IBM - Global Technology and Innovation Pioneer

International Business Machines Corporation (NYSE: IBM) is a leading global technology and consulting company providing hybrid cloud and AI solutions, helping businesses transform through technology innovation across industries and geographies worldwide. Founded in 1911 and headquartered in Armonk, New York, IBM has established itself as one of the world's most innovative technology companies with a rich history of breakthrough innovations that have shaped the modern computing landscape.

With operations in more than 175 countries and serving clients across virtually every industry, IBM combines cutting-edge technology research with deep industry expertise to deliver transformative solutions. The company's strategic focus on hybrid cloud, artificial intelligence, and enterprise modernization positions it to capitalize on the most significant technology trends reshaping business and society.
