## AMT - Global Communications Infrastructure REIT Leader

American Tower Corporation (NYSE: AMT) is one of the largest global real estate investment trusts (REITs) and a leading independent owner, operator, and developer of multitenant communications real estate. Founded in 1995 and headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts, American Tower operates a portfolio of over 149,000 communications sites across 22 countries on five continents, making it a critical enabler of global wireless communications infrastructure.

With a market capitalization exceeding $100 billion and over 4,600 employees worldwide, American Tower provides solutions and services to deploy and support wireless networks for mobile network operators, broadcasters, wireless data providers, and other telecommunications companies. The company's business model focuses on generating stable, recurring revenues through long-term lease agreements with wireless carriers and other communications service providers.

## Comprehensive Communications Infrastructure Portfolio

American Tower operates through diversified geographic segments providing essential infrastructure:

### U.S. & Canada Operations
Core North American wireless infrastructure business:

- **Tower Portfolio**: Extensive network of wireless communications towers across the United States
- **Rooftop Sites**: Urban and suburban rooftop installations for dense coverage areas
- **Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS)**: In-building and small cell networks for enhanced coverage
- **Data Centers**: Interconnected data center facilities supporting edge computing and connectivity
- **Fiber Networks**: Strategic fiber assets connecting tower sites and data centers
- **5G Infrastructure**: Network infrastructure supporting 5G deployment and densification

### International Operations
Global expansion across high-growth markets:

- **Latin America**: Significant presence in Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Chile, Colombia, and Peru
- **Africa**: Tower portfolios in Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, and Uganda
- **Asia-Pacific**: Operations in India and strategic partnerships across the region
- **Europe**: Selected market participation and expansion opportunities
- **Emerging Markets**: Focus on markets with growing mobile penetration and data consumption

### Digital Infrastructure Services
Comprehensive infrastructure solutions beyond traditional towers:

- **Edge Computing**: Edge data center facilities supporting low-latency applications
- **Fiber Connectivity**: Fiber-to-the-tower and metropolitan fiber networks
- **Power Solutions**: Backup power and energy efficiency services for communications sites
- **Network Services**: Site acquisition, zoning, construction, and maintenance services
