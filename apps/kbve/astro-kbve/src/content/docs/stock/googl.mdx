## GOOGL - Global Technology and AI Leader

Alphabet Inc. (NASDAQ: GOOGL) is a multinational technology conglomerate and the parent company of Google, serving as one of the world's most valuable and influential technology companies. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, and restructured as Alphabet in 2015, the company has grown from a search engine startup into a global technology ecosystem that organizes the world's information and makes it universally accessible and useful.

With a market capitalization of over $2.1 trillion and operations spanning the globe, Alphabet operates through multiple segments including Google Search, YouTube, Google Cloud, Android, and various Other Bets initiatives. The company's mission extends beyond traditional search to encompass artificial intelligence, quantum computing, autonomous vehicles, healthcare technology, and other moonshot projects that aim to solve humanity's biggest challenges.
