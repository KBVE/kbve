## AZO - Leading Automotive Aftermarket Retailer

AutoZone, Inc. (NYSE: AZO) is the leading retailer and distributor of automotive replacement parts and accessories in the Americas, serving both do-it-yourself (DIY) customers and professional service providers. Founded in 1979 and headquartered in Memphis, Tennessee, AutoZone has built the largest automotive aftermarket retail chain in the United States, with operations extending across the Americas through multiple retail formats and distribution channels.

With over 7,000 stores across the United States, Mexico, and Brazil, AutoZone serves millions of customers annually through its extensive retail network, commercial delivery programs, and online platforms. The company's success is built on its commitment to providing the right parts, expert advice, and exceptional customer service, making it the go-to destination for automotive maintenance and repair needs.

## Comprehensive Automotive Aftermarket Platform

AutoZone operates through multiple business segments serving diverse customer needs:

### Domestic Auto Parts Stores
Core retail operations serving DIY customers:

- **AutoZone US Stores**: Over 6,000 stores across all 50 states providing comprehensive automotive parts and accessories
- **Product Range**: Extensive inventory including batteries, brakes, engine parts, filters, fluids, and accessories
- **Store Formats**: Various store formats optimized for different market demographics and vehicle populations
- **Hub Strategy**: Mega Hubs and Hub stores providing expanded inventory and faster product availability
- **Services**: Free services including battery testing, oil recycling, and diagnostic assistance

### Commercial Business
Professional service provider solutions:

- **Commercial Sales Program**: Dedicated sales force serving repair shops, service stations, and fleet operators
- **Commercial Delivery**: Same-day and next-day delivery services to professional customers
- **Credit Programs**: Flexible payment terms and credit solutions for commercial accounts
- **Professional Products**: Specialized tools, equipment, and bulk packaging for professional use
- **Technical Support**: Expert technical assistance and product application support

### International Operations
Expanding presence across the Americas:

- **AutoZone Mexico**: Growing retail network serving the Mexican automotive aftermarket
- **AutoZone Brazil**: Retail operations in key Brazilian markets
- **Interamerican Motor Corporation (IMC)**: Wholesale distribution business serving the Caribbean and Central America
- **AutoZone International**: Strategic expansion initiatives in select international markets

### Digital and E-commerce
Omnichannel customer experience:

- **AutoZone.com**: Comprehensive e-commerce platform with extensive product catalog
- **Mobile App**: Mobile application providing store locator, product information, and purchase capabilities
- **Digital Integration**: Seamless integration between online and in-store shopping experiences

- **Commercial Portal**: Dedicated online platform for commercial customers
