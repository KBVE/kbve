## MSFT - Global Technology and Cloud Computing Leader

Microsoft Corporation (NASDAQ: MSFT) is a leading global technology company providing cloud computing, productivity software, artificial intelligence, gaming, and enterprise solutions that empower organizations and individuals to achieve more. Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen and headquartered in Redmond, Washington, Microsoft has evolved from a personal computer software company into one of the world's most valuable technology companies with a comprehensive platform of products and services.

With operations in over 190 countries and serving billions of users worldwide, Microsoft combines deep technology expertise with global scale to deliver innovative solutions spanning productivity and business processes, intelligent cloud services, and more personal computing. The company's strategic focus on artificial intelligence, cloud-first transformation, and digital empowerment positions it at the forefront of the modern technology landscape.
