## AMD - High-Performance Computing Pioneer

Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (NASDAQ: AMD) is a global semiconductor company that designs and manufactures high-performance computing and graphics solutions. Founded in 1969 and headquartered in Santa Clara, California, AMD has established itself as a leading challenger to Intel in the CPU market and a formidable competitor to NVIDIA in graphics and AI acceleration.

With approximately 28,000 employees worldwide, AMD operates through four primary business segments: Data Center, Client, Gaming, and Embedded. The company's mission is to build great products that accelerate next-generation computing experiences, with a particular focus on artificial intelligence, high-performance computing, and immersive gaming platforms.

## Business Segments

AMD's comprehensive semiconductor portfolio spans multiple high-growth computing markets:

### Data Center
Enterprise and cloud computing solutions driving AI and HPC workloads:

- **EPYC Processors**: High-performance server CPUs with industry-leading core counts and performance per watt
- **Instinct Accelerators**: AI and machine learning GPUs for training and inference workloads
- **Pensando DPUs**: Data processing units for network acceleration and security
- **Versal Adaptive SoCs**: FPGA-based solutions for adaptive computing workloads

### Client
Consumer and commercial processors for laptops, desktops, and workstations:

- **Ryzen Processors**: High-performance consumer CPUs for gaming, content creation, and productivity
- **Ryzen AI**: Next-generation processors with integrated AI acceleration capabilities
- **Ryzen PRO**: Commercial-grade processors with enhanced security and manageability
- **Threadripper**: High-end desktop processors for enthusiasts and content creators

### Gaming
Graphics solutions for gaming consoles, PCs, and professional applications:

- **Radeon Graphics**: Consumer graphics cards for gaming and content creation
- **Radeon PRO**: Professional graphics solutions for workstations and content creation
- **Semi-Custom SoCs**: Custom processors for gaming consoles including PlayStation and Xbox
- **Gaming Software**: Adrenalin software suite and developer tools

### Embedded
Specialized processors for automotive, industrial, and edge computing applications:

- **Embedded Processors**: Low-power solutions for industrial and automotive applications
- **Adaptive SoCs**: FPGA-based solutions for edge AI and communications infrastructure
- **Smart NICs**: Network interface cards for data center and edge applications
- **Custom Silicon**: Semi-custom solutions for specific customer requirements
