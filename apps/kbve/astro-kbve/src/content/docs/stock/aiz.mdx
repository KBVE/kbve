## AIZ - Global Lifestyle and Housing Solutions Provider

Assurant, Inc. (NYSE: AIZ) is a leading global provider of lifestyle and housing solutions that support, protect, and connect major consumer purchases. Founded in 1892 and headquartered in New York City, Assurant has evolved into a comprehensive risk management and customer service company serving consumers, businesses, and strategic partners worldwide through innovative products and services that enhance and protect the consumer experience.

With operations in 21 countries and approximately 15,000 employees globally, Assurant provides specialized insurance, warranty, and service solutions across diverse consumer markets including mobile devices, vehicles, homes, and renters. The company's integrated approach combines risk management expertise, technology innovation, and exceptional customer service to deliver value-added solutions that protect consumers' most important purchases and lifestyle needs.

## Comprehensive Consumer Protection Platform

Assurant operates through three primary business segments:

### Global Lifestyle
Device protection and lifestyle solutions for consumer electronics:

- **Mobile Device Protection**: Comprehensive protection plans for smartphones, tablets, and wearable devices
- **Extended Warranties**: Extended warranty programs for consumer electronics and appliances
- **Vehicle Protection**: Mechanical breakdown insurance and extended warranties for vehicles
- **Credit Insurance**: Credit protection products for loan and credit card holders
- **Service Contracts**: Service and repair programs for home appliances and electronics
- **Global Programs**: International device protection and warranty programs

### Global Housing
Housing and renters solutions protecting property and lifestyle:

- **Lender-Placed Insurance**: Property insurance for mortgage servicers and lenders
- **Renters Insurance**: Comprehensive renters insurance protecting personal property and liability
- **Condo and Cooperative Insurance**: Specialized insurance for condominium and cooperative properties
- **Flood Insurance**: National Flood Insurance Program administration and servicing
- **Real Estate Solutions**: Property inspection, valuation, and REO (real estate owned) services
- **Manufactured Housing**: Insurance solutions for manufactured and mobile homes

### Global Preneed
Preneed funeral insurance and services:

- **Preneed Funeral Insurance**: Life insurance products funding future funeral expenses
- **Trust Services**: Preneed trust administration and investment management
- **Funeral Home Services**: Technology and administrative services for funeral homes
- **Claims Processing**: Efficient claims processing and customer service for preneed policies
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Compliance with state preneed insurance regulations
