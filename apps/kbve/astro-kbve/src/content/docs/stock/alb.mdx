## ALB - Essential Elements for Energy Transition

Albemarle Corporation (NYSE: ALB) is a leading global specialty chemicals company that develops, manufactures, and markets engineered specialty chemicals for diverse end markets. Founded in 1887 and headquartered in Charlotte, North Carolina, Albemarle has evolved into one of the world's largest producers of lithium, bromine, and catalysts, serving critical industries including energy storage, mobility, connectivity, and health.

With approximately 8,300 employees operating across 70 countries, Albemarle plays a fundamental role in enabling the global energy transition through its lithium products for electric vehicle batteries and energy storage systems. The company combines deep technical expertise with global manufacturing capabilities to deliver essential materials that power modern life.

## Business Segments

Albemarle operates through three primary business segments serving diverse industrial applications:

### Energy Storage
Leading supplier of lithium compounds for battery applications:

- **Lithium Carbonate**: High-purity lithium carbonate for lithium-ion battery cathodes
- **Lithium Hydroxide**: Battery-grade lithium hydroxide for high-performance EV batteries
- **Lithium Chloride**: Industrial-grade lithium for various chemical processes
- **Technical Services**: Handling, storage, and application support for reactive lithium products
- **Recycling Services**: Lithium recovery and recycling from battery manufacturing by-products
- **Hard Rock Resources**: Spodumene mining and conversion operations
- **Brine Resources**: Lithium extraction from salt brine operations

### Specialties
Bromine and specialized lithium solutions for diverse industries:

- **Flame Retardants**: Bromine-based fire safety compounds for electronics and construction
- **Clear Brine Fluids**: Specialized drilling and completion fluids for oil and gas
- **Elemental Bromine**: High-purity bromine for pharmaceutical and chemical synthesis
- **Organolithium Products**: Butyllithium and other specialized lithium reagents
- **Cesium Products**: High-purity cesium compounds for specialized applications
- **Fine Chemicals**: Custom synthesis and manufacturing services

### Ketjen
Catalysts and catalyst technologies for refining and petrochemicals:

- **Hydroprocessing Catalysts**: Catalysts for fuel desulfurization and upgrading
- **FCC Catalysts**: Fluidized catalytic cracking catalysts and additives
- **Organometallics**: Catalyst precursors and specialty chemical intermediates
- **Clean Fuels Technology**: Advanced catalysts for renewable fuel production
- **Performance Solutions**: Custom catalyst development and technical services
