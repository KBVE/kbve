## ABBV - Leading Biopharmaceutical Innovator

AbbVie Inc. (NYSE: ABBV) is a research-based biopharmaceutical company that discovers, develops, manufactures, and commercializes innovative therapies worldwide. Spun off from Abbott Laboratories in 2013, AbbVie has established itself as one of the leading pharmaceutical companies globally, with a focus on addressing some of the world's most complex and serious diseases.

Headquartered in North Chicago, Illinois, AbbVie employs approximately 55,000 people worldwide and operates in more than 75 countries. The company's mission is to discover and deliver innovative medicines and solutions that solve serious health issues today and address the medical challenges of tomorrow, with a particular emphasis on immunology, oncology, neuroscience, and eye care.

## Therapeutic Focus Areas

AbbVie's research and development efforts are concentrated in four key therapeutic areas:

### Immunology
AbbVie's immunology franchise represents its largest and most successful therapeutic area:

- **Humira (adalimumab)**: The world's best-selling drug for treating autoimmune diseases including rheumatoid arthritis, psoriasis, and inflammatory bowel disease
- **Skyrizi (risankizumab)**: Next-generation treatment for psoriasis, psoriatic arthritis, and Crohn's disease
- **Rinvoq (upadacitinib)**: JAK inhibitor for rheumatoid arthritis, psoriatic arthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, and other inflammatory conditions

### Oncology
Growing focus on hematologic malignancies and solid tumors:

- **Imbruvica (ibrutinib)**: BTK inhibitor for various blood cancers including chronic lymphocytic leukemia and mantle cell lymphoma
- **Venclexta/Venclyxto (venetoclax)**: BCL-2 inhibitor for acute myeloid leukemia and chronic lymphocytic leukemia
- **Elahere (mirvetuximab soravtansine)**: Antibody-drug conjugate for ovarian cancer
- **Epkinly (epcoritamab)**: Bispecific antibody for lymphoma

### Neuroscience
Comprehensive portfolio addressing neurological and psychiatric conditions:

- **Vraylar (cariprazine)**: Antipsychotic for schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, and major depressive disorder
- **Botox**: Therapeutic applications for neurologic diseases including chronic migraine, overactive bladder, and spasticity
- **Duopa/Duodopa**: Continuous infusion therapy for advanced Parkinson's disease
- **Ubrelvy and Qulipta**: Migraine treatments for acute and preventive care

### Eye Care
Specialized treatments for various ophthalmologic conditions:

- **Ozurdex**: Sustained-release implant for diabetic macular edema and retinal vein occlusion
- **Lumigan/Ganfort**: Treatments for glaucoma and ocular hypertension
- **Restasis**: Chronic dry eye treatment
