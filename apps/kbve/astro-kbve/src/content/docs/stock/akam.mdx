## AKAM - Edge Computing and Security Pioneer

Akamai Technologies, Inc. (NASDAQ: AKAM) is a leading provider of content delivery network (CDN), cybersecurity, and cloud computing solutions that power and protect digital experiences. Founded in 1998 and headquartered in Cambridge, Massachusetts, Akamai operates one of the world's largest distributed computing platforms, with hundreds of thousands of servers deployed across more than 135 countries and over 4,000 locations worldwide.

With approximately 10,811 employees globally, Akamai enables organizations to deliver fast, secure, and reliable digital experiences to billions of users worldwide. The company's mission is to power and protect life online by making the internet fast, reliable, and secure for its customers and their users.
