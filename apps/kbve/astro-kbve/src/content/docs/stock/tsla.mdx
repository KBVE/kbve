## TSLA

Tesla, Inc. (NASDAQ: TSLA) is a multinational automotive and clean energy company founded by Elon Musk and others in 2003.
The company is best known for manufacturing electric vehicles, including the Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y, but has expanded into energy storage systems, solar panels, and autonomous driving technology.
Tesla has revolutionized the automotive industry by making electric vehicles mainstream and continues to be a leader in sustainable transportation and energy solutions. The company's stock has been known for its volatility, driven by factors including production milestones, regulatory changes, and CEO announcements.
