## XOM - Global Energy and Chemical Solutions Leader

Exxon Mobil Corporation (NYSE: XOM) is one of the world's largest publicly traded energy companies, engaged in exploring, producing, transporting, and selling crude oil and natural gas, as well as manufacturing and marketing petroleum products and chemicals. Founded in 1999 through the merger of Exxon and Mobil and headquartered in Irving, Texas, ExxonMobil operates across the entire energy value chain with significant operations in more than 50 countries worldwide.

With proven reserves, advanced technology capabilities, and integrated operations spanning upstream, downstream, and chemical businesses, ExxonMobil combines resource development expertise with manufacturing excellence to meet global energy and chemical demand. The company's focus on operational efficiency, technology innovation, and capital discipline positions it as a leader in traditional energy production while investing in emerging energy technologies and carbon capture solutions.

## Comprehensive Energy and Chemical Operations

ExxonMobil operates through three primary business segments:

### Upstream
Oil and natural gas exploration, development, and production:

- **Crude Oil Production**: Global crude oil production from conventional and unconventional resources
- **Natural Gas Production**: Natural gas exploration, development, and production
- **Permian Basin**: Leading position in the prolific Permian Basin with efficient drilling operations
- **Guyana Operations**: Major deepwater oil discoveries and production development
- **LNG Operations**: Liquefied natural gas production and export capabilities
- **International Projects**: Global portfolio of oil and gas development projects

### Downstream
Petroleum refining and marketing operations:

- **Refining**: Integrated refining operations producing gasoline, diesel, and other petroleum products
- **Marketing**: Retail gasoline stations and commercial fuel distribution
- **Lubes and Specialties**: High-performance lubricants and specialty petroleum products
- **Supply Chain**: Global supply chain and logistics for petroleum product distribution
- **Branded Products**: Exxon, Mobil, and Esso branded fuel and convenience retail
- **Aviation Fuels**: Jet fuel and aviation fuel products for commercial and military markets

### Chemical
Petrochemical manufacturing and specialty chemical products:

- **Basic Chemicals**: Ethylene, propylene, and other basic petrochemical building blocks
- **Specialty Products**: High-performance plastics, elastomers, and specialty chemicals
- **Performance Products**: Advanced materials for automotive, packaging, and industrial applications
- **Technology Licensing**: Chemical process technology licensing and catalysts
- **Global Manufacturing**: Integrated chemical manufacturing facilities worldwide
- **Circular Economy**: Chemical recycling and sustainability initiatives
