## V - Global Digital Payments Technology Leader

Visa Inc. (NYSE: V) is the world's leading digital payments technology company connecting consumers, businesses, banks, and governments in more than 200 countries and territories, facilitating secure and reliable electronic payments worldwide. Founded in 1958 and headquartered in San Francisco, California, Visa operates one of the world's most advanced payment processing networks, enabling trillions of dollars in payment volume annually and serving as critical infrastructure for the global economy.

With operations spanning the globe and serving billions of cardholders through thousands of financial institutions, Visa combines network scale, security technology, and data analytics to enable seamless digital commerce. The company's four-party model, connecting cardholders, merchants, issuing banks, and acquiring banks, creates a powerful platform that facilitates economic growth and financial inclusion while generating attractive returns for shareholders.
