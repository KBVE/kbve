## ATO - Leading Natural Gas Distribution Utility

Atmos Energy Corporation (NYSE: ATO) is one of the largest natural gas distribution companies in the United States, safely delivering reliable, affordable, efficient, and abundant natural gas to over 3 million residential, commercial, public authority, and industrial customers across eight states. Founded in 1906 and headquartered in Dallas, Texas, Atmos Energy has built a comprehensive natural gas distribution network spanning approximately 76,000 miles of distribution mains and 80,000 miles of service lines.

With a regulated utility business model focused on safe and reliable natural gas distribution, Atmos Energy operates through two primary business segments: Distribution and Pipeline and Storage. The company's commitment to safety, operational excellence, and customer service has established it as a leading utility serving diverse communities across the South and Midwest regions of the United States.

## Comprehensive Natural Gas Operations

Atmos Energy operates through two distinct business segments:

### Distribution Segment
Regulated natural gas distribution operations:

- **Residential Service**: Natural gas distribution to homes for heating, cooking, and water heating
- **Commercial Service**: Gas distribution to businesses, restaurants, and commercial facilities
- **Industrial Service**: Natural gas supply to manufacturing and industrial customers
- **Public Authority**: Gas service to schools, hospitals, and government facilities
- **Distribution Infrastructure**: Extensive pipeline network connecting customers to natural gas supply
- **Emergency Response**: 24/7 emergency response and leak detection services

### Pipeline and Storage Segment
Regulated natural gas transmission and storage operations:

- **Interstate Pipeline**: Natural gas transmission through the APT system serving Louisiana and Texas
- **Intrastate Pipeline**: Texas intrastate natural gas transmission and transportation
- **Underground Storage**: Natural gas storage facilities providing supply reliability
- **System Interconnections**: Pipeline interconnections with major interstate transmission systems
- **Capacity Services**: Transportation and storage capacity services for natural gas suppliers
- **System Optimization**: Pipeline optimization and efficiency improvement initiatives
