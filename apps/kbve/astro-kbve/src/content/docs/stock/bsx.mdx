## BSX - Global Medical Technology Innovation Leader

Boston Scientific Corporation (NYSE: BSX) is a leading global medical technology company that develops, manufactures, and markets medical devices and technologies used in interventional medical specialties, including cardiology, endoscopy, urology, and neuromodulation. Founded in 1979 and headquartered in Marlborough, Massachusetts, Boston Scientific has established itself as a premier innovator in minimally invasive medical solutions that improve patient outcomes and reduce healthcare costs.

With operations in approximately 40 countries and products sold in more than 130 countries worldwide, Boston Scientific serves healthcare providers and patients through a comprehensive portfolio of medical devices spanning cardiovascular, rhythm and neuro, and MedSurg categories. The company's commitment to research and development, clinical evidence generation, and innovative therapy solutions positions it at the forefront of medical technology advancement.

## Comprehensive Medical Technology Portfolio

Boston Scientific operates through three primary business segments:

### Cardiovascular
Leading cardiovascular intervention and structural heart solutions:

- **Interventional Cardiology**: Coronary stents, balloon catheters, and atherectomy systems
- **Structural Heart**: Transcatheter aortic valve replacement (TAVR) and mitral valve repair systems
- **Peripheral Interventions**: Peripheral artery disease treatment and vascular intervention devices
- **Coronary Stents**: Drug-eluting stents and balloon-expandable stent systems
- **Imaging Systems**: Intravascular imaging and optical coherence tomography
- **Hemodynamics**: Hemodynamic monitoring and cardiac support systems

### Rhythm and Neuro
Advanced cardiac rhythm management and neurological intervention solutions:

- **Cardiac Rhythm Management**: Pacemakers, defibrillators, and cardiac resynchronization therapy
- **Electrophysiology**: Ablation catheters and mapping systems for arrhythmia treatment
- **Neuromodulation**: Spinal cord stimulation and deep brain stimulation systems
- **Interventional Neuroradiology**: Neurovascular devices for stroke and aneurysm treatment
- **Remote Monitoring**: Cardiac device remote monitoring and patient management systems

### MedSurg
Minimally invasive surgical solutions across multiple specialties:

- **Endoscopy**: Gastrointestinal endoscopy devices and accessories
- **Urology**: Urological devices including stone management and benign prostatic hyperplasia treatment
- **Gynecology**: Minimally invasive gynecological surgical solutions
- **Pulmonology**: Interventional pulmonology devices and bronchoscopy solutions
- **Oncology**: Radiation oncology and interventional oncology devices
- **Pain Management**: Pain management devices and minimally invasive spine solutions
