## AVY - Materials Science and Digital Identification Leader

Avery Dennison Corporation (NYSE: AVY) is a leading global materials science and digital identification solutions company, transforming how brands and businesses connect with consumers through innovative labeling and functional materials, radio frequency identification (RFID), and software solutions. Founded in 1935 and headquartered in Mentor, Ohio, Avery Dennison has evolved from a pressure-sensitive adhesive materials company into a comprehensive identification and material science platform serving diverse end markets.

With approximately 35,000 employees across 50+ countries and manufacturing facilities on six continents, Avery Dennison combines materials science expertise with digital innovation to enable supply chain transparency, brand differentiation, and consumer engagement. The company's solutions span from traditional labels and packaging materials to intelligent labels that digitally connect physical products to online experiences.

## Comprehensive Materials and Identification Platform

Avery Dennison operates through three primary business segments:

### Label and Graphic Materials (LGM)
Pressure-sensitive materials and solutions for labeling applications:

- **Label Materials**: Pressure-sensitive label stocks for consumer goods, logistics, and industrial applications
- **Graphics Materials**: Films and adhesives for outdoor graphics, automotive, and architectural applications
- **Reflective Materials**: Safety and visibility solutions for transportation and personal protection
- **Performance Tapes**: Specialty adhesive tapes for automotive, electronics, and industrial applications
- **Converting Technologies**: Materials for label converters and printing companies
- **Sustainable Solutions**: Recycled and bio-based materials supporting circular economy

### Retail Branding and Information Solutions (RBIS)
Brand identification and information management for apparel and retail:

- **Apparel Labels**: Care labels, brand labels, and hangtags for clothing manufacturers
- **Price Tickets**: Retail price tickets and promotional materials
- **RFID Solutions**: Radio frequency identification tags and systems for inventory management
- **Digital Solutions**: Software platforms connecting physical products to digital experiences
- **Brand Protection**: Security and authentication solutions preventing counterfeiting
- **Packaging Solutions**: Flexible packaging and specialty materials for retail applications

### Industrial and Healthcare Materials (IHM)
Specialty materials for industrial and healthcare applications:

- **Medical Materials**: Adhesive materials for medical devices, wound care, and healthcare applications
- **Industrial Tapes**: High-performance tapes for aerospace, automotive, and electronics
- **Construction Materials**: Building and construction adhesive solutions
- **Electronics Materials**: Materials for flexible printed circuits and electronic devices
- **Automotive Materials**: Interior and exterior automotive adhesive applications
- **Specialty Chemicals**: Performance chemicals and adhesive technologies
