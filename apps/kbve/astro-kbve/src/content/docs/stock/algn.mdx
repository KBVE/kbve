## ALGN - Digital Orthodontics Pioneer

Align Technology, Inc. (NASDAQ: ALGN) is a global medical device company that has revolutionized orthodontic treatment through innovative digital technologies. Founded in 1997 and headquartered in Tempe, Arizona, Align Technology is best known for developing the Invisalign system, the world's most advanced clear aligner treatment that has transformed the smiles of over 20.1 million people worldwide.

With approximately 21,200 employees globally and $4.0 billion in annual revenues, Align Technology operates through two primary business segments: Clear Aligner therapy and Imaging Systems and CAD/CAM Services. The company's mission is to reimagine and reinvent orthodontic and restorative treatment delivery through cutting-edge digital technologies and data-driven insights.

## Revolutionary Treatment Solutions

Align Technology's comprehensive portfolio addresses the full spectrum of orthodontic and dental care:

### Clear Aligner Segment
The Invisalign system offering comprehensive orthodontic solutions:

- **Invisalign Comprehensive**: Full treatment for adults and teens with mild to severe malocclusion
- **Invisalign First**: Specialized treatment for younger patients aged 6-10 years
- **Invisalign Express/Lite/Moderate**: Targeted solutions for specific treatment needs
- **Invisalign Go**: Solutions for general practitioners treating mild cases
- **Vivera Retainers**: Custom-made retainers for post-treatment maintenance
- **Professional Whitening**: Integrated whitening system for enhanced aesthetics
- **Palatal Expander**: 3D printed orthodontic device for jaw expansion

### Imaging Systems and CAD/CAM Services
Digital scanning and treatment planning technologies:

- **iTero Intraoral Scanners**: Advanced digital impression technology
- **exocad CAD/CAM Software**: Computer-aided design and manufacturing solutions
- **Orthodontic Software**: Digital records storage and treatment planning
- **Restorative Software**: Solutions for general practitioners and specialists
- **Outcome Simulator**: Chair-side treatment visualization tools
- **Progress Assessment**: Treatment monitoring and adjustment tools
- **TimeLapse Technology**: Historical scan comparison and analysis
