## ANSS - Engineering Simulation Software Leader

ANSYS, Inc. (NASDAQ: ANSS) is the global leader in engineering simulation software, enabling engineers, designers, researchers, and students to predict how their products will work in real-world environments before they are physically created. Founded in 1970 and headquartered in Canonsburg, Pennsylvania, ANSYS has revolutionized product development across industries by providing comprehensive simulation solutions that reduce development time, costs, and risk while improving product performance and innovation.

With a market capitalization exceeding $29 billion and approximately 6,600 employees worldwide, ANSYS serves customers across high-tech, aerospace and defense, automotive, energy, industrial equipment, materials and chemicals, consumer products, healthcare, and construction industries. The company's physics-based simulation platform enables engineers to test, predict, and optimize product performance using virtual prototypes.

## Comprehensive Simulation Portfolio

ANSYS operates through integrated product suites addressing diverse engineering challenges:

### Structural Analysis
Advanced structural simulation capabilities for mechanical design optimization:

- **ANSYS Mechanical**: Comprehensive finite element analysis software for structural simulation
- **LS-DYNA**: Explicit dynamics simulation for crash, impact, and nonlinear analysis
- **Structural Optimization**: Design optimization and material selection tools
- **Fatigue Analysis**: Life prediction and durability assessment capabilities
- **Composite Materials**: Specialized tools for composite structure design and analysis

### Fluids and Thermal Analysis
Computational fluid dynamics and thermal simulation solutions:

- **ANSYS Fluent**: Leading computational fluid dynamics software for flow simulation
- **ANSYS CFX**: Advanced CFD solver for turbomachinery and complex geometries
- **Thermal Analysis**: Heat transfer and thermal management simulation
- **Combustion Modeling**: Advanced combustion and reacting flow simulation
- **Multiphase Flow**: Simulation of gas-liquid and particle-laden flows

### Electronics and Electromagnetics
Electronic design and electromagnetic field simulation:

- **ANSYS HFSS**: High-frequency electromagnetic field simulation for RF and antenna design
- **Signal Integrity**: High-speed digital circuit analysis and optimization
- **Power Electronics**: Simulation of power conversion and motor drive systems
- **Electromagnetic Compatibility**: EMC/EMI analysis and mitigation strategies
- **Chip-Package-System**: Integrated electronic system design and analysis

### Embedded Software and Systems
Mission-critical embedded software development and certification:

- **ANSYS SCADE**: Model-based development environment for safety-critical software
- **Certification Support**: DO-178C, ISO 26262, and other safety standard compliance
- **System Modeling**: Model-based systems engineering and verification
- **Real-Time Simulation**: Hardware-in-the-loop and software-in-the-loop testing
