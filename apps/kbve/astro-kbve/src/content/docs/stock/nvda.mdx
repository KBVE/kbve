## NVDA - Global AI and Accelerated Computing Leader

NVIDIA Corporation (NASDAQ: NVDA) is a leading technology company specializing in graphics processing units (GPUs), artificial intelligence computing, and accelerated computing platforms that power everything from gaming to data centers and autonomous vehicles. Founded in 1993 by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem and headquartered in Santa Clara, California, NVIDIA has transformed from a gaming graphics company into the dominant force in artificial intelligence and accelerated computing.

With operations worldwide and serving customers across gaming, data centers, professional visualization, automotive, and edge computing markets, NVIDIA has established itself as the essential technology provider for the AI revolution. The company's GPU architecture and CUDA programming platform have become the foundation for AI training and inference, machine learning, scientific computing, and high-performance computing applications globally.
