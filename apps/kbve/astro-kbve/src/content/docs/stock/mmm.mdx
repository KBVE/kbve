## MMM

3M Company (NYSE: MMM) is a diversified technology company that provides innovative solutions across multiple industries worldwide. Founded in 1902 and headquartered in Saint Paul, Minnesota, 3M has evolved from a small mining company into a global industrial conglomerate with operations in the Americas, Asia Pacific, Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

The company operates through three main business segments: Safety and Industrial, Transportation and Electronics, and Consumer. With approximately 61,500 employees worldwide, 3M is known for its commitment to research and development, holding thousands of patents and maintaining a strong culture of innovation that has driven consistent growth for over a century.

## Business Segments

### Safety and Industrial
This segment provides industrial abrasives and finishing solutions for metalworking applications, autobody repair solutions, industrial specialty products including personal hygiene materials, electrical products for construction and maintenance, structural adhesives, respiratory and fall protection solutions, and natural mineral granules for roofing shingles.

### Transportation and Electronics
Offers ceramic solutions, attachment and bonding materials for transportation vehicles, graphic films for advertising and fleet signage, reflective signage for highway safety, light management films, electronics assembly solutions, chip packaging, semiconductor production materials, and data center solutions.

### Consumer
Provides cleaning products for homes, consumer air quality products, picture hanging accessories, retail abrasives and paint accessories, stationery and office products, automotive appearance products, and consumer healthcare products including bandages, tapes, and supports.

## Innovation and R&D

3M is renowned for its innovation culture, with a long-standing tradition of encouraging employee creativity through the "15% time" program, allowing researchers to spend 15% of their time on projects of their own choosing. The company maintains:

- **Global R&D Network**: Research facilities worldwide focusing on breakthrough technologies
- **Patent Portfolio**: Thousands of active patents across diverse technology platforms
- **Carlton Society**: Recognition program for 3M's most innovative scientists and engineers
- **Technology Platforms**: Over 50 core technology platforms enabling cross-industry innovation
