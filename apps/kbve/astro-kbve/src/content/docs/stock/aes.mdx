## AES - Accelerating the Future of Energy

The AES Corporation (NYSE: AES) is a Fortune 500 global energy company that generates and distributes electric power through its diversified portfolio of generation assets and utility operations. Founded in 1981 and headquartered in Arlington, Virginia, AES has evolved from a traditional power company into a leading renewable energy and energy storage developer, serving customers across 15 countries worldwide.

With approximately 9,100 employees globally, AES operates a generation portfolio of over 32 gigawatts and distributes power to approximately 2.7 million customers. The company is at the forefront of the global energy transition, focusing on clean energy solutions including solar, wind, energy storage, and advanced grid technologies while systematically reducing its carbon footprint through strategic coal plant retirements and renewable energy expansion.

## Business Operations

AES operates through two primary business segments serving diverse energy markets globally:

### Generation
Power generation assets across multiple technologies and geographies:

- **Renewable Energy**: Solar and wind power plants with integrated energy storage systems
- **Energy Storage**: Leading battery energy storage system developer and operator
- **Natural Gas**: Efficient combined-cycle and peaking power plants
- **Hydro Power**: Hydroelectric generation assets in multiple markets
- **Coal Phase-Out**: Strategic retirement of coal plants by end of 2025

### Utilities
Regulated electric distribution and transmission operations:

- **AES Ohio**: Electric distribution serving customers in Ohio
- **AES Indiana**: Integrated electric utility serving Indiana customers
- **AES El Salvador**: Electricity distribution serving El Salvador
- **AES Dominican Republic**: Power distribution operations
- **Puerto Rico**: Transmission and distribution operations
- **Other International Utilities**: Distribution operations in multiple countries
