## AON - Global Professional Services and Risk Solutions Leader

Aon plc (NYSE: AON) is a leading global professional services firm providing a broad range of risk, retirement, and health solutions to clients worldwide. Founded in 1982 and headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, with operational headquarters in Chicago, Illinois, Aon serves clients through a network of over 60,000 colleagues operating in more than 120 countries and sovereignties. The company is in the business of better decisions, helping protect and enrich the lives of people around the world.

With a market capitalization exceeding $75 billion, Aon operates through two primary business segments: Risk Capital and Human Capital. The company's globally integrated expertise enables clients to navigate today's most pressing risk and people challenges while building resilience and driving growth in an increasingly complex and volatile world.

## Comprehensive Professional Services Platform

Aon operates through two integrated business segments addressing critical client needs:

### Risk Capital
Making better risk decisions through comprehensive risk management solutions:

- **Commercial Risk Solutions**: Property, casualty, and specialty insurance brokerage and consulting
- **Reinsurance Solutions**: Treaty and facultative reinsurance brokerage and capital markets
- **Data and Analytics**: Advanced risk modeling, analytics, and catastrophe modeling
- **Cyber Solutions**: Cybersecurity risk assessment, insurance, and incident response
- **Climate and Sustainability**: Climate risk assessment and sustainable business solutions
- **Investment Banking**: Insurance-linked securities and capital markets advisory

### Human Capital
Making better people decisions through workforce and talent solutions:

- **Health Solutions**: Employee benefits consulting, administration, and technology platforms
- **Wealth Solutions**: Retirement plan design, administration, and investment consulting
- **Talent and Rewards**: Executive compensation, workforce analytics, and talent management
- **Communication and Change Management**: Employee engagement and organizational transformation
- **Compliance and Regulatory**: Benefits compliance and regulatory advisory services
- **Technology Solutions**: HR technology platforms and digital transformation
