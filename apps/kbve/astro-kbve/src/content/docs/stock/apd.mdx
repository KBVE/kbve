## APD - Industrial Gases and Clean Energy Pioneer

Air Products and Chemicals, Inc. (NYSE: APD) is a world-leading industrial gases company that has been serving customers globally since 1940. Headquartered in Allentown, Pennsylvania, Air Products is the largest supplier of hydrogen and helium in the world, providing essential industrial gases, related equipment, and applications expertise to manufacturing markets including refining and petrochemicals, metals, electronics, and healthcare.

With approximately 23,000 employees operating in 50 countries, Air Products has positioned itself at the forefront of the global energy transition through major investments in clean hydrogen production facilities. The company combines decades of industrial gas expertise with cutting-edge technology to support both traditional industrial applications and emerging clean energy solutions.

## Business Operations

Air Products operates through integrated business segments serving diverse industrial and energy markets:

### Industrial Gases
Core atmospheric and process gases serving traditional manufacturing markets:

- **Oxygen**: Essential for steel production, chemicals, and healthcare applications
- **Nitrogen**: Inert atmosphere applications, food preservation, and chemical processing
- **Argon**: Metal production, electronics manufacturing, and specialty applications
- **Hydrogen**: Refining, chemicals, electronics, metals, and emerging clean energy
- **Helium**: Electronics, healthcare, research, and lifting applications
- **Carbon Dioxide**: Food and beverage, chemical processing, and enhanced oil recovery

### Equipment and Technology
Advanced gas production and processing technology solutions:

- **Air Separation Units**: Large-scale oxygen, nitrogen, and argon production plants
- **LNG Process Technology**: Liquefied natural gas production and processing equipment
- **Hydrogen Technologies**: Steam methane reforming, gasification, and electrolysis systems
- **Carbon Capture**: CO2 capture and utilization technologies
- **Process Equipment**: Specialized equipment for gas production and handling

### Clean Energy Solutions
Leading-edge hydrogen and clean energy technologies:

- **Green Hydrogen**: Renewable energy-powered electrolysis production
- **Blue Hydrogen**: Natural gas reformation with carbon capture and storage
- **Hydrogen Fueling**: Infrastructure for transportation and mobility applications
- **Industrial Decarbonization**: Clean hydrogen solutions for steel, cement, and chemicals
- **Energy Storage**: Hydrogen-based energy storage and power generation
