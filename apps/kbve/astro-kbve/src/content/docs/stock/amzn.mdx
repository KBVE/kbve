## AMZN - Everything Store to Everything Cloud

Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ: AMZN) is the world's largest e-commerce company and a leading cloud computing provider that has fundamentally transformed how people shop, businesses operate, and technology infrastructure is delivered. Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos as an online bookstore, Amazon has evolved into a global technology conglomerate serving consumers, sellers, developers, enterprises, and content creators across multiple continents and industries.

With a market capitalization exceeding $2.2 trillion and over 1.5 million employees worldwide, Amazon operates through three primary segments: North America, International, and Amazon Web Services (AWS). The company's mission to be "Earth's Most Customer-Centric Company" drives continuous innovation across e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, artificial intelligence, and logistics infrastructure.

## Comprehensive Business Ecosystem

Amazon's business model spans multiple interconnected platforms and services:

### E-Commerce Platform
The world's largest online marketplace connecting buyers and sellers:

- **Retail Operations**: First-party retail sales of millions of products
- **Third-Party Marketplace**: Platform enabling millions of sellers to reach global customers  
- **Amazon Prime**: Membership program with over 200 million subscribers globally
- **Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)**: Comprehensive logistics and fulfillment services
- **Amazon Fresh and Whole Foods**: Grocery delivery and physical retail presence
- **International Expansion**: Operations in multiple countries and regions

### Amazon Web Services (AWS)
Leading cloud computing platform serving enterprises and governments:

- **Compute Services**: EC2, Lambda, and containerized computing solutions
- **Storage Solutions**: S3, EBS, and comprehensive data storage options
- **Database Services**: RDS, DynamoDB, and managed database solutions
- **Machine Learning**: SageMaker and comprehensive AI/ML development tools
- **Enterprise Applications**: WorkSpaces, Connect, and business productivity solutions
- **Global Infrastructure**: Data centers and edge locations worldwide

### Digital Content and Services
Entertainment and media platforms reaching global audiences:

- **Amazon Prime Video**: Streaming service with original and licensed content
- **Amazon Music**: Music streaming and digital music sales
- **Kindle and Publishing**: E-books, audiobooks, and publishing services
- **Twitch**: Live streaming platform for gaming and creative content
- **Advertising Services**: Digital advertising across Amazon properties
