## LNT - Clean Energy Transition Leader

Alliant Energy Corporation (NASDAQ: LNT) is a regulated utility holding company that has been providing essential electric and natural gas services to customers in Wisconsin and Iowa since 1917. Headquartered in Madison, Wisconsin, Alliant Energy operates through two primary regulated utility subsidiaries: Wisconsin Power and Light Company (WPL) and Interstate Power and Light Company (IPL), serving approximately 970,000 electric and 420,000 natural gas customers.

With approximately 2,998 employees, Alliant Energy is transforming its energy portfolio through significant investments in renewable energy, grid modernization, and clean energy infrastructure. The company is committed to achieving net-zero carbon dioxide emissions from electric generation by 2050 while maintaining reliable, affordable service for customers across its service territories.
