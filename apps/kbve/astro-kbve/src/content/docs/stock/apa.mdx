## APA - Independent Energy Exploration and Production

APA Corporation (NASDAQ: APA) is an independent energy company that explores for, develops, and produces oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids with operations primarily in the United States. Formerly known as Apache Corporation, the company rebranded to APA Corporation in 2021, reflecting its focus on American production assets and commitment to operational excellence in unconventional resource development.

Headquartered in Houston, Texas, APA has built a portfolio of high-quality, long-life assets concentrated in the Permian Basin of West Texas, one of North America's most prolific oil and gas regions. The company's strategy focuses on generating sustainable free cash flow, returning capital to shareholders, and maintaining a strong balance sheet while developing its extensive unconventional resource base.

## Core Operating Assets

APA's operations are concentrated in premier North American unconventional plays:

### Permian Basin Operations
Core development area in West Texas:

- **Delaware Basin**: Primary focus area with extensive horizontal drilling program
- **Central Basin Platform**: Conventional and unconventional development opportunities
- **Midland Basin**: Selective development of high-return drilling locations
- **Multi-Zone Development**: Targeting multiple productive formations including Wolfcamp, Bone Spring, and Delaware formations
- **Enhanced Recovery**: Advanced completion techniques and artificial lift optimization

### Anadarko Basin
Strategic position in Oklahoma's STACK play:

- **STACK Play**: Scoop/Woodford and Anadarko Basin Woodford development
- **Multi-Bench Development**: Targeting multiple productive zones
- **Gas Processing**: Integrated midstream infrastructure and processing capabilities
- **Water Management**: Comprehensive produced water handling and recycling

### Conventional Assets
Stable production from conventional fields:

- **Conventional Oil**: Long-life conventional oil production in various basins
- **Enhanced Oil Recovery**: Secondary and tertiary recovery projects
- **Gas Production**: Natural gas production from conventional reservoirs
- **Infrastructure**: Extensive pipeline and processing infrastructure
