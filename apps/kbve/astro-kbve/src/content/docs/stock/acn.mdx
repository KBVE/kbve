## ACN - Global Professional Services Leader

Accenture plc (NYSE: ACN) is a global professional services company that provides strategy and consulting, technology and operations services, and digital transformation solutions to clients across all industries and geographies. Founded in 1951 and headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, Accenture has evolved from an accounting consultancy into one of the world's largest and most influential technology consulting and professional services companies.

With approximately 801,000 employees serving clients in more than 120 countries, Accenture is at the forefront of digital transformation, helping organizations navigate the complex intersection of technology and business. The company's comprehensive portfolio spans strategic consulting, digital technology implementation, artificial intelligence, cloud services, cybersecurity, and managed operations services.
