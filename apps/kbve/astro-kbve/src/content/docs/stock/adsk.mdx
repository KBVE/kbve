## ADSK - Design and Make Platform Technology Leader

Autodesk, Inc. (NASDAQ: ADSK) is a leading global provider of 3D design, engineering, and entertainment technology solutions that enable customers to design and make anything. Founded in 1982 and headquartered in San Francisco, California, Autodesk empowers designers, engineers, builders, and creators with software tools that span architecture, engineering, construction, manufacturing, media, and entertainment industries.

With a market capitalization exceeding $63 billion and approximately 15,300 employees worldwide, Autodesk has transformed from a traditional software licensing company to a cloud-based subscription platform provider. The company's Design and Make Platform strategy focuses on connecting digital design with physical production, enabling customers to create the world around us through innovative technology solutions.

## Comprehensive Design and Make Platform

Autodesk operates through integrated product portfolios serving diverse industries:

### Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC)
Comprehensive solutions for the built environment:

- **AutoCAD**: Industry-standard 2D and 3D CAD software for design, drafting, and documentation
- **Revit**: Building Information Modeling (BIM) software for architectural and structural design
- **Civil 3D**: Civil engineering design and documentation for infrastructure projects
- **Autodesk Build**: Cloud-based construction management and field collaboration platform
- **BIM Collaborate Pro**: Cloud-based design collaboration and project management
- **Navisworks**: 3D model review, coordination, and clash detection for large projects

### Product Design and Manufacturing (PDM)
Advanced manufacturing and product development solutions:

- **Fusion 360**: Integrated CAD, CAM, CAE, and PCB design platform for product development
- **Inventor**: 3D mechanical design, simulation, and documentation software
- **AutoCAD Mechanical**: Specialized CAD tools for mechanical design and manufacturing
- **Vault**: Product data management for design collaboration and version control
- **Netfabb**: Additive manufacturing and 3D printing preparation software
- **PowerMill**: Advanced CAM software for complex machining operations

### Media and Entertainment (M&E)
Creative tools for film, television, gaming, and visual effects:

- **Maya**: Professional 3D animation, modeling, and rendering software
- **3ds Max**: 3D modeling, animation, and visualization for games and design
- **Flow Production Tracking**: Cloud-based production management and review platform
- **Flow Capture**: Digital dailies and review software for film production
- **MotionBuilder**: 3D character animation and motion capture software
- **Flame**: Advanced visual effects and finishing software for high-end post-production
