## ACIW - Global Electronic Payments Technology Leader

ACI Worldwide, Inc. (NASDAQ: ACIW) is a leading global provider of real-time electronic payment software and solutions, enabling secure, reliable, and efficient electronic payments for financial institutions, retailers, and payment processors around the world. Founded in 1975 and headquartered in Miami, Florida, ACI Worldwide has established itself as a mission-critical technology partner powering the global payments ecosystem that processes over 25 billion transactions annually.

With operations in over 80 countries and serving more than 5,000 organizations globally, ACI Worldwide provides comprehensive payment processing solutions spanning retail payments, merchant acquiring, card issuing, fraud prevention, and digital banking. The company's platform-based approach enables real-time payment processing across multiple channels and payment types, supporting the digital transformation of payment systems worldwide.
