## AVB - Premier Apartment Communities REIT

AvalonBay Communities, Inc. (NYSE: AVB) is a leading real estate investment trust (REIT) focused on developing, acquiring, owning, and operating luxury apartment communities in high-barrier-to-entry markets across the United States. Founded in 1978 and headquartered in Arlington, Virginia, AvalonBay has established itself as one of the largest and most successful apartment REITs, with a portfolio concentrated in some of the nation's most dynamic and supply-constrained metropolitan areas.

With a portfolio of approximately 300 apartment communities containing nearly 90,000 apartment homes across 11 states and the District of Columbia, AvalonBay serves a diverse resident base seeking high-quality rental housing in premier locations. The company's strategy focuses on markets with strong job growth, high barriers to new supply, and favorable demographic trends that support long-term rental demand and rent growth.
