## AOS - Global Water Technology Leader

A.O. Smith Corporation (NYSE: AOS) is a leading global manufacturer and marketer of residential and commercial water heating equipment and water treatment products. Founded in 1874 and headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, the company has established itself as one of the world's most trusted brands in water technology solutions, serving customers across North America, China, Europe, and India.

With over 150 years of innovation and expertise, A.O. Smith has evolved from a small Wisconsin company into a global leader in water heating and water treatment technologies. The company's commitment to quality, efficiency, and sustainability has made it a preferred choice for residential, commercial, and industrial applications worldwide.

## Business Segments

### Water Heating Solutions
A.O. Smith's core business encompasses a comprehensive range of water heating products:

- **Residential Water Heaters**: Gas and electric tank water heaters, tankless water heaters, heat pump water heaters, and hybrid units
- **Commercial Water Heaters**: High-capacity units for restaurants, hotels, office buildings, schools, hospitals, and other large facilities
- **Specialty Products**: Solar water heating systems, swimming pool and spa heaters, boilers, and expansion tanks
- **Energy Efficient Solutions**: ENERGY STAR certified products and high-efficiency models

### Water Treatment Products
The company offers comprehensive water treatment solutions:

- **Point-of-Entry Systems**: Whole-home water softeners and filtration systems
- **Point-of-Use Products**: Carbon filters, reverse osmosis systems, and drinking water solutions
- **Commercial Water Treatment**: Industrial filtration and treatment systems
- **Well Water Solutions**: Specialized systems for private wells and rural applications
