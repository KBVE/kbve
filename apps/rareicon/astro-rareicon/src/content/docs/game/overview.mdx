RareIcon blends bullet-hell roguelite chaos with strategic base building, tactical twin-stick combat, and party dynamics. Each run escalates system threats while giving you tools to chip back.

## Key features

### Bullet-hell roguelite combat
Fast-paced, chaotic encounters where every frame counts. Survive swarms of projectiles, environmental hazards, and evolving digital threats.

### WASD + twin-stick shooting with tactical depth
WASD movement with mouse-aimed shooting. Responsive point-and-click precision layered with spell casting and auto-aim support tools to handle the digital chaos.

### Strategic base building
Between missions, collect resources and schematics to upgrade your sanctuary. Automate systems, unlock new functionality, expand your influence in the data-slums.

### Recruitable allies and party dynamics
Find and recruit unique companions — rogue AIs, glitch-born anomalies, fellow outcasts — each with distinct abilities, synergy potential, and upgrade paths.

### Vast inventory and modular items
Collect, craft, and combine hundreds of modular items that impact combat, exploration, and progression. Experiment with loadouts to push your build further every run.

### Procedurally generated worlds
Ever-changing zones filled with randomized encounters, secrets, and challenges. No two runs are the same.

### Cyber-dystopian storytelling
Uncover the truth behind the DaemonCorps and your commoditized existence. Dialogue, data fragments, and ambient storytelling immerse you in the fractured future.

### Distinct visual + audio identity
Glitch-infused pixel art and a synthwave-driven soundtrack that mirrors the instability of the digital wastelands.
