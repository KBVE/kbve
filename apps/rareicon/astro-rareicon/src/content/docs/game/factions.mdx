RareIcon's world is shaped by the megastructure pressing down on everything and the fragments still fighting it.

## The megastructure

### DaemonCorps
The engine of bio-commoditized servitude. DaemonCorps patrols enforce debt, harvest pay-data, and hunt anyone slipping the leash of the social credit system. Their signature glows through every zone: uniform logic, predictable escalation, overwhelming numbers.

### Rogue constructs
Broken AI and automation left behind as the galactic economy collapsed. Sometimes hostile, sometimes indifferent, sometimes salvageable as allies.

### Demon-like anomalies
Reality-bending entities born from corrupted data vaults — the closest thing the digital wastelands have to gods. Unpredictable, dangerous, and occasionally useful.

## The resistance

### Chip
A last-generational human. Debt-ridden, neural-implanted, running on a dying social credit score. You.

### Recruitable outcasts
Fellow debt-survivors, rogue AIs, and glitch-born entities. Each companion carries distinct abilities, synergy potential, and their own upgrade paths. Party composition matters.
