Every term is one page. Each term holds a set of variants (outline, filled, bold, duotone, glyph). Search the field below, or filter by category, style, or theme. Click a card to see every variant and copy its SVG.

<IconsBrowser />

## How the catalog is organized

- **Term** — one concept per page (`sword`, `close`, `arrow-right`).
- **Variant** — one rendering of a term (outline, filled, ocean-duotone).
- **Tags & themes** — per-variant facets layered on top of the term-level category so you can filter a specific rendering, not just the concept.

All icons use `currentColor` unless the variant explicitly overrides — drop them into any page and they pick up the surrounding text color.
