## What is RareIcon

A fast-paced 2D sci-fi Action-RPG bullet-hell roguelite set in a collapsing digital dystopia. Play as **Chip**, a debt-ridden survivor with WASD + mouse controls, building a sanctuary, upgrading gear, and recruiting allies to fight back against the DaemonCorps, robots, creatures, and more.
