**RareIcon** is a fast-paced 2D sci-fi Action-RPG bullet-hell roguelite set in a collapsing digital dystopia.

Play as **Chip** — a debt-ridden survivor with WASD + mouse controls, building a sanctuary, upgrading gear, and recruiting allies to fight back against the DaemonCorps, robots, creatures, and more.

## The world

The year is **2101 — Great Cyber Year**. The galactic economy has failed into a corrupted neon-glow. As a debt-enslaved worker, survival means hacking, looting pay-data, and resisting the descent into bio-commoditized servitude.

Armed with a jury-rigged neural implant and a dying social credit score, Chip — a last-generational human — scrapes through the data-slums of the nebulae, navigating procedurally generated zones riddled with hostile DaemonCorps patrols, broken AI, corrupted data vaults, and reality-bending demon-like anomalies.

## The loop

- **Run** — bullet-hell combat, procgen zones, escalating system threats.
- **Return** — stock resources, upgrade gear, automate sanctuary systems.
- **Recruit** — rogue AIs, glitch-born entities, fellow outcasts.
- **Resist** — every run chips away at your identity, and chips back at the system.

> Will you escape the purge or become digital ghost matter?
