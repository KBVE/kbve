[h1]chef_paldon_yumsay[/h1]

Open-source custom food pack for Palworld. Adds new cooking recipes built from existing
in-game ingredients. Data-only pak; no UE4SS or runtime scripts required.

[h2]New foods[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Roasted Vegetables[/b]: 2 Carrot, 2 Onion. Restores satiety and gives a small Work
Speed effect.
[*][b]Hearty Stew[/b]: 2 Potato, 1 Carrot, 1 Onion. Restores a large amount of satiety and
gives an Attack Up effect.
[/list]

[h2]Install notes[/h2]
[list]
[*]Data-only pak mod. No UE4SS required.
[*]Dedicated servers: install on both the client and the server.
[/list]
